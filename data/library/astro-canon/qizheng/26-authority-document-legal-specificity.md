# 七政四餘 · Authority, Document, Legal, And Approval Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Qizheng/Guolao. Use as operational rules only; do not expose raw OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about documents, contracts, permits, licenses, visa/immigration, government offices, court, tax, compliance, approval, audit, official letters, evidence, application results, or institutional authority.

Do not flatten this into "work is good" or "risk is bad." Split document quality, authority pressure, counterparty, money/tax, evidence flow, and timing.

## Authority And Document Chain

Read in this order:

1. **命主 / 命度 / 度主 / 身主**: whether the native can carry official pressure and complete the procedure.
2. **官祿**: authority, office, institution, regulation, superior, legal status, formal approval.
3. **兄弟**: documents, messages, applications, written evidence, communication, paperwork flow.
4. **妻妾**: counterparty, contract, open opponent, agency-facing agreement, one-to-one negotiation.
5. **財帛 / 田宅**: fees, tax, debt, property paperwork, collateral, retained asset affected by the document.
6. **疾厄 / 遷移**: legal stress, delay, investigation, visa/foreign/public movement pressure.
7. **行限 / 限度主 / 洞微百六限 / target-year 木土**: whether the period opens approval or creates burden.

## Topic Rules

- **Visa / immigration / relocation permission**: use 遷移 + 官祿 + 兄弟, then fees from 財帛 and timing.
- **Government approval / license / permit**: use 官祿 first, then 兄弟 paperwork and 妻妾 counterparty.
- **Court / dispute / legal opponent**: use 官祿 + 妻妾 + 疾厄; do not promise court victory from one good star.
- **Tax / inheritance / property documents**: use 財帛 + 田宅 + 官祿; separate cash obligation from asset retention.
- **Contract signing**: use 妻妾 for the formal counterparty, 兄弟 for clauses/messages, 官祿 for enforceability.

## Action Calls

- **เดินหน้าได้**: 官祿 and 兄弟 support repeat, counterparty is manageable, and timing opens the asked topic.
- **เดินหน้าแบบมีเงื่อนไข**: approval path exists but 仇/難, weak paperwork, fees/tax pressure, or heavy 土 requires review and staged submission.
- **รอก่อน/แก้เอกสารก่อน**: natal promise exists but document lane or timing is not ready.
- **เลี่ยง/อย่าเซ็นตอนนี้**: authority, document, counterparty, and timing lanes repeat pressure.
