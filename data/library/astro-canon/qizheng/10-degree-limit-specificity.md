# 七政四餘 · Degree Lord, Body Lord, Limit, And Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Qizheng/Guolao. It condenses the retained Guolao, Xingxue Dacheng, Xingping Huihai, Xingming Suyuan, Wuxing Sanming, mansion-degree, and calculation-provenance corpus into operational reading rules. It is not raw source text and must not expose OCR, page order, source prose, or private file paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack to make Qizheng answers specific. A valid Qizheng answer must not stop at "house good" or "star bad." It must connect:

1. 命主,
2. 命度/度主,
3. 身宮/身主,
4. asked palace and palace lord,
5. star role as 恩/用/仇/難,
6. 廟旺/升殿/落陷 status,
7. 格局/三方/拱夾 when supplied,
8. 行限/限度主/洞微百六限 and target-year 木土 row when timing matters.

Do not let one label such as `verdictTh`, `level`, or a single palace badge replace the chain.

## Main Weighting

Weight in this order:

1. **命度/度主**: the degree lord is the precision anchor. If 度主 is strong, the chart can deliver through its house and topic even when 命宮 language is mixed. If 度主 is weak or hostile, reduce confidence.
2. **命主**: shows the native's governing star. Check status, house, and 恩/用/仇/難 network.
3. **身主 / 身宮**: shows embodiment, stamina, concrete life handling, and the way events are carried physically or practically.
4. **Asked palace**: use the exact 12宮 topic and its lord.
5. **Stars inside the asked palace**: read status and role. A star in the palace matters more than a generic star-nature statement elsewhere.
6. **三方/對宮/拱夾**: use only supplied patterns or deterministic packet relations. Do not infer extra patterns from raw signs.
7. **格局**: named patterns sharpen the verdict. Good 格 needs topic support; bad 格 needs repetition before becoming a hard warning.
8. **Timing**: 行限 is stronger than annual 木土 row. 木土 row activates; it does not rewrite the natal promise.

## Degree Lord And Mansion Use

When packet supplies `mingDegree`:

- Use sign, 宿, degree, and 度主 as a concrete anchor.
- If 度主 is 廟/旺/升殿/入垣 and connected to the asked palace, give a stronger verdict.
- If 度主 is 落陷 or classified as 仇/難 relative to 命主, name the friction plainly.
- If the question is career/money/relationship/health, ask: "what house does 度主 occupy or rule, and does it touch the asked house?"
- Do not invent mansion-degree tables if the packet does not supply them.

User-facing shape:

"The precision anchor is 命度 [宿/degree] with 度主 [star/status]; because it sits/touches [house/topic], the matter works through [specific channel]."

## Body Lord Use

身主 is practical delivery:

- Strong 身主: the person can carry the event in real life, even if the mind/emotion is stressed.
- Weak 身主: the event may be theoretically good but tiring, delayed, or harder on body/routine.
- 身主 tied to 疾厄/奴僕/遷移: practical stress, people, travel, workload, or service friction must be named.
- 身主 tied to 官祿/福德/財帛/田宅: work, fortune base, income, or assets become embodied channels.

Do not turn 身主 into diagnosis. It is vitality/practical handling, not medical certainty.

## Asked Palace Specificity

### 官祿 / Career

Use 官祿 palace, 官祿 lord, 命主, 度主, 身主, 福德 support, 貴格/官格, and 行限.

Name the work channel from the actual star/lord:

- 日: public visibility, authority, leadership, formal recognition.
- 月: public response, care, rhythm, audience/client flow.
- 木: teaching, advising, growth, planning, patrons.
- 火: action, competition, technical/urgent work, conflict management.
- 土: systems, duty, institutions, long-term responsibility.
- 金: finance, value, contracts, aesthetics, precision.
- 水: documents, trade, analysis, communication, logistics.
- 羅/計/月孛/紫氣: nonstandard, hidden, disruptive, technical, spiritual, research, or behind-the-scenes channel depending on role/status.

### 財帛 / 田宅 / Money

Separate earning from retention:

- 財帛 = cashflow and money movement.
- 田宅 = assets, property, root, storage.
- 官祿 = income channel through work.
- 福德 = fortune base and ease.

If 官祿 is strong but 財帛 weak, say work can create money but retention leaks. If 財帛 strong but 田宅 weak, income exists but asset consolidation needs discipline.

### 妻妾 / Relationship

Use 妻妾 palace/lord first, then 命主/身主 temperament and 福德 support. In pair mode, use only `PAIR_INTERACTION_PACKET qizheng`.

Separate:

- attraction,
- stable partnership,
- pressure/conflict,
- practical readiness,
- timing.

Do not judge a pair from one person's 妻妾 alone.

### 疾厄 / Health-Routine

Use 疾厄, 身主, 命度主, hostile 仇/難 stars, 火/土/羅/計 style pressure, and 行限 activation.

Allowed: stamina, routine pressure, sleep/rest, workload, heat/cold/dryness symbolism, check-up, discipline.

Forbidden: diagnosis, medication, surgery instruction, death prediction.

### 遷移 / Travel-External Field

Use 遷移 palace/lord, stars in 遷移, 命主/度主 relationship, and timing. Separate travel, external market, foreign clients, public exposure, distance, relocation, and pressure from leaving home base.

## Timing Specificity

For year questions:

1. Name the target year/refDate.
2. Name current 行限 palace and topic.
3. Name 限度主 and its natal status/role.
4. Name 限宮主 and whether it helps or burdens the asked topic.
5. Add 木/土 annual transit only as activation/opportunity/burden.
6. Do not claim exact month/day unless 流月/流日 exists.

If target year packet exists, do not say "only the current year is available."

## Mixed Verdict Rule

Qizheng often shows split results. Write the split instead of flattening:

- "งานขึ้น แต่กินแรงกาย."
- "เงินเข้าได้จากงาน/คน แต่เก็บทรัพย์ยาก."
- "คู่มีแรงดึง แต่ timing ยังหนัก."
- "ย้าย/เดินทางเปิดช่อง แต่เอกสาร/คนกลางกดดัน."

Then choose which side is stronger from 度主 + asked palace + timing.

## Bage Fu Compact Buckets

Use 八格賦 only as a verdict bucket after the concrete evidence chain is named. Do not call a bucket from one clue.

- 貴格: strong 命/身, 官祿/福德 support, clean 日月 or helpful 三方/拱夾. Use for status, title, authority, promotion.
- 富格: 財帛/田宅/官祿/福德 linked by helpful lord or 恩/用 star. Use for wealth, assets, property, income base.
- 貧格: 財帛/田宅 harmed, resources leak, limit lord weak, or hostile 仇/難 invades money houses. Use for cash leakage or weak resource base.
- 賤格: 命主/身主 weak, good stars scattered, malefic pressure close to 命身. Use for unstable role or being driven by others.
- 壽格: 命身/田宅/日月 support vitality. Use only as constitution support, never fixed lifespan.
- 夭格: 命身/主宿 severely harmed with no rescue and timing activates harm. Use only as caution wording, never death prediction.
- 賢格: 金水/木氣/luminaries support 命身 and learning houses. Use for wisdom, skill, study, counsel.
- 愚格: 命/母/日月 lose order and confused/malefic stars attack the ruler. Use for poor judgment or misuse of skill.
- 女命格: when gender/topic makes it relevant, 身/福/夫宮 become central. Apply only from packet fields.

Bucket output rule: say "the chain leans toward [bucket] because..." then give the exact palace/star/status/timing evidence. If evidence is mixed, name the competing bucket instead of hiding the contradiction.
