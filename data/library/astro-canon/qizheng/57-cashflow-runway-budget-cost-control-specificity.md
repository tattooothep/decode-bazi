## Runway Rule

Runway requires both cash inflow and retained base:

- 財帛 = cash coming in,
- 田宅 = cash/base retained,
- 官祿 = operating discipline,
- 兄弟/妻妾 = billing and payment terms,
- 奴僕 = staff/tool/vendor burn,
- 疾厄/仇難 = leak, debt, refund, emergency, or hidden liability.

If 財帛 is supported but 田宅 is weak, revenue arrives but does not stay; cut leakage and build reserve. If 田宅 is supported but 財帛 is weak, protect base and improve collection before scaling. If 奴僕/疾厄 are pressured, headcount/tools/operations consume the business.

## Cost-Cut Rule

Do not recommend cutting everything. Name the cost lane:

- **staff/tool burn**: 奴僕 pressure,
- **vendor/customer term leak**: 妻妾 pressure,
- **billing/document confusion**: 兄弟 pressure,
- **fixed asset/office/platform burden**: 田宅 pressure,
- **refund/tax/debt/emergency**: 疾厄/仇難 pressure,
- **owner comfort/anxiety spending**: 福德 pressure.

Good cash evidence allows controlled reinvestment. Strong leak evidence requires freeze, cap, collect, renegotiate, or reserve.
