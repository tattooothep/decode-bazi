## Service-Chain Rule

A sale is not a delivered result. Require a chain:

- 兄弟 = ticket wording, reply, documentation, instruction,
- 妻妾 = customer expectation and complaint pressure,
- 官祿 = service promise and process standard,
- 奴僕 = staff/helper capacity,
- 財帛/田宅 = cost, reserve, inventory, delivery base, retained records,
- 疾厄/仇難 = bottleneck, burnout, hidden leak, platform or complaint risk.

If 妻妾 is pressured while 兄弟 is weak, customers complain because communication and recovery are unclear. If 官祿 is strong but 奴僕/疾厄 are weak, the promise is good but operations cannot carry the load. If 財帛/田宅 are weak, do not expand service before fixing delivery cost and records.
