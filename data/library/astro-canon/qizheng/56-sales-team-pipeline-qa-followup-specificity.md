# Qizheng Sales Team, Pipeline, QA, And Follow-Up Specificity Pack

Derived runtime rules for owner-side sales team management, CRM pipeline hygiene, lead follow-up, call-review QA, no-show recovery, lost-deal review, and sales coaching. Use as private internal guidance only; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope Gate

Do not treat sales team QA as the same thing as sales page/script writing, paid marketing traffic, hiring a sales role, team compensation/commission plan, customer success/renewal, post-sale support, affiliate/referral partner program, or partner due diligence. This pack is for managing how leads move through the sales team after demand exists: handoff, follow-up cadence, call quality, CRM stages, rep coaching, pipeline hygiene, demo/consult attendance, no-show recovery, lost-deal review, and whether the owner should keep selling or let reps handle calls.

Trigger examples: sales team process, sales QA, CRM pipeline, lead follow-up, follow-up sequence, lead handoff, call review, call recording, lost deal review, no-show recovery, sales coaching, sales manager dashboard, pipeline hygiene, stage conversion, close rate, demo booking, consult follow-up, objection library in practice, sales rep quality.

## Evidence Chain

1. 三主 / 命度 / 度主 / 身主: owner sales-control, enforcement of standards, and whether the owner can inspect without micromanaging.
2. 官祿: sales authority, process standard, CRM discipline, manager dashboard, and who owns the final close standard.
3. 兄弟: follow-up messages, call notes, review scripts in practice, reminder cadence, lead handoff, and evidence trail.
4. 妻妾: lead/customer counterparty, consult/demo buyer readiness, objection pressure, closing friction, no-show pattern.
5. 奴僕: sales reps, setters/closers, team execution, call quality, training adoption, and whether reps follow the playbook.
6. 財帛 / 田宅: pipeline value, cash conversion, CRM/data asset, retained lead list, revenue forecast, and leakage between stage and cash.
7. 福德: trust climate, morale, referral readiness, buyer confidence after sales conversation, and whether pressure damages goodwill.
8. 疾厄 / 仇難: mis-selling, overpromise, no-show, ghosting, lost deal, refund seed, compliance/liability, and internal conflict.
9. 行限 / 流年: timing for owner-led calls, rep handoff, QA audit, pipeline cleanup, coaching sprint, or pausing team scale.

## Decision Vocabulary

Final call must choose a concrete sales-ops action: keep owner selling / assign sales rep only to qualified calls / implement CRM stages / daily pipeline review / call recording QA / coach reps / tighten lead handoff / add follow-up SLA / add no-show recovery / run lost-deal review / pause scaling sales team / move calls back to owner / replace rep / improve script before scaling.
