# 七政四餘 · Business, Investment, And Contract Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Qizheng/Guolao. Use as operational rules only; do not expose raw OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about opening a shop, starting a business, investing, buying/selling, signing a contract, taking a partner, client deals, suppliers, loans, cashflow, or expansion.

Do not flatten business into "money good/bad." Split the business into cashflow, asset base, work channel, counterparties, contract risk, and timing.

## Business Chain

Read in this order:

1. **命主 / 命度 / 度主 / 身主**: whether the native can carry risk and manage execution.
2. **官祿**: operating channel, role, business model, authority, regulation.
3. **財帛**: cashflow, sales, circulating money.
4. **田宅**: retained asset, premises, inventory, property, capital base.
5. **妻妾 / 兄弟 / 奴僕**: formal counterparty, partner, client, supplier, staff, and team.
6. **疾厄 / 福德**: debt burden, hidden cost, stress, reserves, and ability to absorb loss.
7. **行限 / 限度主 / 洞微百六限 / target-year 木土**: whether the period opens growth or forces structure.

## Action Calls

- **ลุย**: 官祿 and 財帛/田宅 support repeat, 三主 can carry it, and timing opens the topic.
- **ลุยแบบมีเงื่อนไข**: business channel is real but 仇/難, weak 田宅, contract/counterparty pressure, or heavy 土 requires staged spending and written controls.
- **รอก่อน**: natal promise exists but 行限/木土 or counterparty palace is heavy; prepare cash reserve, documents, and team first.
- **ไม่ควร**: 官祿, 財帛/田宅, counterparty palace, and timing all repeat pressure.

## Contract And Partner Rules

- 妻妾 in non-romantic business questions = one-to-one contract/counterparty. Keep marriage language out.
- 兄弟/奴僕 pressure = team, staff, peers, supplier, or operational people cost.
- 財帛 strong but 田宅 weak = sales can move but retained profit/capital discipline is weak.
- 田宅 strong but 財帛 weak = assets/premises exist but cashflow needs planning.
- 官祿 strong with 疾厄 pressure = work can grow but debt, compliance, workload, or hidden cost must be controlled.

## Output Contract

Every business answer must include:

- one business-action line naming the exact action and palace,
- one 官祿 operating-channel line,
- one 財帛/田宅 cashflow-versus-asset line,
- one counterparty/contract line from 妻妾/兄弟/奴僕,
- one timing line from 行限/限度主/木土 when supplied,
- one concrete call: ลุย / ลุยแบบมีเงื่อนไข / รอก่อน / ไม่ควร,
- one practical control: cash reserve, staged investment, contract review, partner boundary, or compliance step.
