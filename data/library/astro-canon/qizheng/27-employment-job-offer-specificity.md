# 七政四餘 · Employment, Job Offer, Interview, Promotion, And Salary Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Qizheng/Guolao. Use as operational rules only; do not expose raw OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about applying for a job, interview, new job, job offer, accepting work, promotion, title, salary, boss, workplace, resignation, transfer, contract role, or employment timing.

Do not answer employment from 官祿 alone. Split role/status, salary, hiring process, employer/contract, team climate, workload, and timing.

## Employment Chain

Read in this order:

1. **命主 / 命度 / 度主 / 身主**: whether the native can carry the role and execute consistently.
2. **官祿**: job role, title, promotion, status, employer structure, formal authority.
3. **財帛**: salary, compensation, commission, cash benefit.
4. **兄弟**: resume, interview, messages, internal communication, shortlisting, documents.
5. **妻妾 / 奴僕**: employer offer/contract, hiring manager, open counterparty, team, staff, coworkers.
6. **疾厄 / 福德**: workload, stress, health/routine pressure, psychological ease, reserve.
7. **遷移 / 田宅**: relocation, branch transfer, remote/foreign work, workplace base.
8. **行限 / 限度主 / 洞微百六限 / target-year 木土**: whether the period opens promotion or forces duty.

## Topic Rules

- **Interview / application**: 兄弟 paperwork/message first, then 官祿 employer status and timing.
- **Job offer / accept role**: 官祿 role + 妻妾 contract/counterparty + 財帛 salary + 疾厄 workload.
- **Promotion / title**: 官祿 + 格局/貴格/恩用 support + timing; do not claim promotion from one lucky star.
- **Salary negotiation**: 財帛 first, then 官祿 authority, 妻妾 counterparty, 兄弟 communication.
- **Resignation / job change**: compare current-work pressure from 官祿/疾厄 with new-path support from 遷移/財帛/timing.

## Action Calls

- **รับ/ลุย**: 官祿 and 財帛 support repeat, employer lane is manageable, and timing opens work/status.
- **รับแบบมีเงื่อนไข**: role is real but workload, team, contract, or salary retention needs negotiation.
- **ต่อรอง/รอผลก่อน**: interview/documents or timing is active but not settled.
- **ไม่ควรรับ/ยังไม่ควรลาออก**: role, salary, workload, and timing repeat pressure without enough support.

## Output Contract

Every employment answer must include:

- one exact employment-action line naming apply/interview/offer/promotion/salary/resignation,
- one 官祿 role-status-employer line,
- one 財帛 salary/compensation line,
- one 兄弟 interview-document-communication line,
- one 妻妾/奴僕 employer-contract-team line,
- one workload/stress line from 疾厄/福德 when relevant,
- one timing line from 行限/木土 when supplied,
- one concrete call: รับ/ลุย / รับแบบมีเงื่อนไข / ต่อรองหรือรอผลก่อน / ไม่ควรรับหรือยังไม่ควรลาออก.
