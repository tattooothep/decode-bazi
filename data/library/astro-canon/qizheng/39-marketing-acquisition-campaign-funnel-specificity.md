## Campaign-Chain Rule

Traffic is not the same as customers. Require a chain:

- 兄弟/相貌 = message and ad creative,
- 奴僕/福德 = audience and traffic pool,
- 妻妾 = lead quality and conversion counterparty,
- 官祿 = offer authority,
- 財帛/田宅 = CAC recovery, ROAS, and retained campaign asset,
- 疾厄/仇難 = wasted spend, complaint, bad leads, platform leak, support burden.

If 兄弟/相貌 are strong but 妻妾/財帛 are weak, the campaign gets attention but does not pay back. If 奴僕 is strong but 兄弟 is weak, the audience exists but the hook/copy is wrong. If 財帛 is pressured and 疾厄/仇難 dominate, do not scale paid ads.
