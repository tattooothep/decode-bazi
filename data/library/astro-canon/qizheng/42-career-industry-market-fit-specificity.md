## Fit Chain

1. **Three lords**: 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主 show the native's core operating style, stamina, and whether they can sustain the work style.
2. **官祿**: profession, role, title, authority, institution, business model, and public duty.
3. **財帛 / 田宅**: earning lane, asset base, inventory, premises, data/property/storefront, and whether the industry can retain money.
4. **兄弟**: writing, speaking, sales language, documents, code, teaching, consulting, trading, media, and skill transmission.
5. **奴僕 / 妻妾**: staff, vendors, clients, customers, partners, platforms, and counterparty-heavy industries.
6. **遷移 / 相貌**: outside market, travel, public-facing work, online reach, visible brand, and foreign/non-local market.
7. **福德**: taste, advisory value, spiritual/inner work, comfort market, luxury, healing/support, and the work that keeps the mind usable.
8. **疾厄 / 仇難**: industry fatigue, hidden cost, technical debt, conflict, service burden, risk, or work that burns the native out.
9. **行限 / 限度主 / 木土**: when to pick, switch, specialize, open a shop, go independent, build portfolio, or stay employed.

## Fit Verdict Rule

Career fit is not the same as money fit.

- If 官祿 is strong but 財帛/田宅 weak, the role suits status but money retention needs structure.
- If 財帛 is strong but 官祿 weak, money can come from trade/assets but career identity may feel unstable.
- If 兄弟 is strong, use communication, teaching, writing, code, sales, consulting, documentation, or media as a bridge.
- If 奴僕/妻妾 is stressed, avoid people-heavy industries or use contracts/SOPs before scaling.
- If 疾厄/仇難 repeats, avoid high-conflict/high-emergency/high-maintenance industries even if money looks attractive.
