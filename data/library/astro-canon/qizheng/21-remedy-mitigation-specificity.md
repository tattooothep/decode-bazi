## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks "แก้ยังไง", "เสริมยังไง", "ลดแรงเสีย", "รับมือยังไง", "ป้องกันอะไร", "ทำอะไรให้ดีขึ้น", "แก้ดวง", "เสริมดวง", or mitigation/remedy questions in Qizheng / Guolao.

The answer must convert chart evidence into concrete risk-reduction and strengthening steps. Do not promise that a ritual or object will erase the chart.

## Mitigation Chain

1. Identify the stressed topic palace: 官祿, 財帛/田宅, 妻妾, 疾厄, 遷移, 兄弟/奴僕, 福德, or 相貌.
2. Identify the pressure source: 仇星, 難星, 落陷/弱 status, damaged palace lord, repeated bad 格局, or difficult 行限/木土 activation.
3. Identify available support: 恩星, 用星, strong 命主, strong 命度/度主, strong 身主, helpful 三方, or good timing window.
4. Convert into mitigation: reduce exposure to the pressure source, use the supporting palace/person/channel, and time action away from repeated pressure when possible.
5. If the packet lacks month/day timing, give year/limit-level mitigation only and do not invent auspicious dates.

## Remedy Rules

- For work stress, use 官祿 support and reduce 仇/難 exposure through role clarity, documentation, and controlled commitments.
- For money risk, use 財帛/田宅 evidence; mitigation is budgeting, contract review, reserve, staged investment, and avoiding all-in timing.
- For relationship/counterparty pressure, use 妻妾 plus 福德/兄弟/奴僕; mitigation is boundary, third-party documentation, and choosing helpers carefully.
- For health/routine pressure, use 疾厄 and 身主; mitigation is rest, check-up, sleep, workload reduction, and professional care when needed.
- For inner pressure, use 福德/相貌/身主; mitigation is environment, rest, spiritual practice as support, not as guaranteed cure.

## Output Contract

For remedy/mitigation answers, include:

- one stressed-palace line naming the exact problem area,
- one pressure-source line naming 仇/難/status/格局/timing,
- one support-resource line naming 恩/用/命主/度主/身主 or helpful palace,
- three concrete mitigation steps tied to chart evidence,
- one timing/precision line saying year/limit/month/day support or unavailable,
- one clear warning not to treat ritual/objects as guaranteed replacement for practical action.
