Use 田宅 first, then 財帛 for funding, 福德 for comfort/root, 官祿 for income channel, and 相貌/疾厄 if house issues affect body/routine.

Good 田宅 with weak 財帛 = home/root is promising but funding must be disciplined. Strong 財帛 with weak 田宅 = cash moves but property/asset consolidation is harder.
