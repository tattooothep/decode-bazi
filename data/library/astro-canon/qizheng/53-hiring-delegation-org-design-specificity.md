# Qizheng Hiring, Delegation, And Org Design Specificity Pack

Derived runtime rules for owner-side hiring, delegation, and organization design. Use as private internal guidance only; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope Gate

Do not treat hiring/delegation as the same thing as applying for a job, generic people networking, customer support backlog, partnership program, marketing traffic, or tech automation. This pack is for building the operator's team: who to hire, what role to delegate, how to structure reporting, when to use contractor/VA/manager, and when to delay/fire.

Trigger examples: hire employee, contractor, freelancer, VA, assistant, ops manager, sales role, support role, specialist, manager; write job description; interview candidates; org chart; team structure; delegation; outsource; paid trial; promote internal lead; replace bad hire; fire with documentation.

## Evidence Chain

1. 三主 / 命度 / 度主 / 身主: owner load, delegation capacity, personal bottleneck, boundary enforcement, and whether the owner can carry manager work.
2. 官祿: role standard, management authority, org chart, accountability, reporting line, performance standard, and what function must be professionalized first.
3. 兄弟: job description, interview language, training notes, SOP communication, handoff clarity, and candidate screening questions.
4. 奴僕: staff, team, contractor pool, VA layer, manager layer, handoff capacity, and whether the person can scale through people.
5. 妻妾: working agreement, contractor terms, employment expectation, scope, compensation promise, non-romantic commitment.
6. 財帛 / 田宅: payroll budget, retained process assets, tool budget, margin after labor, and whether the hire creates ROI or fixed burden.
7. 福德: culture, loyalty, morale, trust, psychological safety, and whether a small team stays stable.
8. 疾厄 / 仇難: bad hire, hidden workload, liability, churn, sabotage, unclear ownership, conflict, and firing/documentation risk.
9. 行限 / 流年: timing for hire, trial, promotion, restructure, replacement, or delay.

## Decision Vocabulary

Final call must choose concrete hiring action, not vague team advice: do not hire yet / hire contractor / hire VA / hire assistant / hire ops manager / hire sales role / hire support role / hire specialist / outsource / fractional manager / build SOP first / write job description / run paid trial / promote internal lead / reduce team / replace bad hire / fire with documentation.

## Answer Contract

Every hiring/delegation answer must include:

- one 三主 owner-load/delegation-capacity line
- one 官祿 role-standard/management-authority line
- one 兄弟 JD/interview/SOP line
- one 奴僕 staff/team/contractor line
- one 妻妾 working-agreement/expectation line
- one 財帛/田宅 payroll-ROI/assets line
- one 福德 culture/loyalty/morale line
- one 疾厄/仇難 bad-hire/conflict/liability line
- one 行限/流年 timing line
- one concrete action line using the decision vocabulary, e.g. hire ops manager only if authority, payroll, staff, and timing evidence support it.
