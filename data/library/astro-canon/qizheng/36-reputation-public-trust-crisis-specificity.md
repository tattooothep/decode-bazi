## Purpose

Use this pack for reputation, public image, credibility, reviews, comments, gossip, scandal, public backlash, defamation, being attacked online, platform ban, public apology, and "will people still trust me" questions.

Do not answer from fame or risk language alone. Split visibility, credibility, accusation, audience reaction, money/status damage, and repair timing.

## Core Chain

1. **Three lords**: 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主 show personal standing, resilience, and whether the native can carry public pressure.
2. **Public face**: 相貌 shows visible image, presentation, and how people see the native.
3. **Status and formal name**: 官祿 shows title, role, authority, brand promise, and professional reputation.
4. **Speech and comments**: 兄弟 shows words, posts, documents, replies, rumors, and comment flow.
5. **Crowd and allies**: 奴僕/福德 show followers, helpers, community, inner calm, and whether support absorbs or amplifies attack.
6. **Opponent and accusation**: 妻妾 shows open counterparty, buyer, sponsor, platform, or accuser when the question is public conflict.
7. **Damage and repair**: 財帛/田宅 show money, retained base, assets, and whether reputation damage turns into business damage.
8. **Risk markers**: 疾厄, 仇星/難星, 落陷, repeated hard patterns, and bad timing show escalation or hidden pressure.
9. **Timing**: 行限/限度主/洞微百六限 and target-year 木土 activation decide whether to address, pause, repair, or relaunch.

## Trust-Chain Rule

Public visibility is not public trust. Require a chain:

- 相貌/官祿 = people can identify the person or brand,
- 兄弟 = message, evidence, reply, or rumor path,
- 奴僕/福德 = audience support or emotional/crowd absorption,
- 妻妾 = accuser, customer, platform, sponsor, or counterparty,
- 財帛/田宅 = actual material consequence or retained base.

If 相貌/官祿 are strong but 兄弟 or 奴僕 are damaged, say "visible but trust is unstable." If 財帛/田宅 are protected, say the noise may hurt image more than survival. If 財帛/田宅 are also hit, warn that reputation damage can become cashflow or asset damage.

## Crisis And Repair Rule

For review/backlash/scandal questions, name the situation:

- **misunderstanding**: 兄弟 weak but 妻妾/財帛 not deeply harmed,
- **customer conflict**: 妻妾 and 財帛 involved,
- **public pile-on**: 奴僕/福德 involved with hard timing,
- **formal/status damage**: 官祿 involved,
- **hidden drain**: 疾厄/仇難 and weak 三主 involved.

Repair uses the strongest support axis: clarify message (兄弟), improve conduct/promise (官祿), rebuild visual consistency (相貌), direct customer care (妻妾), community proof (奴僕), or slow asset/base repair (田宅).
