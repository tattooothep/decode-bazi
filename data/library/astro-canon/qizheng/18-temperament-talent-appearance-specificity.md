## Purpose

Use this pack for questions about personality, temperament, strengths, weaknesses, talent, skill, charisma, appearance, body style, personal brand, and "what kind of person is this chart".

Do not answer from star nature alone. In Qizheng, personality must be anchored to 命宮, 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主, 相貌, and star status.

## Core Chain

1. **命宮**: sign/palace baseline and immediate style.
2. **命主**: governing temperament and decision style.
3. **命度 / 度主**: precise talent/temperament anchor; use 宿/degree/lord/status when supplied.
4. **身宮 / 身主**: how the personality becomes behavior, body, routine, and delivery.
5. **相貌**: appearance, presentation, aura, and first impression.
6. **星情**: star nature only after role/status/palace is named.
7. **恩用仇難 and status**: what becomes a strength, what becomes a flaw, and what needs discipline.

## Temperament / Personality

Read temperament from repeated evidence:

- 命主 and 度主 show the core operating style.
- 身主 shows how the person actually behaves under pressure.
- 恩/用 stars show natural strengths.
- 仇/難 stars show friction, insecurity, or overuse.
- 廟旺/升殿 makes the trait usable; 落陷 makes it unstable or defensive.

## Talent / Skill

Use 官祿 for professional talent, 兄弟 for communication/learning, 財帛 for value-making, 福德 for taste/inner resource, and 遷移 for public/outside application.

Separate:

- raw ability,
- usable skill,
- monetizable skill,
- public-facing skill,
- discipline needed to make it reliable.

## Appearance / Personal Brand · 相貌

Use 相貌, 命宮, 身主, Sun/Moon-like visibility, and star status. Do not promise literal beauty from one star. Describe presentation style, aura, recognizability, and body/face impression symbolically.
