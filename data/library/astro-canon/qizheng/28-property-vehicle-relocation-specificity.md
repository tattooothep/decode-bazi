# 七政四餘 · Property, Vehicle, Residence, And Relocation Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Qizheng/Guolao. It condenses retained Guolao/Xingxue/Xingping/Qizheng source groups into operational reading rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about buying, selling, renting, building, renovating, land, house, condo, shopfront, vehicle, moving home, relocation, mortgage, property documents, or whether a residence/asset is good for them.

Do not answer property questions from a generic wealth verdict. Separate the asset itself, the funding channel, document/contract risk, relocation pressure, and timing.

## Evidence Chain

1. **三主 baseline**: 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主 show whether the native can carry the asset and disruption.
2. **田宅**: house, land, residence, base, stored assets, repair condition, family-root pressure.
3. **財帛**: cash, affordability, mortgage/debt load, liquidity after purchase.
4. **官祿**: income stream used to pay for the asset and status effect of workplace/shopfront.
5. **兄弟 / 妻妾**: documents, agent, seller, counterparty, contract, negotiation channel.
6. **遷移**: moving, relocation, distance, outside opportunity, commute, leaving the home base.
7. **疾厄 / 福德**: hidden defect, maintenance burden, sleep/rest comfort, stress from residence.
8. **恩用仇難 + 廟旺落陷**: whether helpful stars can carry the topic or harmful stars create leaks.
9. **行限 / 限度主 / target-year 木土**: use only supplied timing rows for the action window.

## Property / Land / House

Read 田宅 first. Then check 財帛 for money and 官祿 for income support.

Separate:

- asset quality and whether it is stable,
- affordability and debt pressure,
- repair/hidden-cost risk,
- family/elder pressure,
- resale or retention quality,
- whether the place improves rest and life base.

Strong 田宅 with weak 財帛 means the house may be good but payment pressure must be controlled. Strong 財帛 with weak 田宅 means money moves, but property selection or retention is the weak point.

## Vehicle / Commute / Movement Asset

Treat vehicle as a moving asset: combine 田宅 for owned asset, 遷移 for movement/road/commute, 財帛 for payment, and 疾厄 for accident/maintenance strain.

Separate:

- usefulness for work/life,
- price and financing,
- maintenance/repair risk,
- accident/overuse pressure,
- whether to buy now, delay, repair, or change model/budget.

Do not turn one good 遷移 sign into a buy verdict if 財帛 or 疾厄 is clearly stressed.

## Relocation / Moving Home

Use 遷移 and 田宅 together. 遷移 shows the pull outward; 田宅 shows the old/new base. 財帛 and 官祿 decide whether the move is affordable and useful.

Separate:

- move for work,
- move for family,
- move for money,
- move for health/rest,
- long-distance/foreign move,
- temporary move versus permanent base.

## Contract And Document Guard

For any purchase, sale, lease, mortgage, title deed, construction permit, or agent question, include 兄弟/妻妾/官祿 evidence if supplied. If those fields are weak or not supplied, say the reading can judge the asset direction but not the contract detail.

## Output Contract

Every answer using this pack must include:

- one 三主 carrying-capacity line,
- one 田宅 asset/base line,
- one 財帛 affordability/debt line,
- one 遷移 movement/relocation/vehicle line when relevant,
- one contract/document/counterparty line when buying/selling/leasing,
- one timing line when a year/month/date is asked,
- one decisive practical call: buy / buy with conditions / rent / wait / do not buy / sell / renovate / move / stay.
