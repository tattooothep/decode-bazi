# Qizheng Education, Exam, Certification, Writing, And Learning Specificity Pack

Derived operational summary for education, exam/certification, writing/speaking/content, teacher/support, cost, and timing questions in 七政四餘. Use only as internal interpretation rules; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope

Use this pack when the question asks about:
- study, school, university, course, tutoring, certification, license exam, thesis, research, scholarship, admission, grades, exam pass/fail,
- writing, speaking, presentation, teaching, content, publication, portfolio, document submission,
- teacher, mentor, senior support, study partner, paper review, and learning pressure.

If the question is only legal approval or government paperwork, also use the authority/document pack. If it is only job interview, also use the employment pack. If it is broad relocation or foreign study, include travel/relocation evidence but do not let relocation override the education question.

## Evidence Chain

1. **三主 baseline**: start from 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主. If these are weak, the person needs structure, rest, or a slower exam plan even when the topic palace is good. If these are strong, the person can push through pressure.
2. **兄弟**: learning exchange, reading/writing, communication, classmates, study partner, short documents, speaking, presentation, and evidence flow.
3. **官祿**: formal exam outcome, certificate, title, degree, license, institutional recognition, public evaluation, and status gained from study.
4. **福德**: memory, concentration, sleep, recovery, morale, mental load, inner confidence, and the ability to sustain long preparation.
5. **財帛 / 田宅**: tuition, course cost, scholarship cashflow, family funding, equipment, study room, and the stability of the study base.
6. **遷移**: foreign study, online/outside institution, travel for exam, publication beyond home base, or learning with outsiders.
7. **行限 / 限度主 / 洞微百六限 / target-year 木土**: timing permission. If only year-level timing is supplied, answer as year-level, not exact month/day.

## Reading Rules

- Do not answer "study is good" from 兄弟 alone. Require at least one capacity line from 三主 or degree/body lord, one topic line from 兄弟/官祿, and one timing or cost/support line.
- For exam pass/fail, 官祿 and 兄弟 must both be weighed: 官祿 shows formal recognition, 兄弟 shows answer sheet, writing, evidence, and communication. Strong 官祿 with weak 兄弟 means the institution/title is reachable but preparation or paperwork is the bottleneck.
- For writing/speaking/content, 兄弟 is first, then 官祿 for public result, 福德 for sustained focus, and 遷移 for outside audience.
- For scholarship or funding, 財帛 and 田宅 cannot be skipped. Good study signs with weak 財帛 mean study is possible but cost planning is mandatory.
- For teacher/mentor support, use people/support indicators only if the packet supplies them; do not invent a helper.
- If the question asks "should I enroll / take exam / submit / publish", give an action call, not a generic personality reading.

## Answer Contract

For an education/exam/writing question, write:
1. one 三主 capacity line,
2. one 兄弟 learning/communication line,
3. one 官祿 exam/certification/status line,
4. one 福德 focus/rest/morale line when relevant,
5. one cost/support/timing line from 財帛/田宅/遷移/行限,
6. one decisive practical call: sit exam / submit / publish / retake / delay / change course, with the condition that changes the verdict.

If timing is incomplete, say the exact missing layer once, then still answer at the available precision.
