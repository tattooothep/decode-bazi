# Qizheng Customer, Audience, And Persona-Fit Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Qizheng/Guolao. It condenses retained Guolao/Xingxue/Xingping/Qizheng source groups into operational rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks who to sell to, which customer group fits them, who should be their target audience, what client persona is suitable, what type of followers/subscribers/customers convert, or which customers should be avoided.

Do not treat customer fit as the same thing as marketing traffic. Split audience persona, trust path, buying motive, conversion channel, repeat behavior, team/service burden, money quality, and timing.

## Persona Chain

1. **Three lords**: 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主 show the native's delivery style and the customer temperament they can sustain.
2. **妻妾**: direct clients, buyers, contract counterparties, spouse-like one-to-one customer relationship, and whether sales need trust before payment.
3. **奴僕**: community, staff-supported service, group customers, subscribers, followers, vendors, and volume audience.
4. **兄弟**: message, copy, teaching, consultation, documents, chat, sales script, code/spec handoff, and how customers understand the offer.
5. **相貌 / 遷移**: public-facing attraction, online reach, outside market, foreign/non-local customers, and first impression.
6. **官祿**: customer status level, institutional buyer, professional authority, B2B/B2G, and whether customers buy because of expertise.
7. **財帛 / 田宅**: buyer budget, retained cash, subscription/stored value, property/data/product asset, and whether customers leave money behind.
8. **福德**: comfort, trust, taste, emotional safety, spiritual/advisory value, luxury/inner-life market, and customer satisfaction.
9. **疾厄 / 仇難**: complaint pattern, high-maintenance client, refund risk, hidden service cost, burnout, and customers to avoid.
10. **行限 / 限度主 / 木土**: when to target a new segment, raise buyer quality, narrow persona, or stop serving a draining group.

## Customer Type Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic star keywords:

- **妻妾 strong and clean**: direct client, one-to-one service, premium buyer, partner-led sale, referral sale.
- **奴僕 strong and clean**: group customer, community, membership, subscriber, staff-assisted service, platform audience.
- **兄弟 strong**: customers who need explanation, education, documentation, chat, consulting, writing, code/spec clarity.
- **相貌/遷移 strong**: public/online/foreign/non-local audience; customers convert from visibility, travel, public image, or outside field.
- **官祿 strong**: institutional, professional, enterprise, authority-seeking, status-sensitive, regulated buyer.
- **財帛/田宅 strong**: customers with budget, asset purchase, retained subscription, property/data/product value, repeat stored value.
- **福德 strong**: wellness, comfort, spiritual, advisory, luxury, taste-led, emotionally safe customer segment.
- **疾厄/仇難 stressed**: avoid clients who require emergency response, vague scope, conflict, refunds, underpriced service, or endless revisions.

## Verdict Rules

- If 妻妾 is stronger than 奴僕, prefer fewer high-trust buyers over mass audience.
- If 奴僕 is stronger than 妻妾, build community/membership/platform but protect service capacity.
- If 兄弟 is weak, avoid customers who need heavy explanation unless documentation or sales script is fixed first.
- If 財帛 looks good but 疾厄/仇難 repeats, customers pay but drain operations; narrow scope, require deposits, or cap support.
- If 相貌/遷移 is good but 妻妾 weak, visibility can rise while conversion remains weak; do not scale ads before offer trust is fixed.
