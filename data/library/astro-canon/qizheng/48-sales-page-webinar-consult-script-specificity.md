# Qizheng Sales Page, Webinar, And Consultation Script Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Qizheng. It condenses public-domain and licensed-internal operational rules into usable rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about sales page, landing page copy, webinar script, workshop script, consultation script, discovery call, sales call, closing script, DM pitch, proposal pitch, VSL, launch presentation, pitch deck, objection handling, story angle, proof order, CTA wording, or how to write the sales conversation so buyers understand and buy.

Do not treat sales script fit as the same thing as marketing traffic, customer persona, price level, product category, guarantee boundary, or delivery model. This pack decides the persuasion structure, proof order, story angle, objection sequence, call-to-action, and whether the user should use a written sales page, live webinar, short consult call, DM close, or proof-first proposal.

## Sales Message Chain

1. **三主 / 命度 / 度主 / 身主**: the seller voice, confidence, pressure capacity, personal proof, and whether the person can carry a direct pitch or needs a quieter proof-led script.
2. **兄弟**: copy, script, explanation, DM, FAQ, call flow, slides, objection wording, and whether the message is clear enough for the buyer.
3. **相貌 / 官祿**: first impression, authority, status, public promise, expert positioning, and whether the page/webinar should lead with credentials, result, story, or diagnosis.
4. **妻妾**: direct buyer, sales call, objections, trust, agreement, close, and whether the script should qualify first, educate first, or ask for a decision quickly.
5. **奴僕**: webinar room, group audience, community reaction, affiliates, platform comments, and whether a group presentation or one-to-one close fits better.
6. **財帛 / 田宅**: offer value, proof library, case assets, retained knowledge, bonus stack, payment action, and whether the CTA should emphasize money, asset, stability, or access.
7. **福德**: desire, emotional comfort, story, aspiration, and the inner reason the buyer trusts the pitch.
8. **疾厄 / 仇難**: overclaim, fear-based copy, manipulative pressure, refund trigger, burnout from calls, buyer resentment, and claims that should be removed.
9. **行限 / 流年 trigger**: when to write, test, present live, ask for sales, revise script, or avoid a hard close.

## Format Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic star keywords:

- **兄弟 + 官祿 strong**: write a clear expert sales page, proof-first webinar, or structured proposal.
- **相貌 strong, 奴僕 active**: use webinar, live workshop, community launch, or public presentation.
- **妻妾 strong, 兄弟 clean**: use consultation call, DM close, objection script, or one-to-one proposal.
- **財帛/田宅 strong**: lead with value, assets, tangible outcomes, case library, and retained benefit.
- **福德 strong**: lead with story, transformation, aspiration, and emotional relief.
- **兄弟 weak or afflicted**: shorten copy, remove jargon, make FAQ and proof clearer before scaling.
- **疾厄/仇難 active**: avoid fear copy, pressure close, exaggerated income claims, or "everyone must buy now" language.
- **妻妾/財帛 stressed**: qualify buyer before call, make price/fit clear, and avoid open-ended persuasion.

## Verdict Rules

- If the message channel is strong but buyer lane is weak, publish content and collect warm leads before sales calls.
- If buyer lane is strong but message is unclear, use a short consult script with diagnostic questions rather than a long sales page.
- If authority is strong, open with diagnosis and proof; if 福德 is stronger, open with story and relief.
- If group/audience lane is strong, use webinar or workshop; if direct counterpart lane is stronger, use 1:1 call or DM close.
- If 疾厄/仇難 repeats, reduce hype, reduce pressure, and add proof/qualification before CTA.
