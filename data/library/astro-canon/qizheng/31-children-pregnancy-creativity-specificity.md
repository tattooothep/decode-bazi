# Qizheng Children, Pregnancy, Protégé, And Creative Output Specificity Pack

Derived operational summary for children, pregnancy/fertility symbolism, younger people, students/protégés, creative output, and side-project questions in 七政四餘. Use only as internal interpretation rules; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope

Use this pack when the question asks about:
- having children, pregnancy timing, fertility tendency, relationship with children, parenting pressure,
- younger people, students, protégés, dependents, junior staff when the question frames them as children/protégés,
- creative output, authored work, side project, thing the native produces, launch of a personal creation.

Medical policy: astrology may describe symbolic readiness, pressure, timing, and support. It must not diagnose infertility, pregnancy, miscarriage, fetal condition, or medical outcome. If the user asks for medical certainty, state that medical confirmation is required, then answer only the symbolic chart layer.

## Evidence Chain

1. **三主 baseline**: 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主. The native's capacity must be weighed before judging children or creative production.
2. **男女**: first palace for children, descendants, produced work, protégés, and creative offspring.
3. **福德**: joy, emotional readiness, rest, mental ease, fertility symbolism, pleasure, and ability to sustain care.
4. **妻妾**: relationship/partner cooperation when the child question depends on a partner or marriage.
5. **疾厄 / 身主**: body pressure and recovery. Use as symbolic risk-management only; do not turn it into diagnosis.
6. **財帛 / 田宅**: cost, home readiness, childcare budget, resources for a creative project, studio/base.
7. **官祿 / 兄弟 / 遷移**: public launch, project distribution, teaching students, audience, paperwork, outside reach.
8. **行限 / 限度主 / 洞微百六限 / target-year 木土**: timing activation. If only year-level timing is present, answer year-level.

## Reading Rules

- Do not promise "will have a child" from one good 男女 sign. Require 男女, 三主, support/friction, and timing repetition.
- For pregnancy/fertility, separate: symbolic child potential, partner cooperation, body/rest pressure, money/home readiness, and timing. Add medical-confirmation guard once.
- For existing children, judge relationship and burden from 男女 plus 福德/妻妾/財帛/田宅, not from the natal self axis alone.
- For creative output or side project, read 男女 as produced work, 官祿 as public result, 兄弟 as communication/content, 財帛 as monetization, and 遷移 as outside audience.
- If 男女 is weak but 官祿/兄弟 is strong, the creative project may still work but needs discipline and support; do not convert that into pregnancy promise.

## Answer Contract

For a children/pregnancy/creative-output question, write:
1. one 三主 capacity line,
2. one 男女 children/output/protégé line,
3. one 福德 joy/rest/emotional-readiness line,
4. one 妻妾 partner-cooperation line when relevant,
5. one 疾厄/身主 body-pressure line for pregnancy/fertility questions with a medical-confirmation guard,
6. one 財帛/田宅/官祿/兄弟/遷移 practical support line matching the question,
7. one timing line,
8. one decisive practical call: try now / try with conditions / prepare first / wait / focus on project launch / delay launch / get medical or professional confirmation first.
