## Purpose

Use this pack for price, package, tier, coupon, discount, promotion, bundle, upsell, cross-sell, add-on, top-up, credit pack, paid plan, raise price, lower price, intro offer, and "what offer should I sell" questions.

Do not answer from popularity, sales, or retention alone. Split price power, perceived value, customer willingness, delivery burden, cash margin, discount leakage, and timing.

## Core Chain

1. **Three lords**: 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主 show whether the native can carry the promise behind a higher price or premium offer.
2. **Offer promise and public role**: 官祿 shows the product/service promise, authority of the offer, and whether the market can accept a premium positioning.
3. **Price and margin**: 財帛 shows price received, margin, and whether money comes cleanly from the offer.
4. **Stored value and package structure**: 田宅 shows bundle durability, stored credits, package stability, and whether value remains after promotion.
5. **Customer counterparty**: 妻妾 shows buyer, subscriber, sponsor, platform, partner, and whether the customer accepts terms.
6. **Audience and distribution**: 奴僕/福德 show user base, demand mood, supporters, perceived benefit, and emotional appetite for the offer.
7. **Copy and offer explanation**: 兄弟 shows sales page, price explanation, coupon rules, help docs, messages, and checkout clarity.
8. **Leak and hidden cost**: 疾厄, 仇星/難星, weak status, and bad timing show refund leakage, discount abuse, service burden, or margin erosion.
9. **Timing**: 行限/限度主/洞微百六限 and target-year 木土 activation decide whether to raise price, run a short promo, bundle, or wait.

## Pricing-Chain Rule

Price is not the same as demand. Require a chain:

- 官祿 = offer promise and authority,
- 財帛 = price/margin received,
- 妻妾 = buyer accepts the term,
- 奴僕/福德 = user base and perceived value,
- 兄弟 = price explanation and coupon clarity,
- 田宅 = package/credit value retained,
- 疾厄/仇難 = discount leak, refund pressure, or hidden delivery cost.

If 官祿 and 福德 are strong but 財帛 is weak, the offer has prestige but undercharges. If 財帛 is strong but 妻妾 is stressed, price may sell but creates customer friction. If 田宅 is weak, avoid long bundles or stored-credit promises.

## Discount / Promotion Rule

For coupon, launch promo, and discount questions, name the pressure:

- **safe short promo**: 兄弟 clear + 妻妾 cooperative + 財帛 still supported,
- **underpricing risk**: 官祿/F福德 strong but 財帛 weak,
- **discount abuse**: 財帛/田宅 pressure + 疾厄/仇難,
- **unclear offer**: 兄弟 weak or afflicted,
- **premium room**: 官祿/福德/妻妾 supported with clean 財帛.

Do not recommend broad discounting when the leak markers are stronger than the customer markers.
