## Purpose

Use this pack for work, money, health, year-ahead, or combined "งาน เงิน สุขภาพ" questions. It forces Qizheng answers to use the packet's precision anchors instead of broad good/bad labels.

Do not answer from `verdictTh`, one house badge, one star nature, or one 木土 annual row alone.

## Universal Anchor

Every topic must start from:

1. **命主**: governing star, status, house, and 恩/用/仇/難 relation.
2. **命度 / 度主**: precision anchor; stronger than generic sign language.
3. **身宮 / 身主**: practical delivery, stamina, and whether the person can carry the event.
4. **Asked palace**: 官祿, 財帛, 田宅, 疾厄, 福德, 遷移, 妻妾, etc.
5. **Timing**: 行限/限度主/洞微百六限 first, target-year 木土 row second.

If these anchors point in different directions, write the split and then decide which side is stronger from 度主 + asked palace + timing.

## Career / 官祿 Chain

Read career in this order:

1. 官祿 palace, 官祿 lord, stars inside 官祿.
2. 命主/度主/身主 relation to 官祿, 福德, 命宮, 遷移.
3. 恩/用 support versus 仇/難 pressure.
4. 廟旺/升殿/入垣 versus 落陷/退/weak status.
5. 貴格/官祿格/官福互垣/拱夾/三方 only if packet lists them.
6. 行限/限度主 and target-year 木土 activation.

Name the career channel from star/lord evidence:

- 日/月: public visibility, leadership, audience/client response.
- 木: teaching, advising, growth, planning, patronage.
- 火: action, competition, technical/urgent work, conflict handling.
- 土: systems, duty, institution, long-term responsibility.
- 金: finance, value, contract, precision, aesthetics.
- 水: documents, trade, analysis, communication, logistics.
- 羅/計/月孛/紫氣: nonstandard, hidden, disruptive, technical, spiritual, research, or backstage channel depending on role/status.

## Wealth / 財帛 and 田宅 Chain

Separate money into lanes:

1. **財帛**: cashflow and money movement.
2. **田宅**: retained assets, property, root, storage.
3. **官祿**: income through work.
4. **福德**: fortune base, ease, support, spending comfort.
5. **遷移/奴僕** when supplied: outside clients, network, helpers, people-related gains or losses.

Weighting:

- 財帛/田宅 lord strong + 恩/用 support = easier inflow/retention.
- 官祿 strong but 財帛 weak = work can create money but retention leaks or delays.
- 財帛 strong but 田宅 weak = money moves but asset consolidation needs discipline.
- 仇/難 or hostile star in money lanes = leakage, pressure, debt, repair, people cost, or unstable cashflow.
- 富格/田財互垣/財星合格 supports money only after palace/lord/timing evidence is named.

Do not call "wealth" from a single lucky star.

## Health / 疾厄 and 身主 Chain

Use health symbolically and safely:

1. 疾厄 palace and lord.
2. 身主/身宮 and 命度主: stamina and ability to carry pressure.
3. 仇/難, 火/土/羅/計 style pressure, and hostile stars only from packet roles.
4. 疾格 or harm bucket only if packet lists the pattern.
5. 行限/限度主 target period and 木土 year activation.

Allowed: vitality, stamina, sleep/rest, routine pressure, workload risk, check-up, discipline, recovery management.

Forbidden: diagnosis, fixed disease, medication, surgery instruction, death prediction, or replacing clinicians.
