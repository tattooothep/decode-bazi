# Qizheng Travel, Foreign Field, Relocation, And Outside Opportunity Specificity Pack

Derived operational summary for travel, foreign/distant field, relocation, outside clients, public exposure, and leaving the home base in 七政四餘. Use only as internal interpretation rules; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope

Use this pack when the question asks about:
- travel, trip, commute, long-distance travel, moving city, moving country, relocation, migration, overseas opportunity,
- foreign clients, outside market, online/outside audience, work away from home, living away from family base,
- whether to go, stay, delay travel, prepare documents, or choose a destination direction.

If the question is buying/selling/renting a house, land, condo, vehicle, mortgage, title deed, or lease, use the property/vehicle pack first. If the question is visa/permit/legal approval, also use the authority/document pack. If the user asks for exact electional date/time, also use the specialty/electional guard.

## Evidence Chain

1. **三主 baseline**: 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主 show whether the native can carry movement, distance, and disruption.
2. **遷移**: first palace for outside field, travel, relocation, foreign/distant opportunity, public exposure, and being pulled away from the old base.
3. **田宅**: old/new base, home anchor, family root, residence comfort, and whether leaving home destabilizes the native.
4. **官祿**: work reason for travel/relocation, career status outside home, institutional purpose.
5. **財帛**: travel cost, relocation budget, foreign income, cashflow, exchange risk, and whether the move pays for itself.
6. **兄弟 / 妻妾 / 奴僕**: documents, communication, agent, partner, companions, clients, local helpers, counterparties.
7. **疾厄 / 福德**: travel fatigue, stress, sleep, health burden, isolation, recovery after moving.
8. **行限 / 限度主 / 洞微百六限 / target-year 木土**: timing activation. If only year-level timing is present, answer year-level.

## Reading Rules

- Do not answer relocation from 遷移 alone. Require 三主 capacity, 遷移, 田宅, money/work support, and timing.
- Strong 遷移 with weak 田宅 means the outside field calls strongly but home/root stability must be managed.
- Strong 田宅 with weak 遷移 means staying or moving slowly is safer than forcing a distant move.
- For foreign client/outside market questions, combine 遷移 with 官祿/財帛 and people/counterparty indicators. Do not treat it as tourism.
- For travel risk, separate document/companion risk, health/fatigue risk, money leak, and timing. Do not predict accidents as certainty.
- For "go or stay", give a practical call and name the condition that changes the call.

## Answer Contract

For a travel/foreign/relocation question, write:
1. one 三主 capacity-to-move line,
2. one 遷移 outside-field/travel/relocation line,
3. one 田宅 old-base/new-base line,
4. one 官祿/財帛 work-money support line,
5. one 兄弟/妻妾/奴僕 document-companion-client line when relevant,
6. one 疾厄/福德 fatigue/rest/risk-management line when relevant,
7. one timing line,
8. one decisive practical call: go / go with conditions / prepare first / delay / stay / choose closer option / avoid this trip.
