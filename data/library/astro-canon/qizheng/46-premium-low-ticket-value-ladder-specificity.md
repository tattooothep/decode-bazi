## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks whether to sell high-ticket, premium, VIP, low-ticket, starter offer, entry offer, tripwire, mass-market product, value ladder, tier ladder, backend offer, upsell path, or enterprise package.

Do not treat value-ladder fit as the same thing as exact pricing, promotion, marketing traffic, delivery model, or product category. Price level is not the same as price change. This pack decides whether the chart supports trust-heavy premium sales, low-ticket volume, entry-to-backend ladder, or a narrow middle-tier offer.

## Value-Ladder Chain

1. **Three lords**: 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主 show confidence, stamina, personal authority, and whether the native can carry premium trust or should use a lower-friction entry path.
2. **官祿**: status, authority, expertise, public promise, enterprise/B2B posture, and whether the offer can justify high-ticket positioning.
3. **財帛 / 田宅**: margin, retained value, asset base, recurring base, inventory/data/library, and whether low-ticket volume leaks cash.
4. **兄弟**: copy, explanation, sales script, proof, webinar/report/course ladder, and whether the buyer understands why a higher tier exists.
5. **妻妾**: direct buyer trust, contract risk, consultation need, objection handling, refund/refusal risk, and high-ticket closing pressure.
6. **奴僕**: community, volume audience, group buyers, affiliates, platform, sales team, and whether low-ticket or ladder scaling can be shared.
7. **福德 / 相貌**: perceived value, taste, comfort, prestige, brand aura, satisfaction, and whether premium feels natural or forced.
8. **疾厄 / 仇難**: support cost, hidden labor, cheap-client burden, premium liability, refund risk, and tier type to avoid.
9. **行限 / 限度主 / 木土**: when to raise tier, test entry offer, open premium slots, build backend, pause discounting, or narrow the ladder.

## Tier Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic star keywords:

- **官祿 + 妻妾 + 福德/相貌 strong**: high-ticket advisory, premium 1:1, VIP package, enterprise/client-specific offer.
- **兄弟 + 奴僕 strong, 妻妾 weaker**: low-ticket entry, mass-market course, group buyer, template, content-led starter offer.
- **財帛 + 田宅 strong**: subscription, stored-value tier, library, membership, backend asset, recurring ladder.
- **官祿 + 兄弟 strong**: paid report, audit, certification, professional middle-tier, authority-building starter.
- **妻妾 strong but 疾厄 active**: premium only with qualification, deposit, limited slots, and strict scope.
- **奴僕 strong but 財帛 weak**: audience exists but volume may not profit; test small before scaling low-ticket.
- **疾厄/仇難 repeats**: avoid unlimited low-ticket support, broad discounts, cheap custom work, high-ticket guarantee without proof, or too many tiers.

## Verdict Rules

- If trust/authority beats audience volume, choose high-ticket limited slots before low-ticket scale.
- If audience/SOP beats direct trust, choose low-ticket or group entry before premium sales.
- If 財帛/田宅 is strong, build a ladder with retained value instead of one-off labor.
- If 兄弟 is weak, do not create many tiers until the offer ladder is easy to explain.
- If 疾厄/仇難 repeats, simplify to one clear entry offer plus one capped backend offer.
