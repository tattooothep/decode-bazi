# Qizheng Unit Economics, Profit-Margin, Break-Even, And Payback Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about profit quality, margin, unit economics, break-even, payback, LTV/CAC, contribution margin, gross/net profit, cost per order, cost per customer, ARPU/AOV, margin floor, or whether a business model is economically worth doing.

Do not treat unit economics as the same thing as cashflow/runway, price strategy, windfall/speculation, marketing campaign optimization, retention/churn rescue, sales-team QA, team compensation, partner-risk, or technology build. If the user asks "cash left / reserve / runway / burn rate", use the cashflow pack. If the user asks "raise price / discount / coupon / upsell", use the pricing pack. If the user asks "CAC/ROAS for ad creative or funnel traffic", use the marketing pack. This pack is for whether each sale, cohort, customer, product, or plan is profitable after real costs.

## Canon Routing

Classical Qizheng money judgment must not stop at 財帛. For profit and unit economics, read 財帛 together with 官祿, 田宅, 妻妾/兄弟, 奴僕, 疾厄, and 恩用仇難:

- 三主: whether the native can enforce numbers, say no to unprofitable demand, and hold a margin discipline.
- 財帛: gross cash-in, realized revenue, margin quality, collection quality, and whether money arrives cleanly.
- 官祿: the operating model that creates profit; whether the offer is a stable profit engine or only activity.
- 田宅: fixed-cost base, retained assets, inventory/office/platform base, and break-even absorption.
- 兄弟/妻妾: messages, invoices, terms, buyer agreement, channel promise, and price-cost clarity.
- 奴僕: staff, contractor, vendor, tool, AI, SaaS, fulfillment, support, and variable cost per unit.
- 疾厄/仇難: refund drag, tax drag, bad debt, hidden labor, platform leakage, rework, disputes, and margin killers.
- 行限/流年/限度主: when the profit model can be tightened, tested, or should be paused.

## Answer Contract

For unit economics questions, answer with:

1. one 三主 margin-discipline line;
2. one 財帛 revenue-per-unit and collection-quality line;
3. one 官祿 profit-model or operating-engine line;
4. one 田宅 fixed-cost/break-even-base line;
5. one 兄弟/妻妾 price-term/invoice/channel-promise line;
6. one 奴僕 variable-cost/staff/tool/vendor line;
7. one 疾厄/仇難 hidden-cost/refund/tax/rework-risk line;
8. one timing line from 行限/流年/限度主 when available;
9. one practical verdict: raise margin floor, stop an unprofitable SKU, repackage, cap variable cost, collect before delivery, or test smaller.

Never say "good money" unless the answer names the cost line that stays controlled. Never say "scale now" unless 財帛, 官祿, 田宅, and 奴僕 do not contradict each other.
