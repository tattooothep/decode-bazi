# 七政四餘 · Star Nature Operational Summary

> Runtime summary for Fusion Qizheng. This pack promotes the usable star-nature layer from the retained Guolao/Qizheng corpus into a compact operating guide. It is not a raw transcription and must not expose source prose, OCR line order, or file paths.

## Scope And Guard

- Use this as a secondary meaning layer after the chart facts are anchored.
- Primary evidence remains: 命主/度主/身主, house ruler status, 恩星/用星/仇星/難星, 廟旺落陷, 三方對照, 格局, and 行限/限度主.
- Star nature may sharpen why a planet acts in a certain style. It must not override a fallen ruler, hostile 仇難 pressure, or timing contradiction.
- The clean retained layer supports 五行, seasonal rulership, speed/cycle, direct/retrograde behavior, and broad operational symbolism. Full verbatim tables for profession/body/organ/personality are still not clean enough for runtime claims.

## Seven Luminaries And Four Extras

### 太陽

- Core: solar/fire brightness, visibility, authority, public exposure, vitality signal.
- Motion: annual rhythm, stable and regular.
- Use when 太陽 is 命主, 度主, 恩/用/仇/難, or placed in the asked house.
- Strong and supported: the matter becomes visible, formal, publicly accountable, or connected to status.
- Pressured/fallen: visibility becomes pressure, pride conflict, burnout, or public scrutiny.

### 太陰

- Core: lunar/water reflection, feeling, rest, family/body rhythm, receptive support.
- Motion: fast monthly rhythm; shows fluctuation and short-cycle change.
- Strong and supported: adaptation, care, audience response, emotional timing, women/family support.
- Pressured/fallen: mood swing, sleep/rest drain, hidden worry, unstable support.

### 歲星 / 木星

- Core: wood growth, extension, teaching, patrons, planning horizon, benevolent expansion.
- Motion: slow twelve-year rhythm; shows long-range growth rather than instant result.
- Strong and supported: help from mentors, learning, promotion through knowledge, outside expansion.
- Pressured/fallen: overreach, promise larger than delivery, slow growth blocked by structure.

### 熒惑 / 火星

- Core: fire heat, urgency, conflict, cutting action, competitive drive.
- Motion: slower than luminaries, often felt as bursts of pressure.
- Strong and supported: courage, decisive movement, technical/action work, ability to break stagnation.
- Pressured/fallen: arguments, accidents, inflammation-style stress, haste, damage through force.

### 鎮星 / 土星

- Core: earth structure, delay, weight, duty, boundaries, endurance.
- Motion: very slow long cycle; makes topics heavy, durable, and hard to rush.
- Strong and supported: stable office, long-term assets, responsibility that becomes authority.
- Pressured/fallen: delay, burden, chronic pressure, coldness, debt of time or duty.

### 太白 / 金星

- Core: metal refinement, value, agreement, exchange, beauty, resources, precision.
- Motion: annual rhythm, often close to solar/public cycles.
- Strong and supported: money channel, negotiation, aesthetics, contract, resource extraction.
- Pressured/fallen: cost, indulgence, dispute over value, relationship/finance entanglement.

### 辰星 / 水星

- Core: water movement, messages, calculation, trade, documents, quick adjustment.
- Motion: quick annual cycle with visible variation.
- Strong and supported: documents, commerce, speech, analysis, travel logistics.
- Pressured/fallen: confusion, paperwork error, nervous overthinking, scattered decision.

### 紫氣

- Core: wood remainder; subtle growth, hidden assistance, refinement, spiritual/technical support.
- Motion: slow long cycle; acts as a background influence.
- Strong and supported: quiet helper, specialized knowledge, hidden growth channel.
- Pressured/fallen: subtle support is too weak to rescue a damaged house by itself.

### 月孛

- Core: water remainder; obscuration, unusual feeling, hidden fluctuation, nonstandard path.
- Motion: long but more changeable than main slow planets.
- Strong and supported: unconventional insight, hidden opportunity, research/behind-the-scenes work.
- Pressured/fallen: confusion, secrecy, emotional fog, unclear agreement.

### 羅睺

- Core: fire remainder; retrograde disruption, sudden heat, appetite, break in ordinary order.
- Motion: reverse eighteen-year rhythm.
- Strong and supported: disruptive breakthrough, unusual ambition, capacity to act against convention.
- Pressured/fallen: volatility, obsession, abrupt conflict, damage through excess heat.

### 計都

- Core: earth remainder; retrograde contraction, severing, restraint, old burden, hidden blockage.
- Motion: reverse eighteen-year rhythm.
- Strong and supported: discipline, cutting waste, retreat that protects the topic.
- Pressured/fallen: isolation, obstruction, loss of continuity, heavy unresolved pressure.

## How To Use In Answers

For any Qizheng verdict:

1. Start with the asked house and its ruler status.
2. Add 三主/恩用仇難 and 三方/對宮.
3. Add timing if the question asks when.
4. Then add one star-nature sentence only for the planet that is actually relevant.

Good example:

- "官祿 strong by ruler and 三方, and 土星 is 用星 there: this is not easy luck; it is career growth through duty, structure, and long responsibility."

Bad examples:

- "Mars means danger" without house/ruler/timing evidence.
- "Venus means money" when 財帛/田宅 and timing do not support money.
- Dumping all 11 star meanings in a user answer.

## User-Facing Specificity Rule

Use this pack to make the reading concrete, not longer.

- Name the planet.
- Name its role in this chart: 命主, 度主, 恩, 用, 仇, 難, in-house, aspect, timing trigger.
- Translate its nature into one practical consequence for the asked topic.
- If the planet is irrelevant to the asked topic, do not mention it.
