# Qizheng Insurance, Claim, Policy, And Coverage Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about insurance, policy coverage, claim approval, claim denial, appeal, premium, deductible, coverage limit, beneficiary, insurer, broker, adjuster, indemnity, payout, lapse, renewal, rider, exclusion, pre-existing condition, medical insurance, vehicle insurance, property insurance, business insurance, or whether to buy coverage, renew, cancel, file a claim, appeal, or wait.

Do not treat insurance as ordinary debt, tax, property purchase, health treatment, lawsuit, or generic document approval. If the question is only illness/treatment/surgery, use the health pack. If it asks whether the policy pays, whether the claim is accepted, whether coverage is enough, whether to appeal a denial, or whether to buy/renew/cancel coverage, this pack must be included.

Insurance/Claim Judgment

Qizheng insurance judgment must join 三主, 財帛, 田宅, 官祿, 妻妾, 兄弟, 奴僕, 疾厄, 恩用仇難, and 行限. Insurance is not simply "money"; it is paid protection against a named risk under contract wording and claim evidence.

- 三主/命度/度主/身主: insured-risk/exposure capacity, whether the owner carries risk responsibly, and whether a claim decision can be executed without panic.
- 財帛: premium affordability, deductible burden, reimbursement cash-in, indemnity amount, coverage limit, and whether payout actually relieves pressure.
- 田宅: insured asset base, property/vehicle/equipment/home exposure, retained reserve, and whether collateral/asset condition supports the claim.
- 官祿: insurer/company/regulator decision, claim approval authority, underwriting standard, corporate plan, and formal acceptance/rejection.
- 妻妾: insurance contract counterparty, insurer promise, beneficiary, named insured, co-insured, and dispute with the policy side.
- 兄弟: application wording, policy schedule, claim form, evidence file, medical/repair report, message trail, and deadline communication.
- 奴僕: broker, agent, adjuster, claims handler, repair vendor, hospital desk, service queue, and follow-up execution.
- 疾厄 plus 仇難: loss event, illness/accident damage, exclusion, lapse, denial reason, fraud suspicion, penalty, and hidden uncovered risk.
- 行限/限度主: claim window, renewal/lapse timing, premium due timing, appeal period, and when the insurer's decision becomes visible.

Answer Contract

For insurance/claim questions, the answer must include:

1. one 三主 insured-risk/exposure capacity line;
2. one 財帛 premium/deductible/coverage-limit/payout-cash line;
3. one 田宅 insured-asset/reserve/collateral-condition line when asset/property/vehicle/business coverage is involved, or explicitly say why it is not the main axis;
4. one 官祿 insurer/underwriting/approval-authority line;
5. one 妻妾 insurer-contract/beneficiary/counterparty promise line;
6. one 兄弟 claim-file/policy-wording/evidence/deadline line;
7. one 奴僕 broker/agent/adjuster/service-follow-up line;
8. one 疾厄/仇難 exclusion/lapse/denial/fraud-risk/uncovered-loss line;
9. one 行限 timing/action line with a clear action: file claim, appeal, renew, buy coverage, increase coverage, reduce rider, cancel after replacement, wait, avoid uncovered risk, prepare evidence, or escalate to regulator.

Do not say "good insurance luck" without naming premium, coverage, claim file, insurer decision, exclusion, and timing. Do not promise guaranteed payout. If evidence is weak, say "claim possible only with evidence cap" rather than inventing approval.
