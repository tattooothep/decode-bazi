# 七政四餘 · People, Network, And Collaboration Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Qizheng/Guolao. It condenses retained Guolao/Xingxue/Xingping/Qizheng source groups into operational rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack for questions about friends, peers, siblings, helpers, staff, subordinates, coworkers, clients, patrons, allies, business partners, bosses, elders, and social networks.

Do not treat these as romance unless the user explicitly asks about love/marriage. For non-romantic partnership, read the collaboration/counterparty axis and keep it separate from spouse prediction.

## People Chain

For every people/network question:

1. **三主 anchor**: 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主.
2. **People palace**: 兄弟 for siblings/peers/communication; 奴僕 or servant/helper field when supplied for staff/team; 官祿 for boss/workplace hierarchy; 妻妾 only for formal counterpart/contract partner; 福德 for ease/support.
3. **Palace lord and stars**: status, where the lord lands, 恩/用/仇/難 role, 廟旺/升殿/落陷.
4. **Support versus friction**: separate useful people from draining people.
5. **Timing**: 行限/限度主/洞微百六限 and target-year 木土 row when a date/year is asked.

## Peers / Siblings / Communication · 兄弟

Use 兄弟 for siblings, classmates, peers, messaging, daily coordination, and people at a similar level.

Strong 兄弟 with support from 恩/用 = peers help open doors or carry information. 兄弟 hit by 仇/難 or weak lord = talk, gossip, delay, or mixed loyalty must be managed.

## Helpers / Staff / Team

Use 奴僕/helper fields when the packet supplies them. If not supplied, use 兄弟 for peer team, 官祿 for workplace structure, and 福德 for support climate.

Separate:

- reliable helpers,
- paid staff/subordinates,
- coworkers,
- operational burden,
- people who consume time without output.

Do not call someone disloyal from one bad star. Require palace + lord/status + repeated friction.

## Clients / Allies / Business Counterpart

Use 妻妾 only as the formal counterparty/contract axis when the question says client, partner, supplier, or business deal. Do not convert this into marriage language.

Check 財帛/官祿 links to see whether the person brings money/work, and 兄弟/福德 to see whether coordination is smooth.

## Boss / Elders / Authority

Use 官祿 for boss, authority, office structure, and status. Use 福德 for patronage/ease and 父母-style elder/document fields only when packet terminology supplies them.

Good 官祿 with pressured 兄弟 = seniors help but peer environment is noisy. Weak 官祿 with strong 兄弟 = team helps but formal authority is unstable.
