## Conversion Rule

Visibility is not sales. Require a chain:

- 相貌/官祿 or 兄弟 = people can see or hear the message,
- 奴僕/福德 = audience support or community traction,
- 妻妾 = buyer/client/counterparty contact,
- 財帛/田宅 = money received and retained.

If visibility is strong but 財帛/田宅 is weak, say "views without clean conversion" and prescribe offer/pricing/accounting controls. If 財帛 is strong but 兄弟/相貌 is weak, say sales can happen through direct clients or referrals more than public content.

Use 恩/用 and 廟旺/升殿 as support; 仇/難, weak lord, repeated friction, or hard timing means test small, do not overspend, and improve funnel before scaling.
