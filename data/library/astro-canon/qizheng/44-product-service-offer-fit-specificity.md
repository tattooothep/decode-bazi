# Qizheng Product, Service, And Offer-Fit Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Qizheng/Guolao. It condenses retained Guolao/Xingxue/Xingping/Qizheng source groups into operational rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks what product to sell, what service to offer, what package/offer should be built, whether to sell physical product, digital product, advisory service, subscription, premium package, low-ticket entry, bundle, course, tool, or done-for-you service.

Do not treat offer fit as the same thing as industry fit, customer persona, marketing traffic, pricing, or technology build. Split offer promise, delivery burden, buyer trust, money model, asset/inventory/data base, message clarity, scale path, risk, and timing.

## Offer Chain

1. **Three lords**: 命主, 命度/度主, 身宮/身主 show whether the native should sell expertise, service, tool, productized method, advisory, craft, operational help, or asset-backed offer.
2. **官祿**: formal offer authority, professional result, product category, service credibility, and whether the offer should be expert-led or simple transactional.
3. **財帛 / 田宅**: cash model, margin, retained value, inventory, property/data/content asset, subscription base, and whether the offer creates stored value.
4. **兄弟**: explanation, sales page, script, course, documentation, chat handoff, spec, teaching, and whether the offer can be understood quickly.
5. **妻妾**: direct buyer trust, contract scope, one-to-one delivery, refund/refusal risk, and whether the offer needs consultation before payment.
6. **奴僕**: group delivery, membership, platform sale, staffing, vendors, community, and whether the offer can scale.
7. **福德**: comfort, taste, advisory/spiritual value, luxury, wellness, inner-life benefit, and customer satisfaction after purchase.
8. **疾厄 / 仇難**: support load, defect/complaint pattern, liability, hidden labor, burnout, refund risk, and offer types to avoid.
9. **行限 / 限度主 / 木土**: when to launch, productize, bundle, retire, narrow, or test the offer.

## Offer Type Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic star keywords:

- **官祿 + 兄弟 strong**: course, consulting, documentation-heavy service, professional report, audit, training, certification, expert-led package.
- **財帛 + 田宅 strong**: physical product, data/content library, subscription, property/home asset, durable package, stored-value product.
- **妻妾 strong**: premium one-to-one service, contract service, bespoke package, high-trust consultation, referral-only offer.
- **奴僕 strong**: group program, membership, marketplace/platform offer, community package, staff-assisted service.
- **福德 strong**: wellness, comfort, beauty/taste, advisory, spiritual, luxury, rest/recovery, inner-life product.
- **相貌 / 遷移 strong**: public-facing, online, foreign/non-local, visual, travel/outside-market, brand-led offer.
- **疾厄 / 仇難 stressed**: avoid unlimited support, vague custom work, emergency promise, underpriced operations, high-liability offer, or complex product without process.

## Verdict Rules

- If delivery capacity is stronger than scale capacity, sell a focused premium service before building a mass product.
- If 財帛/田宅 is stronger than 官祿, productize stored value instead of selling pure labor.
- If 兄弟 is weak, do not launch a complicated offer until explanation, copy, docs, or onboarding is fixed.
- If 妻妾 is weak, avoid high-trust bespoke packages; sell clearer fixed-scope offers.
- If 疾厄/仇難 repeats, reduce scope, cap revisions, add deposit, or avoid service-heavy products.
