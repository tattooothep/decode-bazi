# Qizheng Team Compensation, Incentive, And Performance Specificity Pack

Derived runtime rules for owner-side team pay, incentives, commission, KPI/OKR, and performance review. Use as private internal guidance only; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope Gate

Do not treat team compensation as the same thing as the user's own salary/job offer, customer pricing, affiliate partner commission, generic finance, or generic hiring. This pack is for how the operator should pay and manage staff/contractors: salary vs commission, bonus, quota, KPI/OKR, probation review, raise, pay cut, incentive abuse, and performance-based firing.

Trigger examples: employee salary, contractor rate, VA pay, sales commission, bonus plan, incentive plan, KPI, OKR, quota, performance review, probation review, payroll budget, raise staff, reduce pay, profit share for staff, commission abuse, underperformer, performance improvement plan.

## Evidence Chain

1. 三主 / 命度 / 度主 / 身主: owner fairness, enforcement capacity, whether the owner can hold standards without overpaying from guilt.
2. 官祿: role standard, performance authority, KPI/OKR structure, quota, review cadence, and what output must be measured.
3. 財帛: payroll budget, margin, fixed salary load, commission affordability, bonus pool, cash leakage, and ROI of labor.
4. 田宅: retained operating assets, tool/process investment, whether pay builds durable capacity or disappears as expense.
5. 兄弟: written pay plan, KPI definitions, review conversation, feedback wording, and commission rules.
6. 奴僕: staff/contractor reliability, incentive fit by role, team morale under pay policy, and whether commission creates healthy effort.
7. 妻妾: employment/contract promise, working agreement, compensation clause, probation terms, non-romantic commitment boundary.
8. 福德: fairness perception, loyalty, culture, trust, morale, and whether incentives improve or poison the team.
9. 疾厄 / 仇難: incentive gaming, bad hire overpaid, payroll liability, resentment, churn, dispute, fraud, and firing/documentation risk.
10. 行限 / 流年: timing for raise, bonus, new commission plan, review, PIP, pay cut, or termination.

## Decision Vocabulary

Final call must choose a concrete pay-management action: fixed salary / hourly contractor rate / commission-only / salary plus commission / bonus pool / KPI bonus / quota plan / profit share for staff / paid trial rate / probation review / performance improvement plan / raise / delay raise / reduce scope before pay cut / replace underperformer / fire with documentation.

## Answer Contract

Every team compensation answer must include:

- one 三主 owner-fairness/enforcement-capacity line
- one 官祿 KPI/role-standard/performance-authority line
- one 財帛 payroll-margin/commission-affordability line
- one 兄弟 written-pay-plan/review-conversation line
- one 奴僕 staff-incentive/reliability line
- one 妻妾 contract/probation/compensation-promise line
- one 福德 morale/fairness/loyalty line
- one 疾厄/仇難 incentive-abuse/dispute/liability line
- one 行限/流年 timing line
- one concrete action line using the decision vocabulary.
