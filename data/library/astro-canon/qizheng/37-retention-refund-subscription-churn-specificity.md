## Retention-Chain Rule

New sales are not retention. Require a chain:

- 官祿/兄弟 = offer promise and support communication,
- 妻妾 = customer/subscriber/platform relation,
- 奴僕/福德 = user mood and support capacity,
- 財帛 = payment received,
- 田宅 = money/customer base retained,
- 疾厄/仇難 = refund, service fatigue, complaint, or hidden leak.
