> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Western. It condenses classical, medieval, early-modern, public-domain modern bridge, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, or expose raw source text.

## Purpose

Use this pack for broad natal questions such as "ดูดวงรวม", "นิสัย", "ตัวตน", "ชีวิตเด่นเรื่องอะไร", "จุดแข็งจุดอ่อน", and "ควรเดินทางไหน". It prevents generic Sun-sign style answers.

Do not answer from Sun sign, one planet, one aspect, one lot, or one transit alone.

## Natal Reading Chain

Read in this order when birth time is reliable:

1. **Ascendant and chart ruler**: how life is steered and how the native acts.
2. **Sun, Moon, and sect**: visibility, vitality, mind/body rhythm, day/night weighting.
3. **MC/IC and angular houses**: public role, home/root, relationship axis, body/personality axis.
4. **Dominant planets and dispositors**: repeated drivers and whose agenda organizes the chart.
5. **Dignity, bounds, triplicity, face, and angularity**: whether the key planets can act directly.
6. **Major aspects, patterns, hidden contacts**: repeated support/friction; minor contacts only after main aspects.
7. **Lots supplied by packet**: Fortune for material ease, Spirit for intention/calling, Eros/Necessity for desire/duty.
8. **Timing**: transits/return cycles activate natal promise; they do not replace it.

If birth time is missing, close Asc/MC/houses/lots/sect/chart-ruler and answer from planets by sign, dignity, aspects, Moon condition, and element/modality only.
