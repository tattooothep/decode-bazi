# Western Horary, Electional, Mundane, Classical Guards

> Compact derived rules; scope guard, not quotation pack.

## Source Families

- Horary/electional: Lilly, Ramesey, Coley, Dariot, Gadbury, Partridge, Kirby/Bishop, Penseyre, Simmonite/Story, Sahl, Masha'allah.
- Mundane/national/weather: Abu Ma'shar conjunction/revolution witnesses, Haly medieval judgment witnesses, Lilly `Englands Propheticall Merline`, Partridge `Mene Tekel`, H. S. Green, Raphael, Goad, Sibly, Goclenius, Leovitius, Heydon, Harvey, comet-signification witnesses, and Planeten-Buch-style planetary prognostics.
- Classical/medieval natal: Ptolemy, Manilius, Valens, Firmicus, Hephaestio, CCAG manuscript fragments/context, Alcabitius, Haly, Leopold, Albubather, Hermes aphorisms, Pruckner compendium, Bonatti, Morin.
- Renaissance natural/geniture context: Giuntini/Junctinus, Cardano-linked aphoristic compilations, Argoli, Garcaeus, John Dee, Rantzau, Gaurico, Regiomontanus, Montulmo, Magini, Taisnier.
- Directions/rectification: Placidus, Partridge, Sepharial; use only when packet supplies computed direction fields.
- Practical synthesis: Zadkiel, Raphael, Parkes, Saint-Germain, Alan Leo, Sepharial, Pearce, Broughton, Robson, C. Aq. Libra.
- Star/esoteric/history: Robson for fixed-star delineation; R. H. Allen and Rosa Baughan for names/lore; Cumont for Greco-Roman historical context; Agrippa and Erra Pater for historical correspondences/folklore only.

No source names in normal user readings.

## Keep Branches Separate

- **Natal**: birth chart.
- **Pair/synastry**: two birth charts plus closed cross-chart packet.
- **Horary**: question chart for question time/place.
- **Electional**: candidate start-time chart.
- **Mundane**: country/event/ingress/lunation/eclipse/market-public chart.

Never answer horary/electional/mundane from natal data alone. If missing, answer from valid natal/transit/pair evidence and name the missing layer once.

## Horary Requirements

Use horary only with question time/place, houses, planets, significators, Moon condition, applications/separations, reception, and prohibition/collection/translation flags.

Priority: Asc ruler + Moon; matter-house ruler; applying aspect between significators; reception/dignity; Moon next aspect/void status if supplied; angularity; prohibition/collection/translation only if computed.

## Electional Requirements

Use electional only with candidate datetime/location, Asc/MC/houses, Moon phase/aspects, Asc ruler, action ruler, benefic/malefic angularity, applications/receptions, and natal tie-in when relevant.

Priority: strengthen Asc ruler, Moon, and action ruler; avoid malefic affliction to Asc/Moon/action ruler/final Moon aspect; prefer benefic reception; avoid void Moon or severe combustion when marked. Chinese datepick rules remain primary unless a Western electional packet exists.

## Mundane Requirements

Use mundane only with event/nation/ingress/lunation/eclipse packet. Anchors: angles; Sun/Moon; lunation/eclipse; Jupiter/Saturn policy/economy; Mars crisis; Mercury trade/media; Venus diplomacy/markets; 2nd/8th finance; 4th land/weather; 7th foreign/enemies; 10th government; 11th allies; 12th hidden/loss.

No political certainty, disaster certainty, or investment instruction from symbolic evidence alone.

Medieval conjunction/revolution, comet, and planetary-prognostic witnesses refine wording only when a computed mundane, annual, event, eclipse, comet, or revolution packet exists. For a private natal question, they are background provenance and must not become a personal fate claim.

## High-Risk Safety

- Medical/decumbiture needs a decumbiture or medical packet; natal alone gives only stamina/risk themes. Culpeper/Saunders medical witnesses are symbolic collation only. No diagnosis, treatment, herbs, bleeding, medication, surgery, fertility, death prediction, or replacement for clinicians.
- Weather/astrometeorology is historical symbolism only; never replace real weather/emergency guidance.
- Finance/speculation: no buy/sell/hold, price targets, portfolio sizing, or guaranteed timing.
- Locality/geodetic: no promise of success/danger from moving. Use only symbolic fit when locality/geodetic packet exists. Do not imitate modern proprietary astrocartography line readings.
- Esoteric/magic correspondences are historical context only; no harmful ritual or guaranteed result.

## Classical Technique Guard

Classical rules modify dignity, lots, houses, sect, reception, and planet condition. Use only with supporting packet fields.

- Lots/Parts require reliable time and correct day/night formula.
- Sect requires reliable day/night status.
- Time-lords, profections, revolutions, distributions, triplicity timing, primary directions, etc. require computed packet fields.
- Translate fatalistic language into practical risk/discipline/opportunity wording.
- Renaissance aphorisms, medieval conjunction/revolution witnesses, and natural-astrology texts can strengthen an already-supported classical judgment, but they cannot create unsupported medical, weather, finance, electional, mundane, or fatal predictions. Require the matching packet branch first.

## Anti-Generic Answer Rule

Every Western answer must name:

1. planet or point,
2. condition/aspect,
3. house/topic if reliable,
4. practical consequence.

Prefer packet anchors over textbook keywords. Missing specialty chart means “answer from valid scope only,” not “no answer.”
