# Western Pricing, Offer, Promotion, And Upsell Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Western. It condenses classical, medieval, early-modern, public-domain modern bridge, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, or expose raw source text.

## Purpose

Use this pack for price, package, tier, coupon, discount, promotion, bundle, upsell, cross-sell, add-on, top-up, credit pack, paid plan, raise price, lower price, intro offer, and "what offer should I sell" questions.

Do not answer from popularity, sales, or retention alone. Split price power, perceived value, customer willingness, delivery burden, cash margin, discount leakage, and timing.

## Core Chain

1. **Capacity and promise owner**: Ascendant/chart ruler when birth time is known; otherwise Sun/Moon/Mercury/Saturn condition and supplied aspects.
2. **Offer authority and positioning**: MC/10th, Sun, Jupiter, and strong dignity show product promise, premium position, and whether the offer looks credible.
3. **Price and owned cash**: 2nd house/ruler and Part of Fortune show received price, margin, and whether cash stays with the native.
4. **Customer willingness and terms**: 7th house/ruler shows buyer, subscriber, sponsor, platform, contract, checkout counterparty, and price acceptance.
5. **Audience and demand base**: 11th shows user base, warm audience, patrons, community support, and upsell pool.
6. **Offer message and checkout clarity**: 3rd/Mercury shows price explanation, coupon terms, sales page, help docs, billing copy, and checkout friction.
7. **Cost, leakage, and service burden**: 6th workload, 8th fees/refunds/shared liability, 12th hidden loss, and hard Saturn/Mars contacts show margin erosion or discount abuse.
8. **Timing**: supplied transits/return cycles activate the natal promise; do not invent exact dates.

## Pricing-Chain Rule

Price is not the same as demand. Require a chain:

- MC/10th = offer promise and authority,
- 2nd/Fortune = price/margin received,
- 7th = buyer/customer/platform accepts terms,
- 11th = user base and upsell pool,
- 3rd/Mercury = price explanation and coupon clarity,
- 6th/8th/12th = delivery cost, refund, discount leak, hidden loss.

If 10th/11th are strong but 2nd is weak, the offer has value but is underpriced. If 2nd is strong but 7th is pressured, the price can sell yet creates customer friction. If 8th/12th dominate, avoid broad coupons and protect margin.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close houses, angles, lots, and house-ruler claims unless packet supplies a reliable fallback. Use Sun/Moon/Mercury/Venus/Jupiter/Saturn condition, aspects, and timing only; avoid confident package or tier calls that depend on houses.

## Discount / Promotion Rule

For coupon, launch promo, and discount questions, name the pressure:

- **safe short promo**: 3rd/Mercury clear + 7th cooperative + 2nd still supported,
- **underpricing risk**: 10th/11th strong but 2nd weak,
- **discount abuse**: 2nd/8th/12th pressure,
- **unclear offer**: 3rd/Mercury afflicted,
- **premium room**: MC/10th/11th/7th supported with clean 2nd/Fortune.

Do not recommend broad discounting when leak markers are stronger than customer markers.
