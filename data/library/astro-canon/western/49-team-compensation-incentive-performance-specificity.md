# Western Team Compensation, Incentive, And Performance Specificity Pack

Derived runtime rules for owner-side team pay, incentives, commission, KPI/OKR, and performance review. Use as private internal guidance only; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope Gate

Do not treat team compensation as the same thing as the user's own salary/job offer, customer pricing, affiliate partner commission, generic finance, or generic hiring. This pack is for how the operator should pay and manage staff/contractors: salary vs commission, bonus, quota, KPI/OKR, probation review, raise, pay cut, incentive abuse, and performance-based firing.

Trigger examples: employee salary, contractor rate, VA pay, sales commission, bonus plan, incentive plan, KPI, OKR, quota, performance review, probation review, payroll budget, raise staff, reduce pay, profit share for staff, commission abuse, underperformer, performance improvement plan.

## Evidence Chain

1. Asc/chart ruler or no-time substitute: owner fairness, enforcement capacity, ability to hold standards, and whether the owner overpays from guilt or avoids hard reviews.
2. MC/10th/Sun/Saturn: role standard, performance authority, KPI/OKR, quota, review cadence, accountability, and manager discipline.
3. 2nd/Fortune/8th: payroll budget, margin, fixed salary load, commission affordability, bonus pool, debt/liability, and cash leakage.
4. 4th: retained operating assets, tools, systems, process investment, and whether pay builds durable capacity.
5. 3rd/Mercury/9th: written pay plan, KPI definitions, review conversation, feedback wording, policy, and commission rules.
6. 6th: staff/contractor labor, incentive fit by role, underperformer handling, service load, and whether pay matches daily output.
7. 7th: working agreement, compensation clause, manager/contractor relationship, probation promise, and accountability terms.
8. 11th/Moon/Venus/Jupiter: team morale, loyalty, fairness perception, culture, trust, and healthy group motivation.
9. 6th/8th/12th/Mars-Saturn: incentive gaming, overpaid bad hire, resentment, churn, fraud, liability, burnout, and firing/documentation risk.
10. Timing: transits/returns/refDate/timing fields must support raise, bonus, new commission plan, review, PIP, pay cut, or termination.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, do not pretend houses/MC are exact. Use Moon, Sun, visible planets, timed transits by date, and packet-provided no-time substitute. Say clearly that house-specific pay policy calls are lower confidence.

## Decision Vocabulary

Final call must choose a concrete pay-management action: fixed salary / hourly contractor rate / commission-only / salary plus commission / bonus pool / KPI bonus / quota plan / profit share for staff / paid trial rate / probation review / performance improvement plan / raise / delay raise / reduce scope before pay cut / replace underperformer / fire with documentation.
