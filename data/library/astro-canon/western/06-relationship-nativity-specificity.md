# Western Relationship And Nativity Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Western. It condenses classical, medieval, early-modern, public-domain modern-bridge, and licensed-internal relationship/nativity material into operational rules. It is not raw source text and must not be quoted, cited, or exposed to users.

## Purpose

Use for love, marriage, attraction, spouse, pair, and demo answers that need to stop sounding generic.

Every relationship verdict must name the concrete chain:

1. 7th house/ruler for commitment,
2. 5th house/ruler for romance/pleasure,
3. Venus, Mars, Moon, and Sun/spouse significator when supplied,
4. Eros and Necessity lots when supplied,
5. aspects/hidden contacts/dignity/sect,
6. pair contacts from `PAIR_INTERACTION_PACKET western`,
7. transits/return cycles when timing is asked.

## Single Chart

Separate the result:

- Attraction: Venus, Mars, 5th ruler, Eros, soft contacts to Moon/Sun/Asc.
- Commitment: 7th house/ruler, Saturn condition, Necessity, 2nd/4th/10th public-family support.
- Emotional fit: Moon, 4th, water emphasis, Moon aspects.
- Conflict style: Mars/Saturn/Rahu/Ketu hard contacts, 6th/8th/12th links, afflicted Venus/Moon/7th ruler.
- Timing: target-date transits to Venus, Moon, Sun, 5th/7th ruler, Asc/Desc, or lots.

Do not declare marriage, breakup, or "soulmate" from Venus alone, one lot alone, or one transit alone.

## Pair Mode

Use only `PAIR_INTERACTION_PACKET western`. Do not invent composite charts, extra cross aspects, house overlays, or fixed-star synastry.

Weight:

1. luminary and Venus/Mars contacts,
2. cross contacts to Asc/MC/Part of Fortune when both times exist,
3. 1st/5th/7th/8th/11th house overlays when both times exist,
4. Saturn/Mars/Rahu/Ketu hard contacts as pressure/duty,
5. antiscia/parallels as secondary hidden pull,
6. each person's own natal 7th/Venus/Moon condition.

Write pair verdict as four lines: attraction, stability, friction, timing. Then choose one final call: "หนุน", "ดึงแรงแต่หนัก", "คบได้ถ้าวางกติกา", or "ไม่ควรเร่งผูกมัด".

## No-Time

If birth time is missing, close Asc/MC/houses/lots/sect/chart-ruler/house overlays. Use planet signs, dignities, aspects, Venus/Mars/Sun/Moon, and conditional Moon only. Say it is a planet-sign/aspect reading, not a house/lot reading.

## Output Rule

If a sentence could fit many couples, rewrite it with owner name + planet/house/lot/aspect/dignity/timing field from the packet.
