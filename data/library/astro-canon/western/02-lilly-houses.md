## 0. หลักการใช้คัมภีร์ (Lilly's own framing)

Lilly ย้ำว่า รู้ดาว+ราศีแต่ไม่รู้เรือน = ไร้ประโยชน์ — *Book I, Chap. XV*:

> "the exact knowledge whereof is so requisite, that he who shall learne the nature of the Planets and Signes without exact judgment of the Houses, is like an improvident man, that furnisheth himselfe with variety of Householdstuffe, having no place wherein to bestow them."

> "There is nothing appertaining to the life of man in this world, which in one way or other hath not relation to one of the twelve Houses of Heaven."

**ลำดับการชั่งน้ำหนัก (จาก Chap. XVIII):** ดู (1) ธรรมชาติดาว+ราศี → (2) fortitude/debility (essential dignities + accidental) → (3) แง่มุมระหว่างดาว. *"by how much you endeavour to straine a judgment beyond nature, by so much the more you augment your Errour."*

## 1. ความหมายเรือนทั้ง 12 (The Twelve Houses)
*Book I, Chap. XV — "Of the twelve Houses, their Nature and signification"*

### เรือนที่ 1 (The first House) — ชีวิต/ร่างกาย/ตัวผู้ถาม
> "It hath signification of the life of man, of the stature, colour, complexion, forme and shape of him that propounds the Question, or is borne; in Eclipses and great Conjunctions, and upon the Sun his annuall ingresse into Aries, it signifieth the common people, or generall State of that Kingdome where the Figure is erected."
> "And as it is the first house, it represents the head and face of man... The house is Masculine."
> "The Consignificators of this house are Aries and Saturn... it is called the Ascendant, because when the Sun commeth to the cusp of this house, he ascends, or then ariseth."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 1 = ชีวิต รูปร่าง สีผิว หน้าตา บุคลิกของเจ้าชะตา/ผู้ถาม · ปกครองศีรษะ-ใบหน้า · เพศชาย (Masculine) · = Ascendant. ในดวงเมือง = ราษฎรทั่วไป.

### เรือนที่ 2 (The second House) — ทรัพย์/รายได้
> "From this house is required judgment concerning the estate or fortune of him that asks the Question, of his Wealth or Poverty, of all moveable Goods, Money lent, of Profit or gaine, losse or damage; in suits of Law, it signifies a mans Friends or Assistants; in private Duels, the Querents second."
> "It represents in man the neck, and hinder part of it towards the shoulders, of colours the green... It's a Feminine house and Succedant, called in some Latin Authors Anaphora."
> "It hath Consignificators Taurus and Jupiter."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 2 = เงินทอง ทรัพย์เคลื่อนที่ กำไร/ขาดทุน เงินให้ยืม · ในคดี = พวกพ้อง/ผู้ช่วย · ปกครองคอ · เพศหญิง · Succedant.

### เรือนที่ 3 (The third House) — พี่น้อง/เดินทางใกล้
> "Hath signification of Brethren, Sisters, Cozens or Kindred, Neighbours, small Journeys, or inland-Journeys, oft removing from one place to another, Epistles, Letters, Rumours, Messengers: It doth rule the Shoulders, Armes, Hands and Fingers."
> "It is a Cadent house, and is the joy of the Moon... The house is Masculine."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 3 = พี่น้อง ญาติ เพื่อนบ้าน เดินทางระยะใกล้ จดหมาย ข่าวลือ · ปกครองไหล่-แขน-มือ · Cadent · เป็น "joy" ของจันทร์ · เพศชาย.

### เรือนที่ 4 (The fourth House) — บิดา/ที่ดิน/บั้นปลาย
> "Giveth Judgment of Fathers in general, and ever of his Father that enquires, or that is borne; of Lands, Houses, Tenements, Inheritances, Tillage of the earth, Treasures hidden, the determination or end of any thing; Townes, Cities or Castles, besieged or not besieged; all ancient Dwellings, Gardens, Fields, Pastures, Orchards; the quality and nature of the grounds one purchaseth."
> "It's Consignificator is Cancer, and of Planets the Sun; we call it the Angle of the Earth, or Imum Celi; it is Feminine, and the North Angle: In Nativities or Questions, this fourth house represents Fathers, so doth the Sun by day and Saturn by night."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 4 = บิดา ที่ดิน บ้าน มรดก สมบัติฝังดิน "จุดจบของเรื่อง" · = Imum Coeli/มุมเหนือ · เพศหญิง · บิดา = อาทิตย์กลางวัน/เสาร์กลางคืน.

### เรือนที่ 5 (The fifth House) — บุตร/ความบันเทิง
> "By this house we judge of Children, of Embassadours, of the state of a Woman with child, of Banquets, of Ale-houses, Tavernes, Playes, Messengers or Agents for Republicks... of the health or sicknesse of his Son or Daughter that asks the Question."
> "It ruleth the Stomack, Liver, Heart, Sides and Back, and is masculine... it's Consignificators are Mercury and Venus, who doth joy in this house, in regard it's the house of Pleasure, Delight and Meriment; it's wholly unfortunate by Saturn or Mars."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 5 = บุตร หญิงตั้งครรภ์ งานเลี้ยง ความบันเทิง โรงละคร · ปกครองท้อง-ตับ-หัวใจ · เพศชาย · ศุกร์ "joy" (เรือนความสุข) · เสีย-เมื่อเสาร์/อังคาร.

### เรือนที่ 6 (The sixth House) — คนรับใช้/โรคภัย/สัตว์เล็ก
> "It concerneth Men and Maid-servants, Galli-slaves, Hogges, Sheep, Goats, Hares, Connies, all manner of lesser Cattle, and profit or losse got thereby; Sicknesse, its quality and cause, principal humor offending, curable or not curable, whether the disease be short or long; Day-Labourers, Tenants, Farmers, Shepherds, Hogheards, Neatherds, Warriners; and it signifieth Unkles, or the Fathers Brothers and Sisters."
> "It ruleth the inferiour part of the Belly, and intestines... this house is a Feminine and Cadent house, unfortunate, as having no aspect to the Ascendant... Mars rejoyceth in this house, but his Consignificator is of the Signes Virgo, of Planets Mercury."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 6 = คนรับใช้ สัตว์เล็ก โรคภัย (เหตุ/รักษาได้ไหม/สั้น-ยาว) ลูกจ้าง อา-ลุง · ปกครองท้องล่าง-ลำไส้ · เพศหญิง · Cadent · ไร้แง่มุมกับลัคนา = อัปมงคล.

### เรือนที่ 7 (The seventh House) — คู่ครอง/ศัตรู/คู่ความ
> "It giveth judgement of Marriage, and describes the person inquired after, whether it be Man or Woman, all manner of Love questions, our publique enemies; the Defendant in a Law-suit, in Warre the opposing party; all Quarrels, Duels, Law-suits; in Astrology the Artist himselfe; in Physicke the Physitian; Theeves and Thefts; the person stealing, whether Man or Woman, Wives, Sweethearts; their shape, description, condition... Fugitives or run-awayes; Banished and Out-lawed-men."
> "It hath consignificator Libra and Venus, Saturn or Mars unfortunate herein, shew ill in Marriage... It is called the Angle of the West: and is Masculine."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 7 = แต่งงาน คู่ครอง คนที่ถามถึง ศัตรูเปิดเผย จำเลย/คู่ความ ขโมย คนหนี · = มุมตะวันตก · เพศชาย · เสาร์/อังคารในนี้ = แต่งงานร้าย.

### เรือนที่ 8 (The eighth House) — ความตาย/มรดก/สินสอด
> "The Estate of Men deceased, Death, its quality and nature; the Wils, Legacies and Testaments of Men deceased; Dowry of the Wife, Portion of the Maid, whether much or little, easie to be obtained or with difficulty. In Duels it represents the Adversaries Second; in Lawsuits the Defendants friends. What kinde of Death a Man shall dye. it signifies feare and anguish of Minde: Who shall enjoy or be heire to the Deceased."
> "It rules the Privy-parts. Of colours, the Green and Black... and is a succedant House, and Feminine."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 8 = ความตาย (ลักษณะ/แบบไหน) พินัยกรรม มรดก สินสอดของภรรยา ความกลัว-ทุกข์ใจ ทายาท · ปกครองอวัยวะลับ · Succedant · เพศหญิง.

### เรือนที่ 9 (The ninth House) — เดินทางไกล/ศาสนา/ความรู้
> "By this House we give judgement of Voyages or long journies beyond Seas, of Religious men, or Clergy of any kinde, whether Bishops or inferiour Ministers; Dreames, Visions, forraigne Countries, of Books, Learning, Church Livings, or Benefices, Advowsions; of the kindred of ones Wife."
> "Of mans body it ruleth the Fundament, the Hippes and Thighes, Sun and Jupiter are consignificators of this House; for if Jupiter be herein placed, it naturally signifies a devout man in his Religion... the Sun rejoyceth to be in this House, which is Masculine, and Cadent."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 9 = เดินทางไกลข้ามทะเล นักบวช/พระ ความฝัน นิมิต ต่างแดน หนังสือ การเรียนรู้ ศาสนา · ปกครองสะโพก-ต้นขา · Cadent · เพศชาย · พฤหัสในนี้ = คนเคร่งศาสนา.

### เรือนที่ 10 (The tenth House) — เกียรติยศ/อาชีพ/มารดา
> "Commonly it personateth Kings, Princes, Dukes, Earles, Judges, prime Officers, Commanders in chiefe, whether in Armies or Townes; all sorts of Magistracy and Officers in Authority, Mothers, Honour, Preferment, Dignity, Office, Lawyers; the profession or Trade any one useth; it signifies Kingdomes, Empires, Dukedomes, Counties."
> "It's called the Medium Celi, or Mid-heaven, and is Feminine. Its consignificators are Capricorn and Mars: either Saturn or the Sun doe much Fortunate this House when they are posited therein, Saturn or Mars usually deny honour."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 10 = เกียรติยศ ตำแหน่ง อาชีพ/อาชีพการงาน มารดา ผู้มีอำนาจ กษัตริย์ ผู้พิพากษา · = Mid-heaven/Medium Coeli · เพศหญิง · อาทิตย์/เสาร์ในนี้ = หนุนเกียรติ.

### เรือนที่ 11 (The eleventh House) — มิตร/ความหวัง
> "It doth naturally represent Friends and Friendship, Hope, Trust, Confidence, the Praise, or Dispraise of any one; the fidelity or falsenesse of Friends; as to Kings it personates their Favourites, Counsellours, Servants, their Associates or Allyes, their Money, Exchequer or Treasure; in Warre their Amunition and Souldiery; it represents Courtiers."
> "Of members it ruleth the Legs to the Ancles... Jupiter doth especially rejoyce in this House; its a succedant House, and masculine, and in vertue is equivalent either to the seventh or fourth Houses."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 11 = มิตรสหาย ความหวัง ความเชื่อใจ คำสรรเสริญ/นินทา · ของกษัตริย์ = ที่ปรึกษา/พันธมิตร/คลัง · ปกครองขาถึงข้อเท้า · Succedant · เพศชาย · พฤหัส "joy".

### เรือนที่ 12 (The twelfth House) — ศัตรูลับ/ทุกข์/คุก
> "It hath signification of private Enemies, of Witches, great Cattle, as Horses, Oxen, Elephants, &c. Sorrow, Tribulation, Imprisonments, all manner of affliction, self-undoing, &c. and of such men as maliciously undermine their neighbours, or informe secretly against them."
> "It hath consignificators Pisces and Venus, Saturne doth much joy in that House, for naturally Saturne is author of mischiefe; and it ruleth in Mans body the Feete... Its a Cadent House, Feminine, and vulgarly sometimes called Cataphora."

[สรุปไทย] เรือน 12 = ศัตรูลับ แม่มด/คุณไสย สัตว์ใหญ่ ความเศร้า ทุกข์ คุก การทำลายตัวเอง คนแอบปองร้าย · ปกครองเท้า · Cadent · เพศหญิง · เสาร์ "joy" (ดาวแห่งภัย).

> **Lilly ปิดท้ายเรือน:** "This is the true Caracter of the severall Houses, according to the Ptolomeian Doctrine, and the experience my selfe have had for some yeers: I must confesse the Arabians have made severall other divisions of the Houses, but I could never in my practise finde any verity in them."

## 2. Essential Dignities (เกียรติยศประจำตัวของดาว + ระบบให้แต้ม)
*Book I, Chap. XVIII — "Of the Essentiall Dignities of the Planets"*

Lilly: ดาว "strong" จริงเมื่อมี essential dignity คือ อยู่ใน **House · Exaltation · Triplicity · Term · Face** ขณะตั้งดวง — แต่ละชั้นให้แต้มต่างกัน:

> "A Planet is then said to be really strong when he hath many Essentiall dignities, which are knowne, by his being either in his House, Exaltation, Triplicity, Terme or Face, at time of erecting the Figure."

### ระบบให้แต้ม (verbatim · จำนวน dignities)
- **House = 5 dignities.** *"if you find a Planet in any of those Signes we call his house or houses, he is then essentially strong, and we allow for that five dignities; as Saturn in Aquarius, Jupiter in Sagittarius."* — *"it represents a man in such a condition, as that he is Lord of his owne house, estate and fortune."*
- **Exaltation = 4 dignities.** *"If he be in that Signe wherein he is exalted, you may allow him four dignities essentiall, whether he be neer the very degree of his exaltation, yea or not."* — *"it presents a person of haughty condition, arrogant, assuming more unto him then his due."*
- **Triplicity = 3 dignities.** *"If he be in any of those Signes which are alotted him for his Triplicity, he hath allowed him three dignities."* — *"A Planet in his triplicity, shewes a man modestly indued with the Goods and Fortune of this world, one prettily descended."* (⚠️ ต้องดู day/night ก่อน — ดู Triplicity rulers ข้างล่าง)
- **Term = 2 dignities.** *"If any Planet be in those degrees we assigne for his Termes, we allow him two dignities."* — *"rather shewes a man more of the corporature and temper of the Planet, then any extraordinary abundance in fortune."*
- **Face/Decanate = 1 dignity.** *"If any Planet be in his Decanate, Decurie or Face... he is then allowed one essentiall dignity; for being in his owne Decanate or Face, cannot then be called peregrine."* — *"is almost like a man ready to be turned out of doores."*

> **Peregrine:** ดาวที่ไม่มี dignity เลยในตำแหน่งนั้น (ไม่อยู่ใน house/exalt/triplicity/term/face ของตน) = peregrine = อ่อนสุด.

### Triplicity rulers (ผู้ครองตรีโกณ กลางวัน/กลางคืน · verbatim "Use of the Table")
> "These twelve Signes are divided into four Triplicities... As over against Leo, Sagittarius, Aries you find Sun, Jupiter, viz. Sun governeth by day in that Triplicity, and Jupiter by night: Over against Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn, you find Venus and Moon; viz. that Venus hath domination by day, and Moon by night in that Triplicity: Over against Libra, Aquarius, Gemini you find Saturn, Mercury, which rule as Lords... Over against Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces, you find Mars, who, according to Ptolomy and Naibod, ruleth onely that Triplicity both night and day."

| Triplicity | ราศี | กลางวัน | กลางคืน |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fiery | Aries · Leo · Sagittarius | Sun ☉ | Jupiter ♃ |
| Earthly | Taurus · Virgo · Capricorn | Venus ♀ | Moon ☽ |
| Airy | Gemini · Libra · Aquarius | Saturn ♄ | Mercury ☿ |
| Watry | Cancer · Scorpio · Pisces | Mars ♂ (ทั้งวัน-คืน) | Mars ♂ |

|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ♄ Saturn | Aquarius | Capricorn | Libra | (Cancer/Leo) | Aries | Airy by day |
| ♃ Jupiter | Sagittarius | Pisces | Cancer | Gemini & Virgo | Capricorn | Fiery by night |
| ♂ Mars | Aries | Scorpio | 28° Capricorn | Libra & Taurus | 28° Cancer | Watry (ทั้งวัน-คืน) |
| ☉ Sun | Leo | — | 19° Aries | Aquarius | Libra | Fiery by day |
| ♀ Venus | Libra | Taurus | 27° Pisces | Aries & Scorpio | 27° Virgo | Earthly by day |
| ☿ Mercury | Gemini | Virgo | 15° Virgo | Sagittarius & Pisces | Pisces | Airy by night |
| ☽ Moon | Cancer | — | 3° Taurus | Capricorn | 3° Scorpio | Earthly by night |

verbatim ยืนยันบางค่า: Saturn *"Capricorne his Night-house, Aquarius his Day-house; he is Exaltated in Libra, he receives his Fall in Aries, he rejoyceth in the Signe Aquarius"* · Mars *"He hath Aries for his Day-house, and Scorpio for his Night-house; he is exalted in 28 degr. of Capricorn, and is depressed in 28 Cancer; he receiveth detriment in Libra and Taurus"* · Sun *"He hath onely the Signe of Leo for his House, and Aquarius for his Detriment... He is Exalted in the 19 degree of Aries, and receives his Fall in [Libra]"* · Venus *"She hath Taurus and Libra for her houses, she is exalted in 27 Pisces, she receiveth detriment in Aries and Scorpio, and hath her fall in 27 Virgo"* · Mercury *"He hath Gemini and Virgo for his Houses, and is exalted in the 15 of Virgo: he receives detriment in Sagittarius and Pisces, his fall is in Pisces"* · Moon *"She hath the Signe Cancer for her house, and Capricorn for her detriment; she is exalted in 3 Taurus, and hath her fall in 3 grad. Scorpio."*

> **Terms & Faces (degrees):** Lilly ให้ตารางองศาเต็มของ Term/Face รายราศี (ตาม Ptolemy) — *"the Termes of Mercury... from six to fourteen, the Termes of Venus"* ฯลฯ — แต่ตัวเลของศาในตารางรวม (Chap. ## 3. นัยของดาวเคราะห์ 7 ดวง (Significations of the Planets)
*Book I, Chap. VII–XIII*

### ♄ Saturn — *Chap. VII*
> **Names:** "He is called usually Saturne, but in some Authors Chronos, Phenon, Falcifer."
> **Nature:** "He is a Diurnall Planet, Cold and Dry (being farre removed from the heat of the Sun) and moyst Vapours, Melancholick, Earthly, Masculine, the greater Infortune, author of Solitarinesse, Malevolent, &c."
> **Well dignified:** "Then he is profound in Imagination, in his Acts severe, in words reserved, in speaking and giving very spare, in labour patient, in arguing or disputing grave, in obtaining the goods of this life studious and solicitous; in all manner of actions austere."
> **Ill dignified:** "Then he is envious, covetous, jealous and mistrustfull, timorous, sordid, outwardly dissembling, suspitious, stubborne, a contemner of women, a close lyar, malicious, murmuring, never contented, ever repining."
> **Quality of men:** "In generall he signifieth Husbandmen, Clownes, Beggars, Day-labourers, Old men, Fathers, Grandfathers, Monks, Jesuits, Sectarists."
> **Orbe & yeers:** "His Orbe is nine degrees before and after." — "The greatest yeers he signifies 465. His greater 57. His meane yeers 43 and a half. His least 30."

[สรุปไทย] เสาร์ = เย็น+แห้ง, melancholick, ดาวร้ายใหญ่ (greater Infortune), ความโดดเดี่ยว · ดี = ลึกซึ้ง สุขุม อดทน เคร่งครัด · ร้าย = อิจฉา ตระหนี่ ขี้ระแวง โกหก · orbe 9° · อายุ 30–57 (max 465 ปี ดวงเมือง/ตระกูล).

### ♃ Jupiter — *Chap. VIII*
> **Names:** "you shall sometimes finde him called Zeus, or Phaeton."
> **Nature:** "He is a Diurnall, Masculine Planet, Temperately Hot and Moyst, Aiery, Sanguine, the greater Fortune, author of Temperance, Modesty, Sobriety, Justice."
> **Well dignified:** "Then is he Magnanimous, Faithfull, Bashfull, Aspiring in an honourable way at high matters, in all his actions a Lover of faire Dealing, desiring to benefit all men, doing Glorious things, Honourable and Religious, of sweet and affable Conversation, wonderfully indulgent to his Wife and Children, reverencing Aged men, a great Reliever of the Poore, full of Charity and Godlinesse, Liberall, hating all Sordid actions, Just, Wise, Prudent, Thankful, Vertuous."
> **Quality of men:** "He signifies Judges, Senators... Bishops, Priests, Ministers... of the Civill Law, young Scholars in a University or Colledge, Lawyers."

[สรุปไทย] พฤหัส = อบอุ่น+ชื้น สมดุล, sanguine, ดาวดีใหญ่ (greater Fortune), ผู้ให้ความยุติธรรม-ความพอประมาณ · ดี = ใจกว้าง ซื่อสัตย์ ศรัทธา เมตตา ยุติธรรม · คน = ผู้พิพากษา นักบวช นักกฎหมาย นักเรียน. (manners "when ill" ของพฤหัสถูก OCR ทำลาย → Gaps)

### ♂ Mars — *Chap. IX*
> **Names:** "the Ancients sometimes called Mavors, Aris, Pyrois, Gradivus."
> **Nature:** "...cholerick and fiery, the lesser Infortune, author of Quarrels, Strifes, Contentions."
> **Well dignified:** "In feats of Warre and Courage invincible, scorning any should exceed him, subject to no Reason; Bold, Confident, Immoveable, Contentious, challenging all Honour to themselves, Valiant, lovers of Warre and things pertaining thereunto, hazarding himselfe to all Perils, willingly will obey no body, or submit to any; a large Reporter of his owne Acts."
> **Ill dignified:** "Then he is a Prattler without modesty or honesty, a lover of Slaughter and Quarrels, Murder, Theevery, a promoter of Sedition, Frayes and Commotions, an Highway-Theefe, as wavering as the Wind, a Traytor, of turbulent Spirit, Perjured, Obscene, Rash, Inhumane, neither fearing God or caring for man, Unthankfull, Trecherous, Oppressors, Ravenous, Cheaters, Furious, Violent."

[สรุปไทย] อังคาร = ร้อน+ไฟ, cholerick, ดาวร้ายเล็ก (lesser Infortune), การวิวาท · ดี = กล้าหาญ ไม่ยอมใคร นักรบ มั่นคง · ร้าย = ฆ่า ปล้น กบฏ ทรยศ โกหก โหดเหี้ยม.

### ☉ Sun — *Chap. X*
> **Names:** "Sol, Titan, Ilios, Phebus, Apollo, Pean, Osyris, Diespiter."
> **Nature:** "He is naturally Hot, Dry; but more temperate then Mars is, a Masculine, Diurnall Planet, Equivalent, if well dignified, to a Fortune."
> **Well dignified:** "Very faithfull, keeping their Promises with all punctuality, a kind of itching desire to Rule and Sway where he comes: Prudent, and of incomparable Judgment; of great Majesty and Statelinesse, Industrious to acquire Honour and a large Patrimony, yet as willingly departing therewith againe; the Solar man usually speaks with gravity, but not many words... full of Thought, Secret, Trusty... Affable, Tractable, and very humane to all people, one loving Sumptuousnesse and Magnificence, and whatsoever is honorable; no sordid thoughts can enter his heart."
> **Ill dignified:** "Then the Solar man is Arrogant and Proud, disdaining all men, cracking of his Pedegree, he is Pur-blind in Sight and Judgment, restlesse, troublesome, domineering, a meer vapour, expensive, foolish... a Spend-thrift, wasting his Patrimony... yet thinks all men are bound to him, because a Gentleman borne."
> "The Sun is alwayes direct, and never can be said to be Retrograde." — "no degrees of the twelve Signs admitted him for his Termes."

[สรุปไทย] อาทิตย์ = ร้อน+แห้ง (อ่อนกว่าอังคาร), เทียบเท่าดาวดีเมื่อ well dignified · ดี = ซื่อสัตย์ สง่างาม ใจกว้าง รักเกียรติ · ร้าย = หยิ่ง อวดดี ผลาญทรัพย์ · ไม่ถอยหลังเลย · ไม่มี Term.

### ♀ Venus — *Chap. XI*
> **Names:** "called Cytherea, Aphrodite, Phosphoros, Hesperus, Ericina."
> **Nature:** "She is a Feminine Planet, temperately Cold and Moyst, Nocturnall, the lesser Fortune, author of Mirth and Jolity."
> **Well dignified:** "She signifies a quiet man, not given to Law, Quarrel or Wrangling; not Vicious, Pleasant, Neat and Spruce, loving Mirth in his words and actions, cleanly in Apparel, rather Drinking much then Gluttonous, prone to Venery, oft entangled in Love-matters, Zealous in their affections, Musicall, delighting in Baths, and all honest merry Meetings, or Maskes and Stage-playes, easie of Beliefe, and not given to Labour."
> **Ill dignified:** "When he is Riotous, Expensive, wholly given to Loosenesse and Lewd companies of Women, nothing regarding his Reputation, coveting unlawful Beds, Incestuous, an Adulterer, Fantastical, a meer Skip-jack, of no Faith, no Repute, no Credit; spending his Meanes in Ale-houses, Taverns, and amongst Scandalous, Loose people; a meer Lazy companion... a meer Atheist and a natural man."
> **Quality of men:** "Musitions, Gamesters, Silk-men, Mercers, Linnen-Drapers, Painters, Jewellers, Players, Lapidaries, Embroiderers, Women-tailors, Wives, Mothers, Virgins, Choristers, Fidlers, Pipers... Perfumers, Sempsters, Picture-drawers, Gravers, Upholsters, Limners, Glovers."

[สรุปไทย] ศุกร์ = เย็น+ชื้นพอเหมาะ, กลางคืน, ดาวดีเล็ก (lesser Fortune), ความรื่นเริง · ดี = สงบ รักสนุก ดนตรี ความรัก สะอาด · ร้าย = สำมะเลเทเมา เจ้าชู้ ไม่มีศรัทธา · คน = นักดนตรี ช่างฝีมือ ช่างวาด.

### ☿ Mercury — *Chap. XII*
> **Names:** "called Hermes, Stilbon, Cyllenius, Archas."
> **Nature:** "We may not call him either Masculine or Feminine, for he is either the one or other as joyned to any Planet... but of his owne nature he is cold and dry, and therefore Melancholly; with the good he is good, with the evill Planets ill... he is author of subtilty, tricks and devices, perjury, &c."
> **Well dignified:** "he represents a man of a subtill and politick braine, intellect, and cogitation; an excellent disputant or Logician, arguing with learning and discretion, and using much eloquence in his speech, a searcher into all kinds of Mysteries, Learning, sharpe and witty, learning almost any thing without a Teacher; ambitious of being exquisite in every Science, desirous naturally of travell and seeing foraign parts; a man of an unwearied fancie, curious in the search of any occult knowledge, able by his owne Genius to produce wonders; given to Divination and the more secret knowledge."
> **Ill dignified:** "A troublesome wit, a kinde of Phrenetick man, his tongue and Pen against every man, wholly bent to foole his estate and time in prating and trying nice conclusions to no purpose; a great lyar, boaster, prattler, busibody, false, a tale-carrier, given to wicked Arts, as Necromancy, and such like ungodly knowledge."

[สรุปไทย] พุธ = เย็น+แห้ง, เพศแปรตามดาวที่ร่วม, ดาวแห่งไหวพริบ/อุบาย · ดี = ฉลาด นักโต้วาที วาทศิลป์ เรียนรู้เร็ว นักค้นคว้าความลับ · ร้าย = โกหก คุยโม้ ยุแยง เวทมนตร์ชั่ว · พุธ "with the good he is good, with the evill ill" (แปรตามคู่).

### ☽ Moon — *Chap. XIII*
> **Names:** "called by the Ancients Lucina, Cynthia, Diana, Phebe, Latona, Noctiluca, Proserpina; she is the neerest to the Earth of all the Planets."
> **Nature:** "She is a Feminine, Nocturnall Planet, Cold, Moyst and Flegmatique."
> **Well dignified:** "She signifieth one of composed Manners, a soft, tender creature, a Lover of all honest and ingenuous Sciences, a Searcher of and Delighter in Novelties, naturally propense to flit and shift his Habitation, unstedfast, wholly caring for the present Times, Timerous, Prodigal, and easily Frighted, however loving Peace, and to live free from the cares of this Life."
> **Ill dignified:** "A meer Vagabond, idle Person, hating Labour, a Drunkard, a Sot, one of no Spirit or Forecast, delighting to live beggarly and carelesly, one content in no condition of Life, either good or ill."
> **Quality of men:** "She signifieth Queens, Countesses, Ladies, all manner of Women; as also, the common People, Travellers, Pilgrims, Sailors, Fishermen, Fish-mongers, Brewers, Tapsters, Vintners... Marriners, Millers, Ale-wives, Malsters... Midwives, Nurses." — "She is never Retrograde, but alwayes direct." — "The Sun and she hath no Termes assigned them."

[สรุปไทย] จันทร์ = เย็น+ชื้น, phlegmatic, กลางคืน, ใกล้โลกสุด · ดี = อ่อนโยน ชอบของใหม่ ย้ายที่บ่อย รักสงบ ขี้กลัว · ร้าย = เร่ร่อน เกียจคร้าน ขี้เมา · คน = หญิง ราชินี นักเดินทาง ชาวเรือ พยาบาล · ไม่ถอยหลังเลย · ไม่มี Term.

## 4. ความหมายของแง่มุม (Aspects)
*Book I, Chap. XIX — "Of severall Termes, Aspects, words of Art"*

> "The most forcible or strongest Rayes, Configurations or Aspects, are onely these... the Sextil, Quadrate, Trine, Opposition; we use to call the Conjunction an Aspect, but very improperly."

- **Sextile (60°):** *"the distance of one Planet from another by the sixt part of the Zodiack."*
- **Quadrate / Square (90°):** *"the distance of two Points, or two Planets by a fourth part of the Circle."* — *"the Quadrate Aspect is a signe of imperfect enmity."*
- **Trine (120°):** *"consists of 120 degrees, or by a third part of the Circle."*
- **Opposition (180°):** *"when two Planets are equally distant 180 degrees, or halfe the Circle from each other."* — *"the Opposition is an aspect or argument of perfect hatred."*
- **Conjunction (0°):** *"when two Planets are in one and the same degree and minute of a Signe."* — *"Conjunctions are good or bad, as the Planets in conjunction are friends or enemies to one another."*

> **ความหมายเชิงตีความ (verbatim):**
> "The Sextil and Trine aspects are arguments of Love, Unity and Friendship; but the Trine is more forcible... no doubt but peace may be easily concluded."
> Quadrate = *"imperfect enmity... the matter is not yet so farre gone, but there may be hopes of reconciliation."*
> Opposition = *"perfect hatred... it's then in nature impossible to expect a peace betwixt them till the suit is ended."*

### Partile / Platick + Orbs
> **Partile:** *"when two Planets are exactly so many degrees from each other as make a perfect aspect... this is a strong signe or argument for performance of any thing."*
> **Platick:** *"that which admits of the Orbs or Rayes of two Planets that signifie any matter."* — แต่ละดาวมี "orbe/moiety" รัศมีอิทธิพล (เช่น Saturn orbe 9°, Jupiter 7° ฯลฯ ดูราย-ดาว); aspect เกิดเมื่อระยะอยู่ใน moiety รวมของทั้งสองดาว.

[สรุปไทย] แง่มุม: Trine/Sextile = รัก สามัคคี เป็นมิตร (Trine แรงกว่า) · Square = เป็นปฏิปักษ์ไม่สมบูรณ์ (ยังคืนดีได้) · Opposition = เกลียดสมบูรณ์ (คืนดียาก) · Conjunction = ดี/ร้ายขึ้นกับว่าสองดาวเป็นมิตรหรือศัตรู · Partile (องศาตรงเป๊ะ) = แรง/ใกล้สำเร็จ · Platick (อยู่ในรัศมี orbe) = อ่อนกว่า.
