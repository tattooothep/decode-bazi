# Western Career, Money, And Health Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Western. It condenses classical, medieval, early-modern, public-domain modern bridge, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, or expose raw source text.

## Purpose

Use this pack for career, income, business, health/routine, year-ahead, or combined "งาน เงิน สุขภาพ" questions. It exists to keep Western readings chart-specific and prevent broad horoscope language.

Do not answer from Sun sign, one transit, one lot, or one house alone.

## Career Chain

Read career in this order:

1. **MC / 10th house / 10th ruler** when birth time is reliable.
2. **Chart ruler and dominant planets**: how the person acts and what drives the life.
3. **6th house/ruler**: daily work, service, employees, workload, health cost of work.
4. **2nd and 11th**: income from work and gains/network.
5. **Sun/Saturn/Mercury/Jupiter/Venus/Mars** according to the actual topic chain.
6. **Lot of Spirit and Victory** if supplied: calling, chosen action, public win.
7. **Dignity, bounds, triplicity, face, sect, angularity**: whether the career significator can act directly or needs support.
8. **Timing**: target-year transits and return cycles activate the natal promise.

Career verdict must name the work channel: management, technical/execution, analysis, commerce, communication, art/beauty, finance/assets, teaching/law, property, public leadership, service, hidden/backstage work, or independent disruption.

## Money Chain

Separate money into lanes:

1. **2nd house/ruler**: earned money, cashflow, assets held by the person.
2. **11th house/ruler**: gains, patrons, customers, audience, network.
3. **8th house/ruler**: debt, tax, shared money, investor/partner money, obligation.
4. **Part of Fortune and Fortune ruler**: material ease, livelihood channel, bodily fortune.
5. **Venus/Jupiter/Mercury/Saturn**: value, opportunity, commerce, structure/scarcity.
6. **Nemesis/Necessity** if supplied: cost, constraint, unavoidable duty.

Do not call wealth from Jupiter/Fortune alone. If 2nd is weak but 11th/Fortune is strong, say money comes through network/opportunity but retention needs structure. If 2nd is strong but 8th is pressured, separate personal earning from debt/shared-money stress.

## Health And Routine Chain

Use symbolic caution only:

1. **1st house/chart ruler**: vitality and body management.
2. **6th house/ruler**: illness tendency, workload, habits, employees, service pressure.
3. **12th house/ruler**: sleep, isolation, retreat, hidden drain, hospital/rest themes.
4. **Moon, Mars, Saturn**: body rhythm, heat/inflammation symbol, chronic pressure/boundaries.
5. **Nemesis/Necessity** if supplied: cost or unavoidable discipline.
6. **Timing**: transits to chart ruler, Moon, 1st/6th/12th rulers, MC/10th when work causes health strain.

Allowed: stamina, stress, sleep, routine, check-up, workload limits, recovery discipline, risk management.

Forbidden: diagnosis, disease name, death prediction, medication, surgery instruction, or replacing clinicians.

## Combined Work Money Health Question

Answer in three mini-verdicts:

1. **งาน**: MC/10th + 10th ruler + chart ruler/dominant planet + timing.
2. **เงิน**: 2nd/11th/8th + Fortune/lots + dignity/sect + timing.
3. **สุขภาพ**: 1st/6th/12th + Moon/Mars/Saturn + timing.

Then add one fused conclusion:

- "work drives income",
- "network brings money but retention is the task",
- "debt/shared money is the pressure point",
- "health/routine limits career speed",
- "consolidate before expanding",
- or another packet-backed phrase.

The fused conclusion must not erase the lanes. If career is strong and health is stressed, say both.

## No-Time Mode

If `hasBirthTime=false`:

- Close MC, Ascendant, houses, house rulers, lots, sect, chart ruler, angle contacts, and house overlays.
- Use planets by sign, dignity, retrograde/combustion, aspects, element/modality balance, and Moon condition if reliable.
- Money/career/health answers become planet-sign/aspect readings, not house/lots readings.

## Timing Discipline

For a target year:

1. Use natal promise first.
2. Use `transits.refDate` for the target year.
3. Name direct transit contacts to natal topic factors if supplied.
4. Use Jupiter/Saturn return-cycle support as background, not as the only verdict.
5. Do not invent progressions, solar returns, annual profections, or directions unless packet fields exist.

## Output Contract

Every answer using this pack must include:

- one MC/10th or no-time substitute line for career,
- one 2nd/11th/8th/Fortune line for money,
- one 1st/6th/12th/Moon-Mars-Saturn line for health/routine,
- one dignity/sect/lots/aspect condition,
- one timing line if the question names a year,
- one support-versus-friction split,
- one decisive practical final call.
