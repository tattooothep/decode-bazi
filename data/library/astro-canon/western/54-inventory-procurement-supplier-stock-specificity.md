# Western Inventory, Procurement, Supplier, And Stock Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about inventory, stock, stockout, overstock, procurement, purchase order, supplier, vendor lead time, MOQ, warehouse, import stock, safety stock, reorder point, raw material, or whether to buy/hold/clear goods.

Do not treat inventory/procurement as the same thing as cashflow/runway, unit economics, pricing, marketing traffic, customer support, sales-team QA, partner due diligence, or generic business. If the user asks only "cash left / burn / reserve", use the cashflow pack. If the user asks "profit per unit / margin / break-even", use the unit-economics pack. This pack is for physical or operational stock decisions and supplier/warehouse flow.

## Canon Routing

Western inventory judgment must join the 4th/IC, 2nd/Fortune, MC/10th, 3rd/Mercury, 7th, 6th, 9th/12th, and 8th/12th/Saturn-Mars. Stock can look like wealth in the 2nd but become a burden through the 4th, 6th, or 12th.

- Asc/chart-ruler or no-time substitute: whether the owner can keep stock discipline and avoid emotional buying.
- 4th/IC: warehouse, stored goods, inventory base, raw material, fixed stock, and goods trapped in place.
- 2nd/Fortune: capital tied in goods, stock value, purchase affordability, and cash recovered after sale.
- MC/10th: operating system, purchasing policy, inventory process, and stock-to-sales rhythm.
- 3rd/Mercury: purchase order, SKU records, vendor communication, stock record, and reorder message.
- 7th: supplier/vendor/customer terms, consignment, preorder, deposit, and delivery promise.
- 6th: warehouse staff, packer, shipping handler, vendor execution, tool, system, and inventory count.
- 9th/12th: import stock, foreign supplier, long-distance logistics, customs, transit goods, and delivery route.
- 8th/12th/Saturn-Mars: stockout, overstock, spoilage, damage, theft, wrong SKU, customs delay, refund, and dead stock.
- Packet-supplied timing: when to buy, pause, clear, switch supplier, or reduce stock depth.

## Answer Contract

For inventory/procurement questions, answer with:

1. one Asc/chart-ruler or no-time substitute stock-discipline line;
2. one 4th/IC warehouse/inventory-base line;
3. one 2nd/Fortune capital-tied-in-stock line;
4. one MC/10th purchasing/process line;
5. one 3rd/Mercury purchase-order/record/reorder-message line;
6. one 7th supplier/customer-term line;
7. one 6th warehouse/vendor-execution line;
8. one 9th/12th import/logistics/transit line when relevant;
9. one 8th/12th/Saturn-Mars stockout/overstock/damage/dead-stock-risk line;
10. one timing and action verdict: buy now, hold, reduce SKU, clear old stock, preorder only, change supplier, or set safety stock/reorder point.

Do not translate "stock value" into profit unless the 6th, 8th, and 12th risks are named. Do not advise bulk buying without checking storage, lead time, and dead-stock risk.
