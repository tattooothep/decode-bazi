# Western Travel, Foreign Field, Relocation, And Outside Opportunity Specificity Pack

Derived operational summary for travel, foreign/distant field, relocation, outside clients, public exposure, and leaving the home base in Western astrology. Use only as internal interpretation rules; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope

Use this pack when the question asks about travel, commute, moving city, moving country, relocation, migration, foreign opportunity, overseas clients, online/outside audience, publishing to a distant market, work away from home, travel risk, or whether to go/stay/delay.

If the question is buying/selling/renting a residence, land, condo, vehicle, mortgage, title deed, or lease, use the property/vehicle pack first. If it asks visa/permit/legal approval, also use authority/document. If it explicitly asks astrocartography or relocation chart lines, use specialty/locality guard and do not invent line maps.

## Evidence Chain

1. **Asc/chart ruler or no-time substitute**: baseline capacity for disruption. With no birth time, use Sun, Moon, Mercury, Jupiter, Saturn/Mars stress, visible aspects, and timing; do not invent houses.
2. **3rd house / Mercury / Moon**: short trips, commute, local movement, documents, communications, neighbors/siblings, travel logistics.
3. **9th house / Jupiter / Sun**: long-distance travel, foreign country, higher study, law, publishing, teachers, visas/institutions when legal/academic.
4. **12th house**: foreign residence, isolation, retreat, exile feeling, hospitals/rest, hidden cost, loss from being away.
5. **4th / IC**: old/new base, home comfort, family root, whether relocation destabilizes the person.
6. **10th/MC and 11th**: work-driven relocation, public status outside home, foreign clients, audience, network gains.
7. **2nd / 8th**: travel/relocation cost, loan, tax, insurance, shared money, exchange or hidden expense.
8. **6th / Mars / Saturn**: fatigue, routine disruption, maintenance, accident/overuse pressure, travel health strain. Use as risk-management, not certainty.
9. **Dignity, sect, reception, aspects, lots, timing**: weigh quality only from supplied fields. If exact electional/astrocartography packet is missing, answer from natal promise plus supplied transits only.

## Reading Rules

- Do not answer relocation from 9th alone. Require personal capacity, 3rd/9th/12th movement field, 4th base, money/work support, and timing.
- Strong 9th/12th with weak 4th means distance/foreign pull is real but home stability must be handled.
- Strong 4th with weak 9th/12th means staying or shorter movement is safer than forcing foreign relocation.
- For foreign clients/outside audience, combine 9th/12th with 10th/11th and Mercury/Jupiter. Do not treat it as tourism.
- With no birth time, be decisive but narrower: close houses/angles/lots-by-house and answer from planets/aspects/timing.

## Answer Contract

For a travel/foreign/relocation question, write:
1. one Asc/chart-ruler or no-time substitute capacity-to-move line,
2. one 3rd/9th/12th travel-foreign-relocation line or explicit no-time limitation,
3. one 4th/IC old-base/new-base line or no-time limitation,
4. one 10th/11th work-client-audience line when relevant,
5. one 2nd/8th cost-hidden-expense line when relevant,
6. one 6th/Mars/Saturn fatigue/risk-management line when relevant,
7. one timing precision line,
8. one decisive practical call: go / go with conditions / prepare first / delay / stay / choose closer option / avoid this trip.
