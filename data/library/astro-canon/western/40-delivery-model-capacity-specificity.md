# Western Delivery Model, Capacity, And Fulfillment Fit Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Western. It condenses classical, medieval, early-modern, public-domain modern bridge, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, or expose raw source text.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks how to deliver an offer: one-to-one, high-touch, low-touch, group program, membership, subscription, self-serve course/library, done-for-you, done-with-you, audit/report, fixed-scope package, retainer, agency service, marketplace/platform, or hybrid delivery.

Do not treat delivery model as the same thing as offer fit, pricing, marketing, customer persona, support operations, or technology build. Delivery model decides how much human labor, team load, documentation, repeatability, service boundary, queue pressure, and scale capacity the chart can carry.

## Delivery Chain

1. **Ascendant/chart ruler or no-time substitute**: owner stamina, personal delivery style, pressure tolerance, and whether the native can carry a high-touch promise.
2. **MC/10th/Sun**: formal role, service authority, promise ownership, professional standard, and whether the delivery must be expert-led.
3. **3rd/Mercury and 5th**: explanation, SOP, scripts, documentation, chat flow, handoff, course material, report structure, creative output, and repeatability.
4. **7th/Venus**: direct client expectation, contract boundary, one-to-one service, bespoke request risk, revision pressure, and trust load.
5. **11th**: team, group delivery, community, platform, referrals, membership, and whether scale can be shared beyond the owner.
6. **2nd/Fortune/4th/8th**: margin, retained asset, backend base, inventory/library, subscription base, debt/refund exposure, and whether delivery creates stored value.
7. **12th**: hidden labor, private work, remote/asynchronous delivery, retreat/recovery, and invisible overhead.
8. **6th/8th/12th/Saturn-Mars**: bottleneck, support burden, complaint risk, burnout, liability, defect queue, and delivery model to avoid.
9. **Dignity/sect/reception/aspects/lots and timing**: judge strength and launch/scale window from supplied packet only.

## Delivery Model Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic planet keywords:

- **Asc/10th + 7th strong, 11th weaker**: premium one-to-one, high-trust advisory, fixed client slot, referral-only service.
- **11th + 3rd/Mercury strong**: group program, cohort, membership, community support, platform-assisted service, staff-assisted model.
- **3rd/Mercury + 4th/2nd strong**: self-serve course, template library, report product, documentation pack, recorded training, knowledge base.
- **MC/10th + 3rd strong, 6th/12th active**: audit/report/fixed-scope package; avoid open-ended done-for-you.
- **10th + 7th strong, 2nd/Fortune stable**: done-for-you or done-with-you package with deposit, milestone, and revision cap.
- **2nd/Fortune + 4th + 11th strong**: subscription, retainer, membership, stored-value backend, recurring delivery with defined scope.
- **5th/Venus/Jupiter strong with low 6th/12th stress**: creative/advisory/community-care/high-satisfaction model.
- **6th/8th/12th/Saturn-Mars repeats**: avoid unlimited chat, vague custom work, urgent-response promise, all-inclusive retainer, and owner-only support.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close house-ruler/lots/MC/11th claims unless the packet supplies reliable fallback. Use Sun/Moon/Mercury/Venus/Mars/Jupiter/Saturn condition, dignity, aspects, and broad timing only; frame delivery model as lower precision.

## Verdict Rules

- If one-to-one trust is stronger than team/community capacity, choose premium limited slots before group scale.
- If 3rd/4th/2nd is stronger than 7th, build self-serve or documented delivery before custom service.
- If 11th is strong but 3rd/Mercury is weak, group delivery needs clearer SOP and moderation before scaling.
- If 6th/8th/12th repeats, cap intake, cap revisions, require deposit, narrow scope, or pause new clients.
- If 2nd/Fortune/4th is weak, do not promise recurring delivery until backend, margin, and retained assets are fixed.
