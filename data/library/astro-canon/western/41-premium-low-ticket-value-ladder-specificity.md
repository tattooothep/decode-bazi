## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks whether to sell high-ticket, premium, VIP, low-ticket, starter offer, entry offer, tripwire, mass-market product, value ladder, tier ladder, backend offer, upsell path, or enterprise package.

Do not treat value-ladder fit as the same thing as exact pricing, promotion, marketing traffic, delivery model, or product category. Price level is not the same as price change. This pack decides whether the chart supports trust-heavy premium sales, low-ticket volume, entry-to-backend ladder, or a narrow middle-tier offer.

## Value-Ladder Chain

1. **Ascendant/chart ruler or no-time substitute**: confidence, stamina, personal authority, and whether the native can carry premium trust or should use a lower-friction entry path.
2. **MC/10th/Sun**: status, authority, expertise, public promise, enterprise/B2B posture, and whether the offer can justify high-ticket positioning.
3. **2nd/Fortune/4th/8th**: margin, retained value, asset base, recurring base, inventory/data/library, shared-money/refund exposure, and whether low-ticket volume leaks cash.
4. **3rd/Mercury and 5th**: copy, explanation, sales script, proof, webinar/report/course ladder, creative output, and whether the buyer understands why a higher tier exists.
5. **7th/Venus**: direct buyer trust, contract risk, consultation need, objection handling, refund/refusal risk, and high-ticket closing pressure.
6. **11th**: community, volume audience, group buyers, affiliates, platform, sales team, and whether low-ticket or ladder scaling can be shared.
7. **9th/10th/11th/Fortune**: perceived value, prestige, public credibility, institutional trust, social proof, and whether premium feels natural or forced.
8. **6th/8th/12th/Saturn-Mars**: support cost, hidden labor, cheap-client burden, premium liability, refund risk, and tier type to avoid.
9. **Dignity/sect/reception/aspects/lots and timing**: judge strength and launch/raise/ladder window from supplied packet only.

## Tier Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic planet keywords:

- **MC/10th + 7th + 9th/11th strong**: high-ticket advisory, premium 1:1, VIP package, enterprise/client-specific offer.
- **3rd/5th + 11th strong, 7th weaker**: low-ticket entry, mass-market course, group buyer, template, content-led starter offer.
- **2nd/Fortune + 4th strong**: subscription, stored-value tier, library, membership, backend asset, recurring ladder.
- **MC/10th + 3rd strong**: paid report, audit, certification, professional middle-tier, authority-building starter.
- **7th strong but 6th/12th active**: premium only with qualification, deposit, limited slots, and strict scope.
- **11th strong but 2nd weak**: audience exists but volume may not profit; test small before scaling low-ticket.
- **6th/8th/12th/Saturn-Mars repeats**: avoid unlimited low-ticket support, broad discounts, cheap custom work, high-ticket guarantee without proof, or too many tiers.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close house-ruler/lots/MC/11th claims unless the packet supplies reliable fallback. Use Sun/Moon/Mercury/Venus/Mars/Jupiter/Saturn condition, dignity, aspects, and broad timing only; frame tier advice as lower precision.

## Verdict Rules

- If trust/authority beats audience volume, choose high-ticket limited slots before low-ticket scale.
- If audience/SOP beats direct trust, choose low-ticket or group entry before premium sales.
- If 2nd/Fortune/4th is strong, build a ladder with retained value instead of one-off labor.
- If 3rd/Mercury is weak, do not create many tiers until the offer ladder is easy to explain.
- If 6th/8th/12th repeats, simplify to one clear entry offer plus one capped backend offer.
