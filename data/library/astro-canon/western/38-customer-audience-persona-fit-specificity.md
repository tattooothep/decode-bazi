# Western Customer, Audience, And Persona-Fit Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Western. It condenses classical, medieval, early-modern, public-domain modern bridge, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, or expose raw source text.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks who to sell to, which customer group fits them, who should be their target audience, what client persona is suitable, what type of followers/subscribers/customers convert, or which customers should be avoided.

Do not treat customer fit as the same thing as marketing traffic. Split audience persona, trust path, buying motive, conversion channel, repeat behavior, team/service burden, money quality, and timing.

## Persona Chain

1. **Ascendant/chart ruler or no-time substitute**: delivery style, pressure tolerance, and customer temperament the native can handle.
2. **7th house/ruler/Venus if relevant**: direct clients, buyers, contracts, one-to-one customer relationship, and trust before payment.
3. **11th house/ruler**: audience, community, subscribers, network gains, referrals, platforms, and social proof.
4. **3rd house/Mercury**: message, copy, chat, sales script, documentation, teaching, content, code/spec handoff, and how customers understand the offer.
5. **10th/MC/Sun**: public authority, professional status, institutional buyer, B2B/B2G, and expertise-led conversion.
6. **2nd/Fortune/8th**: buyer budget, cash retention, pricing power, debt/refund/shared-money risk, and whether customers leave money behind.
7. **6th/12th**: service load, support burden, hidden labor, staff/process strain, complaint handling, and sleep/recovery cost.
8. **Dignity/sect/reception/aspects/lots**: whether customer significators act cleanly or need support.
9. **Timing**: supplied transits and return cycles decide when to target a new segment, raise buyer quality, narrow persona, or stop serving a draining group.

## Customer Type Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic planet keywords:

- **7th strong and clean**: direct client, one-to-one service, premium buyer, contract buyer, referral sale.
- **11th strong and clean**: community, membership, subscriber, followers, group buyer, platform audience.
- **3rd/Mercury strong**: customers who need explanation, education, documentation, content, chat, consulting, code/spec clarity.
- **10th/MC strong**: professional, institutional, executive, status-sensitive, enterprise, regulated, authority-seeking buyer.
- **2nd/Fortune strong**: customers with budget, repeat purchase, retained subscription, product/data/property value, stored value.
- **4th/12th strong**: home, care, retreat, private, institutional, backend, archive/data, sleep/rest or hidden-work markets.
- **6th/8th/12th/Saturn-Mars stress**: avoid high-maintenance, high-liability, refund-prone, vague-scope, emergency, or underpriced customers.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close 7th/11th/10th/house-ruler/lots claims unless the packet supplies reliable fallback. Use Sun/Moon/Mercury/Venus/Mars/Jupiter/Saturn condition, aspects, dignity, and broad timing only; frame persona fit as lower precision.

## Verdict Rules

- If 7th is stronger than 11th, prefer fewer high-trust buyers over mass audience.
- If 11th is stronger than 7th, build community/membership/platform but protect service capacity.
- If 3rd/Mercury is weak, avoid customers who need heavy explanation unless documentation or sales script is fixed first.
- If 2nd/Fortune is good but 6th/8th/12th repeats, customers pay but drain operations; narrow scope, require deposits, or cap support.
- If 10th/MC is good but 7th weak, authority can rise while conversion remains weak; do not scale ads before offer trust is fixed.
