## Purpose

Use this pack for reputation, public image, credibility, reviews, comments, gossip, scandal, public backlash, defamation, being attacked online, platform ban, public apology, and "will people still trust me" questions.

Do not answer from MC, Sun, or fame language alone. Split visibility, credibility, accusation, audience reaction, money/status damage, and repair timing.

## Core Chain

1. **Capacity to carry public pressure**: Ascendant/chart ruler when birth time is known; otherwise Sun/Moon/Mercury/Venus/Saturn condition and supplied aspects.
2. **Public image and formal name**: MC/10th/Sun for reputation, title, authority, brand promise, and professional visibility.
3. **Message and evidence**: 3rd/Mercury for posts, comments, documents, replies, receipts, rumor flow, and explanation quality.
4. **Audience and social proof**: 11th/Jupiter/Venus/Mercury for followers, allies, patrons, public support, and group amplification.
5. **Opponent/customer/platform**: 7th for customers, sponsors, accusers, open enemies, contracts, and platforms.
6. **Material consequence**: 2nd/Fortune for revenue and pricing trust; 8th for fees, shared risk, tax, debt, investors, and reputational liability.
7. **Hidden drain and workload**: 12th for hidden loss, 6th for service burden and complaint handling, Saturn/Mars hard contact for friction.
8. **Timing**: supplied transits/return cycles activate the natal promise; do not invent exact dates.

## Trust-Chain Rule

Public visibility is not public trust. Require a chain:

- MC/10th/Sun = public identity and authority,
- 3rd/Mercury = message, evidence, or rumor path,
- 11th = audience support or group reaction,
- 7th = accuser/customer/platform/counterparty,
- 2nd/Fortune/8th = money, liability, and retained value.

If MC/10th is strong but Mercury/3rd is pressured, say reputation exists but messaging is the weak point. If 11th is pressured, the crowd reaction is unstable. If 2nd/Fortune/8th is protected, noise may not become financial damage; if not, reputation risk can hit cashflow or obligations.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close houses, angles, lots, and house-ruler claims unless packet supplies a reliable fallback. Use Sun/Moon/Mercury/Venus/Mars/Saturn/Jupiter dignity and aspects only, and do not claim precise public-house damage.

## Crisis And Repair Rule

For review/backlash/scandal questions, name the situation:

- **misunderstanding**: Mercury/3rd problem without deep 2nd/8th damage,
- **customer/platform conflict**: 7th and 2nd/8th involved,
- **public pile-on**: 11th involved with hard contacts,
- **formal/status damage**: MC/10th/Sun involved,
- **hidden drain**: 12th/6th involved.

Repair uses the strongest support axis: clarify evidence (3rd/Mercury), rebuild professional promise (10th), answer customers directly (7th), use allies/social proof (11th), fix offer/pricing/refund flow (2nd/8th), and reduce workload leak (6th/12th).
