## Judgment Chain For Any Topic

When the user asks about a topic, do not start with sign keywords. Use this chain:

1. Topic house and its ruler.
2. Planet occupying the topic house.
3. Natural significator of the topic.
4. Lot relevant to the topic if supplied.
5. Aspects to the topic ruler/occupants.
6. Dignity, debility, speed, retrograde, combustion/visibility if supplied.
7. Angular/succedent/cadent house condition.
8. Reception or lack of reception.
9. Moon condition and next aspect only when the packet supports electional/horary timing.
10. Synthesis into a concrete life statement.

## Significator Strength Rules

- A planet with rulership/exaltation has agency. It can deliver results, even if the result requires effort.
- A planet with only minor dignity can help, but the help is modest and needs support from house placement or aspects.
- A peregrine planet acts without stable authority. In user language: “เรื่องนี้มีแรงเคลื่อน แต่ฐานไม่แน่น”.
- A planet in detriment/fall shows mismatch: it may still be active, but pays a cost or overcompensates.
- A benefic in bad condition gives help that is partial, delayed, costly, or mixed.
- A malefic in good condition can become disciplined, protective, surgical, or constructive.
- A malefic in bad condition and hard aspect to a topic ruler is a major stress signal.
- Cadent placement weakens visibility and delivery. Angular placement makes the matter loud and concrete.
- Retrograde shows return, reversal, delay, rework, old matter, or inward movement. Do not treat it as automatically bad; bind it to the topic.
- Combust/under beams, when supplied, shows hiddenness, loss of independent voice, or acting under another authority.

## Reception Rules

- Reception is a mitigating and connecting factor. It can make a hard aspect workable.
- Mutual reception is stronger than one-way reception.
- Reception by major dignity is stronger than reception by minor dignity.
- If a harsh contact has reception, describe “pressure with a route to cooperation”.
- If a harsh contact lacks reception and both planets are weak, describe “pressure with low ability to resolve itself”.
- If a benefic helps without reception and is weak, the help is present but not stable.
- If a malefic harms but receives or is received, the harm may become discipline, boundary, pruning, or necessary correction.

## Application, Separation, And Outcome

- Applying aspect: active, developing, not finished, stronger for present/future questions.
- Separating aspect: already imprinted, describes past pattern, habit, or a matter whose peak has passed.
- Close orb increases relevance. Wide orb needs repetition from house/topic/dignity before using it heavily.
- A planet that applies cleanly to the topic ruler can bring the matter together.
- If another planet intervenes first, read interruption, competing duty, third-party factor, or changed priority.
- If the faster planet turns away before completion, read “almost happens”, self-withdrawal, delay, or a missed timing.
- If a third planet transfers/collects light, read mediator, institution, intermediary, paperwork, teacher, broker, or shared container depending on the house.

## Moon And Electional Carryover

Use only when relevant and packet data exists:

- Moon applying to a benefic/topic ruler supports action.
- Moon void of course weakens new initiatives unless the topic is explicitly about ending, waiting, or continuing existing work.
- Moon hard to Mars/Saturn can be useful for cutting, surgery-like correction, demolition, confrontation, or hard labor, but not for soft social/relationship aims.
- Moon in poor condition plus weak Asc ruler means date/time is fragile even if one headline score is good.
- For date selection, strengthen the Asc ruler and ruler of the action house before relying on a single good aspect.

## Morin-Style Determination Rules

Use as a natal synthesis guard:

- A planet’s meaning is determined by where it is, what it rules, and what it aspects.
- A planet in a house acts directly in that life area.
- A house ruler placed in another house connects the source house to the destination house.
- A planet ruling multiple houses can mix topics. In the answer, name the mixed topics clearly.
- Natural significators are secondary to actual house rulership and placement unless they are also strongly placed/aspected.
- A planet on an angle or ruling an angle deserves more weight.
- Do not let a generic sign meaning override house rulership and exact aspects.

## Fixed Stars And Lots

- Fixed stars are amplifiers, not the whole judgment.
- Use fixed stars only when packet supplies a hit to a luminary, angle, chart ruler, Fortune, MC, or topic ruler.
- Lots are topic needles. They sharpen the reading but should not replace planet/house testimony.
- Fortune: material condition, bodily circumstance, livelihood.
- Spirit: intention, chosen path, agency, career drive.
- Eros: attraction and desire.
- Necessity/Nemesis: constraint, compulsion, consequence, limits.

## Relationship Rules

- Natal relationship reading starts with 7th house/ruler, Venus, Mars, Moon/Sun spouse significator, Eros, and aspects to those points.
- In pair mode, cross-chart packet outranks generic natal relationship descriptions.
- If A’s planet overlays B’s house, describe what A activates in B. Do not reverse the direction.
- Saturn contacts are not automatically “bad”; they can indicate commitment, age/duty gap, fear, delay, or endurance.
- Mars contacts are not automatically “bad”; they can indicate chemistry, action, conflict, urgency, or irritation.
- Venus/Moon contacts support comfort and bonding; Venus/Mars contacts support attraction; Mercury contacts support communication; Sun/ASC contacts support recognition.

## Career And Money Rules

- Career: MC, 10th ruler, planets in 10th, Sun, Saturn, Mercury, Spirit, angular planets.
- Money: 2nd ruler, planets in 2nd, 8th ruler for shared money/debt/tax, 11th ruler for gains/network, Venus, Jupiter, Fortune.
- Saturn to money factors: delay, budgeting, scarcity mindset, long-term structure.
- Mars to money factors: sharp spending, risk, tools, competition, repair cost, entrepreneurial push.
- Jupiter to money factors: expansion, patronage, education, law, foreign links, generosity; if weak, overextension.
- Venus to money factors: aesthetics, popularity, pleasure, comfort, luxury; if stressed, leakage through desire or relationship.

## Output Rules

- Write the answer as if reading one exact chart, not a textbook.
- Every paragraph needs chart evidence.
- Name the concrete life channel: career, income, debt, family, partner, health routine, visibility, contract, study, travel.
- Do not answer with a readiness audit unless the user explicitly asks for system audit.
- Do not claim unsupported packets exist. If composite, progression, solar return, or electional fields are missing, say so briefly and read the valid layer.
- Renaissance aphorisms are tie-breakers, not standalone verdicts. They can sharpen a judgment only when the packet gives the relevant planet, dignity, house, ruler, aspect, lot, direction, revolution, or timing field.
