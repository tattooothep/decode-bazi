# Western Loan, Credit, Debt, And Refinance Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about loans, credit approval, credit cards, debt repayment, debt restructuring, refinancing, installment plans, interest rate, principal, collateral, guarantor, creditor pressure, collection, default, delinquency, or whether to borrow, refinance, restructure, consolidate debt, pay down debt, negotiate with creditors, or avoid new debt.

Do not treat loan/credit/debt as the same thing as cashflow/runway, unit economics, inventory, tax/accounting, property purchase, general legal approval, windfall/speculation, or generic business. If the question is only "cash left / burn / reserve", use the cashflow pack. If it is only "buy house / sell land / move home", use the property pack. If it asks loan terms, repayment, refinance, collateral, guarantor, credit approval, default, or creditor pressure, this pack must be included.

## Canon Routing

Western loan/debt judgment must join Asc/chart ruler, 2nd/Fortune, 8th, 4th/IC, 7th, 3rd/Mercury, 6th, MC/10th, 12th, and Saturn/Mars. Debt is not simply money; it is borrowed money under a contract and a repayment clock.

- Asc/chart-ruler or no-time substitute: borrower discipline, repayment capacity, and whether the owner can carry obligation without avoidance.
- 2nd/Fortune: cash-in, owned cash, income available for installments, interest burden, and payment priority.
- 8th: loan, credit line, lender money, tax/insurance entanglement, shared liability, penalty, refinance, and debt restructuring.
- 4th/IC: collateral, reserve base, retained asset, mortgage/security value, and whether the foundation can support the obligation.
- 7th: creditor, bank officer, guarantor, co-borrower, partner, counterparty contract, and negotiation channel.
- 3rd/Mercury: application paperwork, credit file, debt statement, repayment schedule, communication record, and negotiation messages.
- 6th: repayment routine, auto-pay, admin execution, bookkeeper/staff handling payments, and debt service discipline.
- MC/10th: formal approval standard, public/status duty, business credit policy, and responsibility to maintain standing.
- 12th/Saturn-Mars: default, delinquency, collection, penalty interest, hidden fee, debt spiral, and avoidable loss.
- Packet-supplied timing: when to borrow, refinance, restructure, pay down, negotiate, avoid new debt, or face creditor pressure.

## Answer Contract

For loan/credit/debt/refinance questions, answer with:

1. one Asc/chart-ruler or no-time substitute borrower-discipline/repayment-capacity line;
2. one 2nd/Fortune cash-in/installment/debt-service/interest line;
3. one 8th loan/refinance/shared-liability line;
4. one 4th/IC collateral/reserve/asset-security line;
5. one 7th creditor/guarantor/co-borrower/counterparty-contract line;
6. one 3rd/Mercury application-document/credit-file/repayment-schedule line;
7. one 6th payment-admin/auto-pay/collection-follow-up line;
8. one 12th/Saturn-Mars default/penalty/hidden-fee/refinance-trap line;
9. one timing and action verdict: borrow, borrow only with cap, refinance, restructure, consolidate, negotiate, pay down first, avoid new debt, prepare documents, increase reserve, or wait.

Do not turn an 8th-house loan into a simple "money opportunity." The answer must separate owned money, borrowed money, collateral, creditor behavior, repayment routine, and default risk.
