## Purpose

Use this pack for ads, paid traffic, campaign, launch campaign, lead generation, funnel, landing page, CAC, ROAS, CPA, CPL, retargeting, pixel, conversion rate, ad creative, media buying, channel test, affiliate/referral traffic, and "should I spend ad budget" questions.

## Core Chain

1. **Capacity and campaign owner**: Ascendant/chart ruler when birth time is known; otherwise Sun/Moon/Mercury/Saturn condition and supplied aspects.
2. **Message and ad creative**: 3rd house/Mercury show copy, hook, landing page, tracking terms, comment replies, chat scripts, and campaign clarity.
3. **Visibility and positioning**: MC/10th, Sun, and strong dignities show public claim, product credibility, and whether the ad promise looks believable.
4. **Audience and traffic pool**: 11th house shows audience base, warm pool, affiliates, patrons, community, and whether traffic has quality.
5. **Buyer and conversion**: 7th house/ruler shows customer, platform, sponsor, checkout counterparty, lead quality, and whether traffic becomes transaction.
6. **Cash recovery**: 2nd house/ruler and Part of Fortune show collected revenue, CAC recovery, ROAS, and whether spend comes back as cash.
7. **Budget reserve and shared cost**: 8th shows ad debt, fees, platform liability, financing, refunds, and outside money pressure.
8. **Workload and leakage**: 6th workload/support complaints and 12th hidden loss show wasted spend, bad leads, ad fatigue, support burden, or unseen drain.
9. **Timing**: supplied transits/return cycles activate the natal promise; do not invent exact dates.

## Campaign-Chain Rule

Traffic is not the same as customers. Require a chain:

- 3rd/Mercury = message and landing page,
- MC/10th = public claim and offer authority,
- 11th = audience and traffic pool,
- 7th = lead quality and conversion counterparty,
- 2nd/Fortune = CAC recovery and ROAS,
- 6th/8th/12th = wasted spend, complaint, bad leads, platform leak, hidden cost.

If 3rd/11th are strong but 7th/2nd are weak, the campaign gets attention but does not pay back. If 11th is strong but Mercury/3rd is weak, the audience exists but the hook/copy is wrong. If 8th/12th dominate, do not scale paid ads.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close houses, angles, lots, and house-ruler claims unless packet supplies a reliable fallback. Use Sun/Moon/Mercury/Venus/Jupiter/Saturn condition, aspects, and timing only; avoid confident CAC/ROAS calls that depend on houses.
