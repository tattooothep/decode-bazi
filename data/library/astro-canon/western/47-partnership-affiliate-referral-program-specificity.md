# Western Partnership, Affiliate, And Referral Program Specificity Pack

Private derived runtime rules for questions about partner programs, affiliate programs, referral programs, channel partners, ambassadors, co-marketing, joint ventures, revenue share, commission structure, resellers, agency partners, partner tiers, partner onboarding, and partner fraud/conflict risk.

## Purpose

Do not treat partnership program fit as the same thing as romantic relationship, marketing traffic, sales script, customer success referral ask, customer support, or generic business investment. This pack is for a structured external growth channel where other people or organizations bring audience, trust, distribution, or sales in exchange for commission, status, reciprocal value, or shared revenue.

## Partnership Chain

1. **Ascendant / chart ruler / Sun-Moon baseline**: owner capacity, boundary enforcement, and whether the native can carry shared incentives without losing control.
2. **MC / 10th / Sun / Saturn**: strategic authority, partner selection standard, compliance, public responsibility, and professional credibility.
3. **3rd / Mercury / 9th**: referral message, affiliate instructions, contract terms, tracking links, introductions, and co-marketing education.
4. **7th / Venus**: contractual partner relationship, expectation match, revenue share, commission promise, and agreement stability. Do not read this as romance unless the user asks romance.
5. **11th**: affiliate pool, ambassadors, reseller network, agencies, community partners, partner managers, and scaled coordination.
6. **2nd / Lot of Fortune / 4th / 8th**: commission economics, margin after payouts, retained assets, partner-generated recurring value, shared money, and backend ownership.
7. **9th / Jupiter / Moon / 5th**: trust transfer, reputation fit, shared audience belief, teaching/evangelism, ambassador goodwill, and referral halo.
8. **6th / 8th / 12th / Mars-Saturn**: partner fraud, tracking dispute, commission abuse, brand contamination, hidden support burden, legal/accounting leak, and termination risk.
9. **Dignity / sect / reception / aspects / lots / timing**: weigh whether partner planets can deliver, whether promises are stable, and when to recruit, pilot, sign, scale, pause, or terminate.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is unknown, do not use Ascendant, MC, houses, Lot of Fortune, or house overlays. Substitute Sun, Moon, Mercury, Venus, Jupiter, Saturn, aspects, sign condition, and no-time timing windows. Say clearly which partner-program judgment is reduced by unknown houses.

## Program Mapping

- Strong MC/10th + 3rd/Mercury + 11th: partner program can work as public distribution with clear message and coordinated channel.
- Strong 7th + 2nd/Fortune/8th: revenue-share or affiliate agreement can be commercial, but only if tracking and payout terms are explicit.
- Strong 9th/Jupiter/Moon/5th: referral, ambassador, and trust-transfer channels are favored over cold paid acquisition.
- Stressed 6th/8th/12th or hard Mars-Saturn contacts: do not open broad affiliate access; use invite-only pilot, capped commissions, manual approval, and fraud checks.
- Strong 11th but weak Mercury/3rd: many partners can appear, but onboarding material and tracking will decide whether they sell correctly.
- Strong Mercury/3rd but weak 2nd/Fortune: buzz and introductions may happen, but commission model can leak margin.

## Verdict Rules

- If partnership evidence is mixed, split the answer into **partner type**, **program structure**, **payout/terms**, **tracking/onboarding**, and **risk control**.
- Do not say "find partners" generically. Name the channel: affiliate link, referral bounty, reseller, agency partner, ambassador, JV webinar, co-marketing, creator affiliate, strategic alliance, or no partner yet.
- If 6th/8th/12th is stressed, recommend invite-only, low cap, manual approval, short pilot, fraud audit, or termination clause.
- If timing is asked, tie the call to natal promise + transit/profection/return fields present in packet; do not invent progressions or electional charts.
