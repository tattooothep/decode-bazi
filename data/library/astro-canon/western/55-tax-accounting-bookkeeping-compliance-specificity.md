# Western Tax, Accounting, Bookkeeping, And Compliance Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about tax filing, VAT, sales tax, withholding tax, accounting, bookkeeping, invoices, receipts, P&L, balance sheet, reconciliation, audit trail, financial records, accountant, filing deadline, compliance, tax penalty, or whether to clean books, file now, delay filing, hire accountant, or prepare for audit.

Do not treat tax/accounting as the same thing as cashflow/runway, unit economics, inventory, pricing, marketing, general legal approval, generic debt risk, or generic business. If the user asks only "cash left / burn / reserve", use the cashflow pack. If the user asks "court / visa / permit / license / lawsuit", use the authority/legal pack unless accounting records, tax filing, VAT, invoices, or bookkeeping are the core issue.

## Canon Routing

Western tax/accounting judgment must join Asc/chart ruler, 3rd/Mercury, MC/10th, 2nd/Fortune, 4th/IC, 7th, 6th, 8th, 9th/10th, and 12th/Saturn-Mars. Tax is a document-and-liability question, not simply a money question.

- Asc/chart-ruler or no-time substitute: whether the owner can keep financial discipline, deadlines, and proof without avoidance.
- 3rd/Mercury: invoice number, receipt, ledger, reconciliation, message to accountant, document trail, and data entry.
- MC/10th: compliance responsibility, filing process, accounting policy, and public/formal accountability.
- 2nd/Fortune: taxable revenue, tax reserve, VAT output/input, withholding, payable/refundable tax, and cash impact.
- 4th/IC: retained records, stored receipts, fixed assets, depreciation base, and old-book cleanup.
- 7th: accountant, auditor, tax officer, customer/vendor documents, counterparty invoice terms, and filing representation.
- 6th: bookkeeper, admin, accounting software, data-entry workflow, payroll support, and monthly close execution.
- 8th: tax liability, back tax, shared-money tax, penalty, audit exposure, and insurance/loan/tax entanglement.
- 9th/10th: tax law, regulator, legal filing standard, government portal, and official compliance lane.
- 12th/Saturn-Mars: hidden document loss, late filing, fines, back-office backlog, and avoidable penalty.
- Packet-supplied timing: when to file, amend, clean books, hire accountant, reserve tax, or face audit pressure.

## Answer Contract

For tax/accounting questions, answer with:

1. one Asc/chart-ruler or no-time substitute financial-discipline/deadline line;
2. one 3rd/Mercury invoice/receipt/ledger/reconciliation line;
3. one MC/10th tax-compliance/filing-process line;
4. one 2nd/Fortune taxable-revenue/VAT/withholding/tax-reserve line;
5. one 4th/IC retained-record/fixed-asset/old-book line;
6. one 7th accountant/auditor/tax-office/counterparty-doc line;
7. one 6th bookkeeping/software/admin-execution line;
8. one 8th/12th/Saturn-Mars audit/penalty/missing-document/back-tax-risk line;
9. one timing and action verdict: file now, amend, clean books first, hire accountant, reserve tax, reconcile monthly, separate personal/business accounts, prepare audit folder, or delay only with documented reason.

Never give tax/legal advice as a legal guarantee. Use the chart to decide the operational stance, then tell the user to confirm amounts and filing duties with a qualified tax professional.
