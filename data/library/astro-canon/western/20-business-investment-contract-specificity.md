# Western Business, Investment, And Contract Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Western. Use as operational rules only; do not expose raw OCR, licensed prose, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack for opening a shop, starting a company, investing, buying/selling, contracts, clients, suppliers, partners, loans, expansion, pricing, and business risk.

Do not answer business from the 2nd house alone. Split career channel, cashflow, gains/customers, debt/investor money, contracts, and timing.

## Business Chain

Read in this order:

1. **Ascendant/chart ruler or no-time substitute**: capacity to act and carry risk.
2. **MC/10th and 10th ruler**: business role, public status, authority, operating model.
3. **2nd house/ruler and Fortune**: cashflow, pricing, personal capital, retained money.
4. **11th house/ruler**: customers, patrons, audience, recurring gains, network.
5. **7th house/ruler**: contracts, buyers/sellers, clients, suppliers, business partners.
6. **8th house/ruler and Necessity/Nemesis if supplied**: loans, investor/shared money, tax, debt, obligation, hidden cost.
7. **Mercury/Venus/Jupiter/Saturn/Mars** by role: commerce, value, growth, structure, execution/risk.
8. **Target-date transits/return cycles**: timing support or pressure.

## Action Calls

- **go**: 10th/2nd/11th/7th repeat support, chart ruler/topic ruler can act, and timing reinforces.
- **conditional go**: business channel is real but 8th/12th, hard Mars/Saturn, weak dignity, or contract pressure requires controls.
- **wait/prepare**: natal promise exists but timing or contract/debt lane is heavy.
- **no**: repeated contradiction from business houses, chart ruler/topic ruler, contract/debt lane, and timing.

## Contract And Investment Rules

- 7th = one-to-one contract/counterparty, not romance unless the user asks relationship.
- 8th = borrowed/investor/shared money, tax, debt, repayment, and leverage.
- 11th strong with 2nd weak = customers/network can bring money but retention needs accounting discipline.
- 2nd strong with 8th pressured = earning exists but leverage/debt/shared money must be limited.
- Do not give buy/sell/hold, price target, guaranteed profit, or portfolio sizing.

## Output Contract

Every business answer must include:

- one business-action line naming the exact action and house/significator,
- one MC/10th operating-channel line,
- one 2nd/11th/Fortune cashflow-versus-gains line,
- one 7th/8th contract-debt-counterparty line,
- one timing line from transits/return cycles if supplied,
- one concrete call: go / conditional go / wait / no,
- one practical control: cash reserve, staged investment, contract review, partner boundary, accounting, or compliance step.
