# Western Employment, Job Offer, Interview, Promotion, And Salary Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Western astrology. Use as operational rules only; do not expose raw OCR, licensed prose, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack for job applications, interviews, job offers, accepting work, promotions, title changes, salary negotiation, boss/workplace, resignation, transfer, contract roles, and employment timing.

Do not answer employment from the 10th house alone. Split career role, day-to-day job conditions, salary, interview/documents, employer/counterparty, team/network, and timing.

## Employment Chain

Read in this order:

1. **Ascendant / chart ruler or no-time substitute**: capacity to take the role and handle pressure.
2. **MC / 10th house / 10th ruler / Sun-Saturn**: role, title, promotion, authority, reputation, employer structure.
3. **6th house / 6th ruler / Saturn-Mars-Mercury**: daily work, service, workload, coworkers, routines, conflict.
4. **2nd house / Fortune / Venus-Jupiter-Mercury**: salary, compensation, cash benefit, retention.
5. **3rd house / Mercury**: application, interview, resume, message, negotiation, offer letter detail.
6. **7th house**: employer as counterparty, hiring manager, contract, consultant/client role.
7. **11th house**: professional network, referrals, team gains, audience/client pool.
8. **9th/12th/4th houses**: relocation, foreign work, remote/backstage work, workplace base.
9. **Dignity, sect, reception, aspects, lots, and supplied transits/refDate**: timing and capacity.

## Topic Rules

- **Interview / application**: 3rd/Mercury plus 10th employer/status and timing.
- **Job offer / accept role**: 10th role + 7th contract/employer + 2nd salary + 6th workload.
- **Promotion / title**: MC/10th/Sun/Saturn plus dignity/aspect support and timing; do not promise promotion from one transit.
- **Salary negotiation**: 2nd/Fortune first, then 10th authority, 7th counterparty, 3rd negotiation.
- **Resignation / job change**: compare 6th/10th pressure in current role with 10th/11th/2nd support for the next role.

## No-Time Mode

If `hasBirthTime=false`, close Ascendant, houses, MC, lots, and house rulers unless a valid fallback is supplied. Use planet condition, Mercury/Sun/Saturn/Venus/Jupiter, aspects, retrograde/combustion, and Moon condition if reliable. State that role/salary timing is limited.

## Output Contract

Every employment answer must include:

- one exact employment-action line naming apply/interview/offer/promotion/salary/resignation,
- one MC/10th role-status-employer line or no-time substitute,
- one 6th workload-coworker-routine line,
- one 2nd/Fortune salary-compensation line,
- one 3rd/Mercury interview-document-negotiation line,
- one 7th/11th employer-contract-network line when relevant,
- one timing line from supplied transits/refDate when a date/year is asked,
- one concrete call: accept/go / accept with conditions / negotiate or wait / decline or do not resign yet.
