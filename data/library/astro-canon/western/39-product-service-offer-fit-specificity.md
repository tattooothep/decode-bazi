# Western Product, Service, And Offer-Fit Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Western. It condenses classical, medieval, early-modern, public-domain modern bridge, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, or expose raw source text.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks what product to sell, what service to offer, what package/offer should be built, whether to sell physical product, digital product, advisory service, subscription, premium package, low-ticket entry, bundle, course, tool, or done-for-you service.

Do not treat offer fit as the same thing as industry fit, customer persona, marketing traffic, pricing, or technology build. Split offer promise, delivery burden, buyer trust, money model, asset/inventory/data base, message clarity, scale path, risk, and timing.

## Offer Chain

1. **Ascendant/chart ruler or no-time substitute**: native delivery style, pressure tolerance, and whether the offer should be personal-service, systemized, advisory, creative, technical, or asset-backed.
2. **MC/10th/Sun**: professional authority, offer category, public result, B2B/B2G/regulated offer, and whether the product should carry expert status.
3. **2nd/Fortune/8th**: cash model, margin, buyer payment style, subscription/stored value, debt/refund/shared-money risk, and whether the offer leaves retained value.
4. **3rd/Mercury and 5th**: explanation, copy, teaching, documentation, creative output, course, content, script, code/spec, and product clarity.
5. **7th/Venus**: direct buyer trust, contract scope, bespoke service, refund/refusal risk, and whether the offer needs consultation before payment.
6. **11th**: group delivery, membership, platform sale, audience, referrals, social proof, and whether the offer can scale.
7. **4th/12th**: backend, home/property/data/archive, retreat/private work, hidden labor, and operational base.
8. **6th/8th/12th/Saturn-Mars**: support load, defect/complaint pattern, liability, burnout, hidden cost, and offer types to avoid.
9. **Dignity/sect/reception/aspects/lots and timing**: judge strength and launch window from supplied packet only.

## Offer Type Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic planet keywords:

- **MC/10th + 3rd/Mercury strong**: course, consulting, report, audit, documentation-heavy service, expert package, certification, training.
- **2nd/Fortune + 4th/12th strong**: physical product, data/content library, subscription, property/home asset, durable package, stored-value product.
- **7th strong**: premium one-to-one service, contract service, bespoke package, high-trust consultation, referral-only offer.
- **11th strong**: group program, membership, community package, marketplace/platform offer, scalable audience product.
- **5th/Venus/Jupiter strong**: creative, beauty/taste, education, children/entertainment, luxury, joy-led or growth-led offer.
- **6th/8th/12th/Saturn-Mars stress**: avoid unlimited support, vague custom work, emergency promise, underpriced operations, high-liability offer, or complex product without process.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close house-ruler/lots/MC/11th claims unless the packet supplies reliable fallback. Use Sun/Moon/Mercury/Venus/Mars/Jupiter/Saturn condition, dignity, aspects, and broad timing only; frame offer fit as lower precision.

## Verdict Rules

- If 7th is stronger than 11th, sell a focused premium service before building a mass product.
- If 2nd/Fortune/4th is stronger than 10th, productize stored value instead of selling pure labor.
- If 3rd/Mercury is weak, do not launch a complicated offer until explanation, copy, docs, or onboarding is fixed.
- If 7th is weak, avoid high-trust bespoke packages; sell clearer fixed-scope offers.
- If 6th/8th/12th repeats, reduce scope, cap revisions, add deposit, or avoid service-heavy products.
