# Western Public-Domain Interaction Rule Pack

> Internal summary pack for user readings. Built from public-domain / rights-safe classical sources and project synthesis. Do not quote this as a modern copyrighted author. Use it to make Western readings more specific when packet evidence is present.

## Source Basis

- Ptolemy, *Tetrabiblos* (Ashmand 1822 public-domain tradition): planetary mixture, aspect geometry, familiarity/hostility, sect and topical judgment.
- William Lilly, *Christian Astrology* (1647 public-domain English): dignity, debility, aspect, application/separation, reception, and house/topic workflow.
- Bonatti/Coley/Lilly, *Anima Astrologiae* (1676 public-domain English): aphoristic readiness checks, impediments, and condition of significators.
- William Ramesey, *Astrologia Restaurata* (1653 public-domain English): electional/date-selection transfer of Moon condition, applying aspects, and action timing.
- Claude Dariot, *A Briefe and Most Easie Introduction* (1598 public-domain English): early English terminology cross-check for aspects, houses, and judgment framing.

## Master Rule: Interaction Is Not Just Aspect Type

When a planet touches another planet/point, judge in this order:

1. **Who acts?** Identify the two bodies/points and their chart roles: chart ruler, house ruler, luminary, Venus/Mars, Saturn/Jupiter, Fortune/Lot, angle, or topic lord.
2. **What life topic is triggered?** Use house, house ruler, topic matrix, and user question. Do not explain a generic planet meaning without linking it to the topic.
3. **Can each planet act?** Check essential dignity, minor dignity, sect relevance, retrograde, combustion/under beams if supplied, angularity/house strength, and whether the planet is cadent or hidden.
4. **How do they meet?** Aspect type, orb, applying/separating, hidden contact, declination, antiscia, fixed star hit if supplied.
5. **Do they receive or reject each other?** Reception by sign/exaltation/triplicity/term/face, if packet gives enough evidence. Reception can soften a hard aspect; lack of dignity can weaken even a good aspect.
6. **Is the contact completed or blocked?** In horary/electional logic, check perfection, prohibition, frustration/refranation, translation, collection. For natal reading, translate this into “works smoothly / delayed / mediated by third factor / unstable”.
7. **Then judge.** Good aspect + strong planets = clean support. Hard aspect + strong planets = conflict with capacity to act. Good aspect + weak planets = opportunity with low delivery. Hard aspect + weak planets = stress that easily drains or misfires.

## Aspect Tone Matrix

Use these as the starting tone, then modify by dignity, house, topic, and applying/separating.

- **Conjunction**: fusion, intensification, shared fate. Good or bad depends on the planets involved and their condition. Benefic + dignified planet can concentrate help; malefic + weak planet can concentrate pressure.
- **Sextile**: accessible cooperation. It helps when the native acts; it rarely forces results by itself.
- **Square**: friction, effort, conflict, urgency. If planets are strong, the native can turn pressure into output. If weak, it becomes repeated irritation or blockage.
- **Trine**: ease, flow, inherited support, natural talent. If planets are weak, it may become laziness or unused potential.
- **Opposition**: polarity, confrontation, other people, projection, separation and negotiation. It often becomes visible through relationships, contracts, competitors, or public tension.
- **Minor aspects**: treat as secondary unless the packet marks a tight orb, repeats a theme already shown by major aspects, or forms a named pattern.
- **Antiscia/parallel contacts**: hidden or non-obvious resonance. Use as supporting evidence, not as the main judgment unless it hits a luminary, angle, chart ruler, or exact topic lord.

## Applying / Separating Rule

- **Applying**: the matter is still building, more active, easier to feel as current pressure or current opportunity.
- **Separating**: the matter has already imprinted the life pattern, is familiar, or describes past conditioning and a habit that still echoes.
- If user asks “จะเกิดไหม / เมื่อไหร่”, applying contacts carry more timing weight than separating contacts.
- If user asks “ทำไมชีวิตเป็นแบบนี้”, separating contacts still matter strongly because they describe established life pattern.

## Reception And Dignity Modifiers

- **Mutual reception**: two planets have a route to cooperate even through hard aspect. In user language: “มีแรงตึง แต่คุยกันรู้เรื่อง / มีทางแลกเปลี่ยน”.
- **One-way reception**: one side helps or accommodates the other more than the reverse. Use this in relationship readings to identify imbalance.
- **No dignity / peregrine**: planet has less reliable agency. It may still act if angular or tightly aspected, but acts without stable footing.
- **Detriment/fall**: planet’s topic is strained, overcompensating, or working in an unnatural environment. Do not call it “bad only”; state the concrete cost.
- **Rulership/exaltation**: planet has agency. If it is in hard aspect, the native has power but also sharp conflict.
- **Triplicity/term/face**: minor support. It can rescue nuance, but should not outweigh a clear fall/detriment plus hard aspect unless repeated evidence supports it.

## Classical Interaction State Machine

Use this only when packet evidence supports it. Do not invent missing intermediate planets.

- **Perfection**: two significators connect cleanly. In natal reading, this becomes a life channel that can produce concrete results.
- **Prohibition**: a third planet reaches one significator first and blocks the intended contact. In natal reading, this means another duty/person/factor interrupts the topic.
- **Frustration / refranation**: the contact almost happens but one planet turns away or loses capacity. In natal reading, this shows repeated “almost” patterns, delay, or self-withdrawal.
- **Translation of light**: a faster planet carries influence between two planets. In natal reading, a third skill/person/channel mediates the result.
- **Collection of light**: a slower planet gathers the influence of two planets. In natal reading, an institution, authority, long project, or older person becomes the container.
- **Combust / under beams**: if supplied, a planet near the Sun is less visible and may struggle to act openly. It can still matter strongly if the Sun/topic is central.
- **Void Moon / Moon condition**: for electional/date-selection use only when packet supplies it. Do not apply if not computed.

## Planet Pair Reading Rules

Use the actual pair and aspect from packet. Do not output generic planet descriptions; always bind to sign, house, dignity, and user question.

- **Sun-Moon**: identity versus emotional rhythm. Harmonious contact supports inner coherence; hard contact shows gap between will and need.
- **Sun-Mercury**: self-expression and thinking. Close contacts make voice/identity central; stress can create overthinking or pride in opinions.
- **Sun-Venus**: identity and attraction/value. Supports charm and visibility; stress can make approval or pleasure a major life lesson.
- **Sun-Mars**: will and drive. Supports courage; stress can show impatience, confrontation, or burnout.
- **Sun-Jupiter**: confidence, meaning, expansion. Supports teaching, leadership, patrons; stress can overpromise.
- **Sun-Saturn**: authority, duty, father/structure. Supports discipline when integrated; stress shows pressure, delay, self-criticism.
- **Moon-Mercury**: feeling and thought. Supports memory, language, adaptive mind; stress shows anxious loops.
- **Moon-Venus**: comfort, bonding, taste. Supports affection and social ease; stress shows emotional dependency or pleasure conflict.
- **Moon-Mars**: instinct and heat. Supports quick response; stress shows irritability, inflammation, domestic tension.
- **Moon-Jupiter**: emotional generosity and faith. Supports protection; stress shows excess or emotional inflation.
- **Moon-Saturn**: emotional restraint and duty. Supports endurance; stress shows loneliness, fear, or heavy family pattern.
- **Mercury-Venus**: pleasant speech, design, negotiation. Stress shows people-pleasing or indecision around values.
- **Mercury-Mars**: sharp speech, technical speed, debate. Stress shows argument, haste, cutting words.
- **Mercury-Jupiter**: learning, teaching, law, language. Stress shows exaggeration or scattered judgment.
- **Mercury-Saturn**: structure of thought, planning, accounting. Stress shows worry, pessimism, speech inhibition.
- **Venus-Mars**: attraction, desire, aesthetics plus action. Stress shows sexual/relational friction or impulsive spending.
- **Venus-Jupiter**: pleasure, wealth, popularity, generosity. Stress shows indulgence, overgiving, luxury leakage.
- **Venus-Saturn**: commitment, restraint, durability. Stress shows fear of love, scarcity around pleasure/money.
- **Mars-Jupiter**: bold growth, enterprise, risk. Stress shows overconfidence, legal/moral clashes, reckless expansion.
- **Mars-Saturn**: force meets resistance. Supports engineering/endurance under discipline; stress shows blocked anger, injury risk, stop-go effort.
- **Jupiter-Saturn**: expansion versus structure. Supports mature building; stress shows cycles of hope versus fear, growth constrained by duty.
- **Outer planets**: Uranus/Neptune/Pluto are modern layers. Use if packet includes them, but do not let them override luminaries, angles, chart ruler, and topic lords unless exact and repeated.

## Topic Binding

- **Career**: prioritize MC, 10th ruler, Sun, Saturn, Mercury, Lot of Spirit, angular planets, and aspects to these.
- **Money**: prioritize 2nd/8th/11th rulers, Venus, Jupiter, Fortune, Necessity, and aspects from Saturn/Mars that show leak/block.
- **Relationship**: prioritize 7th ruler, Venus, Mars, Moon/Sun spouse significator, Eros, Descendant, and synastry packet in pair mode.
- **Health / workload**: prioritize 1st/6th/12th rulers, Moon, Saturn, Mars, Nemesis, and hard contacts. Use as astrological caution, not medical diagnosis.
- **Creativity/children**: prioritize 5th ruler, Sun, Venus, Jupiter, and aspects to 5th-house evidence.

## Pair / Synastry Use

When `PAIR_INTERACTION_PACKET western` exists:

1. Use only packet-listed cross aspects/overlays/hidden contacts.
2. Name direction: A planet of which person touches B planet/house of which person.
3. Sort weight: luminary/angle/chart ruler/Venus/Mars/Saturn contacts before minor contacts.
4. Separate **support factors** and **friction factors**. Do not collapse into one score.
5. House overlay is asymmetric. A’s Venus in B’s 7th is not the same statement as B’s Venus in A’s 7th.
6. If composite/Davison packet is absent, say relationship-as-entity chart is not supplied, but still read synastry evidence already supplied.

## Electional / Date Selection Use

For Western-style date selection, if a packet later supplies electional fields:

- Prefer Moon applying to benefic or topic lord, not separating from all support.
- Avoid Moon applying hard to malefic for the action topic unless the action needs cutting, surgery, confrontation, or demolition.
- Strengthen the Asc ruler and the ruler of the action house.
- Good aspect is not enough if the ruler of the action is debilitated, cadent, combust, or blocked.
- Timing must be tied to packet-supplied date/time. Do not create electional search results manually.

## Output Discipline

- Every interpretation sentence must include at least one chart-specific anchor: planet, aspect, house, dignity, lot, angle, or named packet field.
- Do not say “good/bad” alone. Say what it does: supports income, delays marriage, sharpens speech, drains health routine, improves career visibility, etc.
- If data is missing, name the exact missing layer and still read the valid layer. Avoid long system-audit language in user readings.
