# Western Temperament, Talent, Appearance, And Personal Style Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Western. It condenses classical, medieval, early-modern, public-domain modern bridge, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, or expose raw source text.

## Purpose

Use this pack for personality, temperament, strengths, weaknesses, talent, skill, charisma, appearance, body style, personal brand, and "what kind of person is this chart" questions.

Do not answer from Sun sign alone. With reliable birth time, use Ascendant, chart ruler, planets in/around the 1st, Moon, Sun, Mercury, Venus, Mars, dignity, aspects, sect, and dominant planets. If birth time is missing, close Ascendant, houses, angles, lots, sect, and chart ruler.

## Core Chain

1. **Ascendant and chart ruler**: first impression, body style, behavioral steering.
2. **Planets in or aspecting the 1st**: visible traits and immediate reaction style.
3. **Sun and Moon**: vitality/visibility and emotional rhythm.
4. **Mercury/Venus/Mars**: mind/speech, charm/taste, drive/assertion.
5. **Dominant planets and dispositors**: repeated personal agenda.
6. **Dignity, bounds, triplicity, face, sect, angularity**: whether the trait becomes usable strength or unstable habit.
7. **Main aspects and patterns**: how traits combine or conflict.

## Temperament / Personality

Separate:

- how the person appears,
- how the mind/emotions operate,
- what drives decisions,
- what becomes attractive or difficult,
- what matures with discipline.

Do not flatten mixed charts into a list of generic adjectives. Tie every trait to a planet/house/ruler/aspect/dignity.

## Talent / Skill

Use Mercury for learning/speech/analysis, Venus for art/design/harmony, Mars for action/tools/competition, Jupiter for teaching/counsel/law, Saturn for structure/endurance, Sun for leadership/visibility, Moon for care/adaptation.

Confirm talent through 2nd/3rd/5th/6th/10th/11th, dignity, aspects, and timing when supplied.

## Appearance / Charisma / Personal Brand

Use Ascendant, chart ruler, 1st-house planets, Venus/Sun/Moon, angularity, fixed-star hits only when supplied, and MC/10th for public brand.

No birth time: describe style from planet-sign-aspect patterns only, not body/Ascendant claims.

## Output Contract

Every answer using this pack must include:

- one Asc/chart-ruler or no-time substitute line,
- one Sun/Moon temperament line,
- one Mercury/Venus/Mars talent-style line,
- one dignity/aspect/dominant-planet line,
- one appearance/brand line with a time-known or no-time guard,
- one decisive practical verdict for the exact personality/talent/appearance question.
