## Mitigation Chain

1. Identify the stressed topic house/significator: 10th/MC, 2nd/8th/11th/Fortune, 5th/7th/Venus/Mars, 6th/12th, 3rd/9th, 4th, or Moon/Asc/chart ruler.
2. Identify pressure source: weak dignity, hard Mars/Saturn contact, retrograde/combustion if supplied, 6th/8th/12th pressure, afflicted topic ruler, or transit trigger.
3. Identify available support: chart ruler strength, benefic aspect/reception, dignity/minor dignity, sect support, lots, stabilizing house, or supportive transit window.
4. Convert into mitigation: reduce the exposed house risk, use the supportive planet/house behavior, and schedule action around the supplied timing fields.
5. If no birth time exists, do not prescribe house/angle remedies; use planet/sign/Moon/no-time substitutes only.

## Remedy Rules

- For work stress, strengthen 10th/MC/ruler actions: role clarity, authority management, public proof, and milestone planning.
- For money risk, use 2nd/8th/11th/Fortune evidence: budgeting, debt limits, contract review, reserves, and staged exposure.
- For relationship stress, use 5th/7th/Venus/Mars/Moon/Sun evidence: boundaries, repair conversations, pacing, and realistic expectations.
- For health/routine pressure, use 1st/6th/12th/Moon-Mars-Saturn evidence: rest, routine, check-up, workload limits, and professional support.
- Spiritual or symbolic practice may be framed as focus/support only; never as guaranteed astrological cure.

## Output Contract

For remedy/mitigation answers, include:

- one stressed-house/significator line naming the exact problem area,
- one pressure-source line naming dignity/aspect/house/transit evidence,
- one support-resource line naming planet/house/lots/aspect support,
- three concrete mitigation steps tied to chart evidence,
- one timing/precision line saying target-year/refDate/month/day/no-time support or unavailable,
