## Conversion Rule

Visibility is not sales. Require a chain:

- 3rd/5th/10th = message/content/public promise,
- 11th = audience/community,
- 7th = buyer/client/sponsor/counterparty,
- 2nd/Fortune = money received,
- 8th/12th/6th = cost, debt, fees, workload, leakage.

If 10th/11th are strong but 2nd/7th are weak, say "reach without clean conversion." If 2nd/7th are strong but 11th is weak, direct client sales beat mass content.
