## Purpose

Use this pack for documents, contracts, permits, licenses, visa/immigration, court, tax, inheritance papers, compliance, audit, applications, official approval, government offices, certifications, and institutional decisions.

Do not answer these questions from one house or one transit. Split paperwork, authority, counterparty/opponent, fees/shared money, travel/foreign lane, and timing.

## Authority And Document Chain

Read in this order:

1. **Ascendant / chart ruler or no-time substitute**: capacity to manage the process and respond.
2. **3rd house / Mercury**: paperwork, applications, messages, signatures, evidence, short-form documents.
3. **9th house / Jupiter**: law, visa, immigration, higher institutions, foreign affairs, courts of principle, certification.
4. **10th house / MC / Sun / Saturn**: government, authority, regulator, public status, official decision.
5. **7th house**: contract, counterparty, open opponent, agency-facing agreement.
6. **8th house**: tax, inheritance, shared money, insurance, debt, hidden liability.
7. **2nd/4th/12th houses**: fees/cash, property papers, foreign confinement/loss/behind-the-scenes delay.
8. **Dignity, sect, reception, aspects, lots, and supplied transits/refDate**: whether the document path can carry the requested action.

## Topic Rules

- **Visa / immigration / foreign permit**: 9th + 12th + 3rd paperwork + 10th authority; use 7th if a specific agency/consulate is the counterparty.
- **License / approval / certification**: 3rd documents + 10th authority + 9th institution/law.
- **Court / legal dispute**: 7th opponent + 9th law + 10th judgment/authority + 8th liability; do not promise a win from one benefic.
- **Tax / inheritance / insurance**: 8th first, then 2nd cash, 4th property, 10th authority, 3rd documents.
- **Contract signing**: 7th counterparty + 3rd signatures/clauses + Mercury condition + 10th enforceability.
