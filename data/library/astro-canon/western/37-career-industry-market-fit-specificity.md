# Western Career, Industry, Market, And Role-Fit Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Western. It condenses classical, medieval, early-modern, public-domain modern bridge, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, or expose raw source text.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks what career, profession, business type, industry, market, niche, role, shop, service, product lane, or monetizable skill suits them.

Do not answer from MC, Sun sign, or one planet alone. Split talent, role, market, money model, operating burden, public visibility, people channel, asset base, and timing.

## Fit Chain

1. **Ascendant/chart ruler or no-time substitute**: core operating style, pressure tolerance, and how the native carries work.
2. **MC/10th**: profession, role, title, authority, institution, public duty, business model, and career status.
3. **2nd / Fortune / 8th**: earning model, retained money, pricing power, debt/liability, investor/partner cost, and whether the industry retains value.
4. **3rd / Mercury**: writing, speaking, code, sales language, documents, teaching, consulting, trade, media, and skill transmission.
5. **6th**: daily work, service load, operations, employees, maintenance, health cost, and repetitive execution.
6. **7th / 11th**: clients, customers, partners, platforms, audience, community, network gains, and social proof.
7. **4th / 12th**: property, premises, backend, hidden work, retreat, institutions, data/archives, sleep cost, and behind-the-scenes burden.
8. **Dignity/sect/reception/lots/aspects**: whether the career significators can act directly or need support.
9. **Timing**: supplied transits and return cycles decide when to specialize, switch, launch, consolidate, or wait.

## Fit Verdict Rule

Career fit is not the same as money fit.

- If MC/10th is strong but 2nd/Fortune is weak, the role suits status but money retention needs structure.
- If 2nd/Fortune is strong but MC/10th is weak, money can come from trade/assets/client work but career identity may feel unstable.
- If 3rd/Mercury is strong, use communication, teaching, writing, code, sales, consulting, documentation, or media as a bridge.
- If 7th/11th is stressed, avoid people-heavy industries or use contracts/SOPs before scaling.
- If 6th/12th/8th repeats pressure, avoid high-maintenance/high-liability industries even if the public role looks attractive.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close houses, MC/Asc, lots, and house-ruler claims unless the packet supplies a reliable fallback. Use Sun/Moon/Mercury/Venus/Mars/Jupiter/Saturn condition, aspects, dignity, element/modality balance, and timing only; frame industry fit as lower precision.
