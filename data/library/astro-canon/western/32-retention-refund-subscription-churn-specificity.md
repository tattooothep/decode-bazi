## Retention-Chain Rule

New sales are not retention. Require a chain:

- MC/10th/6th = offer promise and service delivery,
- 3rd/Mercury = onboarding/support/billing communication,
- 7th = customer/subscriber/platform relation,
- 11th = user base and recurring gains,
- 2nd/Fortune = payment received,
- 4th/11th = base retained,
- 8th/12th/6th = refund, fee, churn, service burden, hidden loss.

If 2nd/11th are strong but 8th/12th are pressured, call "revenue with leakage." If 7th is pressured, customer relationship or platform conflict drives churn. If 3rd/Mercury is weak, fix onboarding and billing explanation before scaling.

## Refund / Cancellation Rule

For refund/cancel/chargeback questions, name the pressure:

- **billing confusion**: 3rd/Mercury + 2nd/8th,
- **customer dissatisfaction**: 7th + 10th/6th,
- **support overload**: 6th + 12th,
- **cash leakage**: 2nd/Fortune + 8th/12th,
- **community churn**: 11th under pressure.

Do not promise zero refunds. Give a symbolic risk level and a practical retention action.
