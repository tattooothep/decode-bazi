# Jyotish Tax, Accounting, Bookkeeping, And Compliance Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about tax filing, VAT, GST, withholding tax, accounting, bookkeeping, invoices, receipts, P&L, balance sheet, reconciliation, audit trail, financial records, accountant, filing deadline, compliance, tax penalty, or whether to clean books, file now, delay filing, hire accountant, or prepare for audit.

Do not treat tax/accounting as the same thing as cashflow/runway, unit economics, inventory, pricing, marketing, general legal approval, generic debt risk, or generic business. If the user asks only "cash left / burn / reserve", use the cashflow pack. If the user asks "court / visa / permit / license / lawsuit", use the authority/legal pack unless accounting records, tax filing, VAT/GST, invoices, or bookkeeping are the core issue.

## Canon Routing

Jyotish tax/accounting judgment must join Lagna/Moon, 3rd/Mercury, 10th/D10, 2nd/11th, 4th, 7th, 6th, 8th, 9th, and 12th/Rahu-Ketu/Mars-Saturn. Tax is a record, duty, and liability question, not only a cash question.

- Lagna/Moon: whether the owner can keep financial discipline, deadlines, and proof without avoidance.
- 3rd/Mercury: invoice number, receipt, ledger, reconciliation, message to accountant, document trail, and data entry.
- 10th/D10: compliance responsibility, filing process, accounting policy, and formal business duty.
- 2nd/11th: taxable revenue, tax reserve, VAT/GST output/input, withholding, payable/refundable tax, and cash impact.
- 4th: retained records, stored receipts, fixed assets, depreciation base, and old-book cleanup.
- 7th: accountant, auditor, tax officer, customer/vendor documents, counterparty invoice terms, and filing representation.
- 6th: bookkeeper, admin, accounting software, data-entry workflow, payroll support, and monthly close execution.
- 8th: tax liability, back tax, shared-money tax, penalty, audit exposure, and insurance/loan/tax entanglement.
- 9th: law, regulator, formal filing standard, government portal, and ethical duty.
- 12th/Rahu-Ketu/Mars-Saturn: hidden document loss, late filing, fines, back-office backlog, and avoidable penalty.
- Dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga when packet-supplied: when to file, amend, clean books, hire accountant, reserve tax, or face audit pressure.

## Answer Contract

For tax/accounting questions, answer with:

1. one Lagna/Moon financial-discipline/deadline line;
2. one 3rd/Mercury invoice/receipt/ledger/reconciliation line;
3. one 10th/D10 tax-compliance/filing-process line;
4. one 2nd/11th taxable-revenue/VAT-GST/withholding/tax-reserve line;
5. one 4th retained-record/fixed-asset/old-book line;
6. one 7th accountant/auditor/tax-office/counterparty-doc line;
7. one 6th bookkeeping/software/admin-execution line;
8. one 8th/12th/Rahu-Ketu/Mars-Saturn audit/penalty/missing-document/back-tax-risk line;
9. one timing and action verdict: file now, amend, clean books first, hire accountant, reserve tax, reconcile monthly, separate personal/business accounts, prepare audit folder, or delay only with documented reason.

Do not turn 2nd/11th money strength into tax safety. If Mercury, 6th, 8th, or 12th is stressed, require better records before expansion, filing, or claiming deductions.
