## Purpose

Use this pack for friends, siblings, coworkers, staff, employees, clients, business partners, bosses, elders, patrons, teams, social networks, and collaboration questions.

Do not answer from one graha or one bhava alone. Use bhava, lord, karaka, graha condition, varga, bala/bindu, dasha, and gochara.

3. **Natural karaka**: Mercury for communication/contracts, Venus for harmony, Jupiter for patrons/guidance, Saturn for staff/hierarchy/duty, Mars for conflict/competition, Rahu for outsiders/unusual networks.

If birth time is missing, close Lagna-dependent bhava/lord/varga-house claims unless packet explicitly supplies a reliable fallback. Use Moon, planetary condition, and dasha only when available.

## Siblings / Peers / Effort · 3rd

Use 3rd bhava/lord, Mars/Mercury, Moon, and dasha for siblings, peers, messaging, initiative, and short coordination.

## Coworkers / Employees / Conflict · 6th

Use 6th bhava/lord, Saturn/Mars/Mercury, upachaya strength, and D10 when supplied for coworkers, staff, service, competition, disputes, and operational burden.

Strong 6th can defeat problems but may also create conflict-heavy environments. Separate capacity to manage people from ease with people.

## Clients / Partners / Public Counterpart · 7th

Use 7th bhava/lord for client, contract partner, open counterpart, consultant, supplier, and public-facing people. Read romantically only when the user asks about love/marriage.

Check 2nd/10th/11th links for money/work gains and 6th/8th/12th links for debt, dispute, hidden pressure, or loss.

## Boss / Elders / Institution · 10th and 9th

Use 10th/D10 for boss/workplace/status and 9th/Jupiter/Sun for mentors, elders, teachers, and institutional blessing.

## Friends / Network / Gains · 11th
