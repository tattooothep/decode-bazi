# Jyotish Property, Vehicle, Residence, And Relocation Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Vedic/Jyotish. It condenses Parashari/Jataka, dasha, varga, ashtakavarga, gochara, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about buying, selling, renting, building, renovating, land, house, condo, shopfront, vehicle, mortgage, title deed, moving home, relocation, foreign move, or whether a residence/property is good for them.

Do not answer property questions from wealth houses alone. Separate sukha/residence quality, ownership, cashflow, loan/debt, documents/counterparty, move pressure, and dasha/gocara timing.

## Evidence Chain

1. **Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon**: capacity, comfort, mental readiness; if no time, use Moon/rashi/nakshatra fallback only.
2. **4th bhava/lord**: home, land, property, vehicle, mother/family root, sukha.
3. **Karaka**: Moon/Venus for comfort/vehicle, Mars for land/building, Saturn for old property/delay/structure, Mercury for papers, Jupiter for protection/advice.
4. **2nd and 11th**: savings and gains supporting purchase or sale.
5. **8th and 12th**: loan, mortgage, tax, insurance, hidden cost, loss, foreign/away residence.
6. **3rd / 9th / 12th**: short move, long-distance/foreign move, retreat/settlement away from root.
7. **7th**: seller, buyer, landlord, tenant, bank/agent/contractor as counterparty.
8. **D9 and D10 when supplied**: maturity/support and career-driven property decision; use other vargas only when supplied.
9. **Shadbala, ashtakavarga, dasha, gochara**: strength and timing activation.

If birth time is missing, close bhava/lord/varga-house/dasha-heavy claims unless the packet explicitly supplies a reliable fallback.

## Property / Land / House

Use 4th bhava/lord first. Then check 2nd/11th for affordability/gains and 8th/12th for debt/hidden cost.

Separate:

- comfort and suitability,
- ownership/retention,
- land/building condition,
- vehicle if asked,
- affordability and cash reserve,
- mortgage/debt/tax/insurance,
- counterparty/document friction,
- whether the period supports purchase, sale, rent, repair, or delay.

Strong 4th with weak 2nd/11th means the property can be suitable but financing is the pressure point. Strong 2nd/11th with afflicted 4th means money may be available but selection, comfort, or hidden defects need caution.

## Vehicle

Use 4th for vehicle, Venus/Moon for comfort/use, Mars for machinery/accident heat, Saturn for maintenance/old vehicle/delay, 2nd/8th for payment/insurance, and dasha/gocara for timing.

Separate buy, repair, change model, reduce budget, or wait.

## Relocation / Foreign Residence

Use 4th for base, 3rd/9th/12th for movement distance, Rahu/Saturn/Moon for foreign/away-from-root patterns, and 10th/D10 when work drives the move.

Separate local move, foreign move, work move, family move, temporary residence, and permanent settlement.
