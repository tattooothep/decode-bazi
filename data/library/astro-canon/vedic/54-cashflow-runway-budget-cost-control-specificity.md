## Purpose

Use this pack for cashflow, runway, burn rate, monthly expenses, fixed costs, budget, cost cutting, hiring freeze, AI cost, ad cost, SaaS cost, inventory cost, tax reserve, reserve cash, accounts receivable, delayed payment, receivables collection, refund reserve, debt service, and "will the business have enough cash" questions.

Do not treat cashflow/runway as the same thing as price strategy, windfall/speculation, generic investment, sales-team QA, or customer retention. Split money coming in, money staying, fixed outflow, delayed receivables, hidden leakage, and timing.

## Core Chain

1. **Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon**: owner discipline, stress tolerance, and ability to enforce budget decisions.
2. **2nd bhava/lord and dhanayoga indicators when supplied**: cash on hand, savings, billing reliability, and retained money.
3. **11th bhava/lord**: gains, recurring revenue, subscribers, collection pool, and whether growth supports cashflow.
4. **4th bhava/lord**: reserve base, fixed commitments, property/infrastructure, office, stored assets, and what remains.
5. **10th/D10 and Saturn**: operating model, management authority, policy, controls, and whether cuts can be enforced.
6. **3rd/Mercury/2nd/9th**: invoices, accounting reports, payment instructions, tax/compliance documents, and collection communication.
7. **7th/Venus/Jupiter**: customers, vendors, platforms, counterparties, accounts receivable, payment terms, and delayed payment.
8. **6th bhava/lord**: staff, contractors, tools, systems, payroll, daily operations, AI/SaaS/ad cost, and debt service discipline.
9. **8th/12th/Rahu-Ketu/Mars-Saturn**: tax, debt, refunds, chargebacks, hidden loss, emergency cost, and leakage.
10. **Dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga**: timing and whether the cash promise is active.

## Runway Rule

Runway requires both cash and retention:

- 2nd = money received and cash held,
- 11th = recurring gains,
- 4th = reserve/fixed base,
- 10th/D10 = operating discipline,
- 3rd/Mercury/9th = invoice/accounting/tax documentation,
- 7th = customer/vendor payment terms,
- 6th = staff/tool/platform burn and debt service,
- 8th/12th = debt, refund, tax, hidden loss.

If 2nd/11th are supported but 4th is weak, revenue comes but reserve does not build. If 4th is supported but 2nd/11th are weak, protect base and improve collection. If 6th/8th/12th dominate, operating burn and liabilities must be capped before expansion.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close Lagna-dependent bhava, varga-house, and exact dasha claims unless packet supplies a reliable fallback. Use Moon sign/nakshatra, graha condition, and any packet-provided dasha only; avoid confident runway length claims that depend on bhavas.

## Cost-Cut Rule

Name the cost lane:

- **staff/tool burn**: 6th pressure,
- **vendor/customer term leak**: 7th pressure,
- **invoice/report/tax confusion**: 3rd/Mercury/9th pressure,
- **fixed commitments/infrastructure**: 4th pressure,
- **debt/refund/tax/emergency**: 8th/12th pressure,
- **growth without retained cash**: 10th/11th stronger than 2nd/4th.

Good cash evidence allows controlled reinvestment. Strong leak evidence requires freeze, cap, collect, renegotiate, or reserve.
