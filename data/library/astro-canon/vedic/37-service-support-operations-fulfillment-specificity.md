## Purpose

Use this pack for customer support, service quality, complaint handling, ticket backlog, delivery, fulfillment, onboarding, SLA, operations workload, staff capacity, service recovery, product delivery, and "can we serve customers well" questions.

Do not answer from sales, marketing, price, or reputation alone. Split demand, service promise, staff capacity, customer pressure, communication, operating cost, bottleneck, and timing.

## Core Chain

1. **Capacity and owner**: Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon show ability to carry service pressure, complaint load, and repeated operational work. If birth time is missing, use Moon and graha condition only.
2. **Support message and ticket flow**: 3rd bhava/Mercury and 2nd speech show reply speed, documentation, scripts, ticket clarity, escalation wording, and onboarding instructions.
3. **Customer pressure**: 7th bhava/lord shows customer, client, subscriber, platform counterparty, complaint source, and whether recovery is accepted.
4. **Service promise and standard**: 10th/D10 show official promise, quality standard, process discipline, and whether the service claim can be fulfilled.
5. **Staff and workload**: 6th bhava/lord shows employees, queues, repetitive work, support burden, defects, disputes, and operational friction.
6. **Gains and community**: 11th shows user base, community helpers, repeat users, and whether users support or amplify complaints.
7. **Cost, reserve, and leakage**: 2nd/11th show support budget and recovery; 8th shows refunds/liability; 12th shows hidden loss, burnout, and unseen backlog. Saturn/Mars/Rahu/Ketu pressure raises operational friction.
8. **Varga and bala**: D10, shadbala, ashtakavarga/bindu when supplied show service durability and scaling capacity.
9. **Timing**: dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga activation decides whether to expand service, pause intake, fix SOP, automate, hire, or narrow the promise.

## Service-Chain Rule

A sale is not a delivered result. Require a chain:

- 3rd/Mercury/2nd = support wording, ticket flow, documentation,
- 7th = customer expectation and complaint pressure,
- 10th/D10 = service promise and SOP,
- 6th = staff, queue, defects, workload,
- 2nd/11th/8th = support cost, reserve, liability,
- 12th/Saturn-Mars-Rahu = hidden leak, burnout, unseen backlog.

If 7th is pressured while 3rd/Mercury is weak, customers complain because communication and recovery are unclear. If 10th/D10 is strong but 6th/12th are weak, the promise is good but operations cannot carry the load. If 2nd/11th/8th are weak, do not expand service before fixing delivery cost and refund exposure.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close Lagna-dependent bhava, D10, and house-lord claims unless packet supplies a reliable fallback. Use Moon, Mercury, Saturn/Mars/Rahu/Ketu condition, nakshatra, and dasha only when available.
