## Purpose

Use this pack for subscription, membership, renewal, repeat purchase, refund, cancellation, chargeback, customer churn, recurring revenue, retained customer base, customer service burden, and "will users stay or cancel" questions.

Do not answer from sales or popularity alone. Split new customer acquisition, renewal, refund pressure, customer-service load, cash retention, and timing.

## Core Chain

1. **Service-pressure capacity**: Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon show self, mind, and capacity to carry repeated customer load. If birth time is missing, use Moon and graha condition only.
2. **Offer and delivery promise**: 10th/D10 and 6th show service obligation, work quality, operational burden, and whether the product can keep the promise.
3. **Customer counterparty**: 7th bhava/lord shows subscribers, customers, sponsors, contracts, platform, refund opponent, and chargeback counterparty.
4. **Cash and recurring gains**: 2nd is payment/stored wealth; 11th is gains, repeat patrons, and fulfillment; 4th can show retained base when reliable; 8th shows refunds, fees, chargebacks, tax, liability, and shared-risk money.
5. **Support communication**: 3rd/Mercury and 2nd speech show onboarding, billing explanation, help docs, replies, tickets, and dispute messages.
6. **Leak and burden**: 6th complaints/service load/enemies; 8th liability; 12th loss/refund drain; Saturn/Mars/Rahu/Ketu pressure shows churn or support strain.
7. **Varga and bala**: D10, shadbala, ashtakavarga/bindu when supplied show durability of delivery and retention.
8. **Timing**: dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga activation decides whether the matter is temporary leakage, repair phase, or structural churn.

## Retention-Chain Rule

New sales are not retention. Require a chain:

- 10th/D10/6th = offer promise and service delivery,
- 3rd/Mercury/2nd = onboarding/support/billing communication,
- 7th = customer/subscriber/platform relation,
- 11th = repeat gains and user base,
- 2nd = payment received,
- 4th/11th = base retained,
- 8th/12th/6th = refund, liability, churn, service burden, hidden loss.

If 2nd/11th are strong but 8th/12th are pressured, call "revenue with leakage." If 7th is pressured, customer relationship or platform conflict drives churn. If 3rd/Mercury is weak, fix onboarding and billing explanation before scaling.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close Lagna-dependent bhava, D10, and house-lord claims unless packet supplies a reliable fallback. Use Moon, Mercury, Venus/Jupiter, Saturn/Mars/Rahu/Ketu condition, nakshatra, and dasha only when available.

## Refund / Cancellation Rule

For refund/cancel/chargeback questions, name the pressure:

- **billing confusion**: 3rd/Mercury/2nd + 8th,
- **customer dissatisfaction**: 7th + 10th/6th,
- **support overload**: 6th + Saturn/Mars,
- **cash leakage**: 2nd/11th + 8th/12th,
- **structural churn**: 11th/4th weak under active dasha/gocara.

Do not promise zero refunds. Give a symbolic risk level and a practical retention action.
