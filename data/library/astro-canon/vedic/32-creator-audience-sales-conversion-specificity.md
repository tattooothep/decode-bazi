## Purpose

Use this pack for content creator, social media, livestream, online selling, audience growth, followers, fans, customer conversion, public trust, marketing campaign, launch, and "will people buy from me" questions.

Do not answer from fame, Jupiter, or Rahu alone. Split visibility, message, trust, audience, sales conversion, retention, and cost.

## Core Chain

1. **Capacity to present**: Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon show public composure, consistency, emotional rhythm, and ability to handle attention.
2. **Message and content**: 3rd bhava/Mercury for posting, writing, speaking, comments, and daily content effort; 5th for creativity, entertainment, intelligence, and campaign ideas.
3. **Public role and authority**: 10th/D10/Sun for professional standing, public promise, and platform role.
4. **Audience and gains**: 11th/Jupiter/Venus/Mercury/Rahu for followers, patrons, network reach, and gains through groups.
5. **Customer and conversion**: 7th for buyers, clients, sponsors, collaborations; 2nd for cash/voice/pricing; 8th for platform fees, taxes, investor/shared risk, hidden cost.
6. **Leak and workload**: 6th for service load/competition, 12th for loss/rest drain, Saturn/Mars/Rahu for pressure or over-scaling.
7. **Timing**: dasha/antardasha and gochara decide whether to publish, test, scale, or wait.

## Conversion Rule

Visibility is not sales. Require a chain:

- 3rd/5th/10th = message/content/public promise,
- 11th = audience/community,
- 7th = buyer/client/sponsor/counterparty,
- 2nd = money and pricing,
- 8th/12th/6th = hidden cost, fees, debt, competition, overwork.

If 10th/11th are strong but 2nd/7th are weak, say "reach without clean conversion." If 2nd/7th are strong but 11th is weak, direct clients or referrals beat mass audience.

For livestream/campaign/launch questions, name whether the chart favors:

Do not promise virality, follower counts, revenue numbers, or ad ROI.
