# Vedic / Jyotish · topic packs for Fusion

Source use:
- Derived operating framework from BPHS working summaries plus public-domain OCR corpus: Brihat Jataka and Vijnananda/Bhattotpala/commentary witnesses, Saravali, Phaladeepika/Phala Deepika witnesses, Jataka Parijata, Uttara Kalamrita, Jaimini Sutras, Laghu/Madhya Parashari, Sambhu Hora, Bhava Kutuhala/Bhava Prakasha, Jyotisha/Jyotir/Nav Ratnamala, Prasna Marga/Tantra/Manorama/Ratna/Skandhasthi witnesses, Shatpanchasika, Tajika/Varsha Phala, Ashtakavarga/Nakshatra/Dasha witnesses, Siddhanta/Aryabhatiya/Jyotisha Siddhanta calculation witnesses, and Muhurta/Martanda/Kalaprakashika/Vivaha witnesses.
- Licensed/internal incoming witnesses include Hora Ratnam, Daivajna Vallabha, Samaya Shuddhi Viveka, Dasha Phala Vichara / Gochara Phala Vichara, Vedic Chronology/Vedanga Jyotisha, Daivagya Kamadhenu, Hora Shastra, Jyotisha Siddhanta Sangraha, Yavana Jataka, and selected B. Suryanarayana/Suryanarain Row witnesses as derived-summary-only support. Never quote them or expose source text/page order.
- This pack is not a raw transcription.
- Use only computed packet fields. Do not invent divisional charts, dasha periods, or yogas not supplied.

## General Weight Order

1. Lagna and Lagnesha when birth time is known.
2. Moon sign/nakshatra and Chandra Lagna support.
3. Topic bhava and its lord.
4. Karaka planet for the topic.
5. Dignity, combustion, retrograde, relation to sign lord.
6. D9/D10/other varga when supplied.
7. Vimshottari dasha/antardasha and gochara.
8. Shadbala/ashtakavarga as strength confirmation, not as override.
9. Classical topic rules as specificity layer.

Bhava Kutuhala/Bhava Prakasha witnesses may sharpen house-topic wording only after the packet confirms the house, lordship, placement, and strength. They must not create house claims in no-time mode.

Jyotisha/Jyotir/Nav Ratnamala witnesses may sharpen compilation-style topic language only after the packet confirms the relevant graha, bhava, lordship, strength, dasha, varga, or muhurta field. Treat them as corroboration, not an override.

Bhattotpala/Brihat Jataka commentary witnesses may sharpen graha/rashi/bhava/yoga interpretation only after the packet confirms the relevant graha, rashi, bhava, dignity, lordship, and cancellation context.

Hora Ratnam, Daivajna Vallabha, Daivagya Kamadhenu, Hora Shastra, and Yavana Jataka may sharpen concise natal result language after packet evidence exists. They are not authority to create missing bhava, varga, dasha, or yoga fields.

Row witnesses may sharpen their matching topic only after packet evidence exists: Stri Jataka for relationship/marriage patterns, Row Brihat Jataka and Sarvartha Chintamani as English collation, Astrological Self Instructor as terminology/training support, and Chappanna/Prasana Sastra only for marked prashna packets.

Phala Deepika alternate witnesses may sharpen topic-specific natal language after the same packet facts are present. They do not override dasha, varga, or functional lordship.

Surya Siddhanta, Siddhanta Shiromani, Aryabhatiya, Jyotisha Siddhanta Sangraha, and Vedanga Jyotisha witnesses are calculation provenance only. The AI must not recalculate positions, tithi, nakshatra, ayanamsa, or calendar factors.

Samaya Shuddhi Viveka may sharpen time-purity and electional calendar suitability only when panchang/electional fields exist.

If birth time is missing, do not use Lagna/bhava/varga houses unless the packet explicitly supplies a no-time fallback.

## Marriage / Relationship

Read:
- 7th bhava and 7th lord,
- Venus for relationship/pleasure, Jupiter as husband significator in female charts where used, Mars/Venus desire dynamics,
- Moon for emotional compatibility,
- D9/Navamsa when supplied,
- Stri Jataka/relationship-specific witnesses only as support after 7th bhava, Venus/Jupiter, Moon, D9, and dasha facts are present,
- Rahu/Ketu or Saturn/Mars pressure to 7th/Venus/Moon,
- current dasha of 7th lord, Venus, Moon, or planets placed in 7th.

Support:
- 7th lord strong, benefic influence to 7th/Venus, D9 reinforcement, supportive dasha.
- Moon/Venus supported by Jupiter or benefics without severe affliction.

Stress:
- 7th lord in 6/8/12 or afflicted,
- Venus/Moon under Saturn/Mars/Rahu/Ketu pressure,
- dasha lord connected to 6/8/12 or maraka without relief,
- D9 conflict.

Answer rule:
- Separate "attraction", "stability", and "timing readiness".
- Do not declare marriage/divorce from one factor.

## Career / Profession

Read:
- 10th bhava, 10th lord, planets in 10th,
- Lagnesha and 6th/11th for work/service/gains,
- Sun for authority, Saturn for responsibility, Mercury for trade/skill, Jupiter for teaching/advice, Mars for technical/action fields, Venus for design/arts/luxury,
- D10/Dashamsa when supplied,
- dasha/gocara hitting 10th lord, 10th house, Lagnesha, or D10 indicators.

Support:
- 10th lord strong or connected to Lagna/9th/11th,
- D10 repeats the same planet/topic,
- Saturn/Jupiter timing supports structure or expansion.

Stress:
- 10th lord afflicted/in dusthana without viparita use,
- dasha lord weak and connected to 8th/12th,
- Saturn/Mars/Rahu pressure without benefic support.

Answer rule:
- Name the work channel: authority, service, trade, analysis, teaching, foreign/backstage, technical/action, creative/beauty, property, finance.
- Do not answer "career good" without naming 10th lord/D10/dasha evidence.

## Wealth / Finance

Read:
- 2nd bhava/lord for assets and speech/family resource,
- 11th bhava/lord for gains/network,
- 5th/9th for fortune, skill, speculation, merit,
- Jupiter/Venus/Mercury for wealth channels,
- Rahu in 2nd/11th as unusual/nonlinear gain-risk,
- dasha of 2nd/11th/5th/9th lords,
- ashtakavarga support for signs involved.

Support:
- 2nd/11th lords strong or linked to Lagna/9th/10th,
- dhana yoga links between 2/5/9/11,
- D9/D10 or dasha confirms.

Stress:
- 2nd/11th afflicted by dusthana lords/malefics without relief,
- 12th/8th connection causing leakage/debt/tax/hidden loss,
- Rahu/Ketu amplifying desire or volatility.

Answer rule:
- Separate "earning", "saving", "windfall/speculation", and "leakage".
- Do not let one wealth yoga erase dasha or 12th/8th drain.

## Health / Risk

Read:
- 1st/Lagna body, 6th disease, 8th chronic/crisis, 12th hospital/rest/isolation,
- Moon for mind/sleep, Sun vitality, Saturn chronic, Mars inflammation/injury, Rahu/Ketu unusual symptoms,
- dasha/gocara activating 1/6/8/12,
- shadbala/ashtakavarga for resilience.

Support:
- Lagna/Lagnesha strong,
- benefic influence to 1st/6th/8th,
- strong Moon and routine-supporting Saturn.

Stress:
- dasha lord in/owning 6/8/12 and afflicted,
- Moon/Saturn/Rahu patterns around sleep, anxiety, chronic pressure,
- Mars/Rahu/Ketu to 1st/6th/8th for acute/inflammatory/cut risk.

Answer rule:
- Use medical-safe language: risk signal, check-up, routine, sleep, professional advice.
- No diagnosis, death prediction, or surgery instruction.

## Dasha / Yoga

Declare yoga only when the packet provides:
- exact planets,
- house ownership,
- placement,
- dignity/strength,
- cancellation or affliction context when available.

If cancellation is not checked, call it "candidate".

Dasha judgment:
- A planet gives results of its lordship, placement, aspects, nakshatra connection, dignity, and varga condition.
- Mahadasha sets the field; antardasha triggers the sub-topic.
- Good planet in bad house can give mixed results; difficult planet with functional support can produce useful outcomes.
- Ashtottari Dasha and Graha Dasha Phalam witnesses are support layers only when that dasha system/period/lord is in the packet. Do not introduce a second dasha system if the packet only supplies Vimshottari.
- Dasha Phala Vichara / Gochara Phala Vichara can sharpen event-language only after the packet provides dasha or transit fields; it cannot create timing windows by itself.

Timing answer must name:
- current mahadasha and antardasha,
- the houses/lordships of those planets,
- whether gochara repeats or contradicts the dasha promise.

## Prashna / Horary

Use only when the packet explicitly marks a question-time/prashna chart or supplies question-time fields.

Read:
- question Lagna and its lord,
- Moon condition,
- topic house and house lord,
- benefic/malefic influence to the topic,
- applying/separating condition if supplied,
- relevant prashna-specific indicators from the packet.

Answer rule:
- Do not convert a natal chart into prashna.
- If the user asks a yes/no question but the packet is natal-only, answer from natal/dasha tendency and state that prashna timing is unavailable.
- Use Prasna Marga/Tantra/Manorama/Ratna, Chappanna or Prasana Sastra, Skandhasthi Prashna, 120 Prashna Grantha, Shatpanchasika, and Bhrigu prashna sources as specificity support only after packet facts are present.

## Annual / Tajika / Varshaphala

Use only when annual-chart fields are supplied: annual Lagna, Muntha, annual lord, sahama, Tajika aspects/yogas, or a marked varshaphala packet.

Read:
- natal promise first,
- annual Lagna/Muntha and annual lord,
- Tajika aspect/yoga fields if supplied,
- dasha/gochara agreement,
- asked topic house in the annual chart.

Answer rule:
- Do not invent annual chart factors from a natal-only packet.
- If the user asks a future year and only natal+dasha+gochara exist, answer from those layers and say annual Tajika layer is not supplied.

## Muhurta / Electional Date Selection

Use only when the packet supplies an electional date/time, tithi, nakshatra, weekday, yoga/karana, lagna or activity-class fields.

Read:
- activity class first,
- tithi/nakshatra/weekday/yoga/karana fit,
- electional Lagna and Lagna lord if supplied,
- Moon condition and asked-topic house if supplied,
- conflict factors such as dusthana, maraka, combustion, weak Moon, or activity-specific prohibitions when supplied by the packet.

Answer rule:
- Do not use Muhurta Darpana, Muhurta Sindhu, Muhurta Ganapati, Kalaprakashika, or other electional witnesses to rewrite a natal chart.
- Use Vivaha-focused witnesses only for marriage electional suitability or marriage-timing questions when the packet supplies matching tithi/nakshatra/weekday/yoga/karana/lagna/activity fields.
- Use added Jyotisha Muhurta and Muhurta Martanda witnesses as electional tie-breakers only when the action class and calendar factors are supplied.
- Use Samaya Shuddhi Viveka only for time-purity/calendar suitability after the packet supplies panchang/electional factors. Do not let it override natal promise or dasha.
- If the user asks for a birth-chart reading, ignore muhurta rules unless they ask for a date/time to act.
- For date selection, name the concrete activity and the concrete calendar factors; avoid generic “good day” language.

## Ashtakavarga

Use only when the packet supplies SAV/BAV/ashtakavarga fields.

Read:
- bindu support for the sign/house involved,
- whether transit/gochara moves through a supported or weak sign,
- whether ashtakavarga agrees with dasha and house lordship.
- Jyotisha Vichara Vivaha-Ashtakavarga can sharpen marriage/ashtakavarga wording only after BAV/SAV or marriage-electional fields are supplied.

Answer rule:
- Ashtakavarga confirms resilience or friction; it does not erase severe 6/8/12, maraka, affliction, weak dasha, or varga problems.

## No-Time Mode

Without birth time:
- Use Sun, Moon if stable, graha rashi, nakshatra if stable, and broad planetary aspects.
- Avoid Lagna, bhava, exact varga, exact Vimshottari if Moon position is uncertain.
- State alternatives if Moon/nakshatra changes that day.
- Nakshatra Ratnakara-style support applies only if the packet confirms a stable Moon nakshatra or explicitly supplies a no-time nakshatra fallback.

Keep the answer specific by citing exact graha/rashi/nakshatra from the packet, not generic sign descriptions.
