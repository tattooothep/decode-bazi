# Jyotish Loan, Credit, Debt, And Refinance Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about loans, credit approval, credit cards, debt repayment, debt restructuring, refinancing, installment plans, interest rate, principal, collateral, guarantor, creditor pressure, collection, default, delinquency, or whether to borrow, refinance, restructure, consolidate debt, pay down debt, negotiate with creditors, or avoid new debt.

Do not treat loan/credit/debt as the same thing as cashflow/runway, unit economics, inventory, tax/accounting, property purchase, general legal approval, windfall/speculation, or generic business. If the question is only "cash left / burn / reserve", use the cashflow pack. If it is only "buy house / sell land / move home", use the property pack. If it asks loan terms, repayment, refinance, collateral, guarantor, credit approval, default, or creditor pressure, this pack must be included.

## Canon Routing

Jyotish loan/debt judgment must join Lagna/Moon, 2nd/11th, 6th, 8th, 4th, 7th, 3rd/Mercury, 10th/D10, 12th, and Rahu-Ketu/Mars-Saturn. Debt is a roga-ripu-rna lane: it can fund action, but it also creates duty, pressure, and repayment karma.

- Lagna/Moon: borrower discipline, repayment capacity, and whether the owner can carry obligation without avoidance.
- 2nd/11th: cash-in, owned cash, gains available for installments, interest burden, and payment priority.
- 6th: debt, creditor pressure, repayment routine, service obligation, collection, disputes with lenders, and the ability to defeat debt.
- 8th: shared liability, hidden debt, refinance, penalty, tax/insurance entanglement, and sudden debt shock.
- 4th: collateral, reserve base, retained asset, mortgage/security value, and whether the foundation can support the obligation.
- 7th: creditor, bank officer, guarantor, co-borrower, partner, counterparty contract, and negotiation channel.
- 3rd/Mercury: application paperwork, credit file, debt statement, repayment schedule, communication record, and negotiation messages.
- 10th/D10: formal approval standard, business credit policy, public duty, and responsibility to maintain standing.
- 12th/Rahu-Ketu/Mars-Saturn: default, delinquency, collection, penalty interest, hidden fee, debt spiral, and avoidable loss.
- Dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga when packet-supplied: when to borrow, refinance, restructure, pay down, negotiate, avoid new debt, or face creditor pressure.

## Answer Contract

For loan/credit/debt/refinance questions, answer with:

1. one Lagna/Moon borrower-discipline/repayment-capacity line;
2. one 2nd/11th cash-in/installment/debt-service/interest line;
3. one 6th debt/creditor-pressure/repayment-routine line;
4. one 8th refinance/shared-liability/hidden-debt line;
5. one 4th collateral/reserve/asset-security line;
6. one 7th creditor/guarantor/co-borrower/counterparty-contract line;
7. one 3rd/Mercury application-document/credit-file/repayment-schedule line;
8. one 12th/Rahu-Ketu/Mars-Saturn default/penalty/hidden-fee/refinance-trap line;
9. one timing and action verdict: borrow, borrow only with cap, refinance, restructure, consolidate, negotiate, pay down first, avoid new debt, prepare documents, increase reserve, or wait.

Do not treat 2nd/11th gains as debt safety. If 6th, 8th, 12th, Rahu, Mars, or Saturn is stressed, require repayment discipline, written terms, reserve, and conservative borrowing.
