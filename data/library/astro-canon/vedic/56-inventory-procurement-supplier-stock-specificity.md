# Jyotish Inventory, Procurement, Supplier, And Stock Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about inventory, stock, stockout, overstock, procurement, purchase order, supplier, vendor lead time, MOQ, warehouse, import stock, safety stock, reorder point, raw material, or whether to buy/hold/clear goods.

Do not treat inventory/procurement as the same thing as cashflow/runway, unit economics, pricing, marketing traffic, customer support, sales-team QA, partner due diligence, or generic business. If the user asks only "cash left / burn / reserve", use the cashflow pack. If the user asks "profit per unit / margin / break-even", use the unit-economics pack. This pack is for physical or operational stock decisions and supplier/warehouse flow.

## Canon Routing

Jyotish inventory judgment must join Lagna/Moon, 4th, 2nd/11th, 10th/D10, 3rd/Mercury, 7th, 6th, 9th/12th, and 8th/12th/Rahu-Ketu/Mars-Saturn. Stock can appear as gain through 2nd/11th but become loss through 6th/8th/12th.

- Lagna/Moon: whether the owner can keep stock discipline and avoid emotional buying.
- 4th: warehouse, stored goods, inventory base, raw material, fixed stock, and goods trapped in place.
- 2nd/11th: capital tied in goods, stock value, purchase affordability, and cash recovered after sale.
- 10th/D10: operating system, purchasing policy, inventory process, and stock-to-sales rhythm.
- 3rd/Mercury: purchase order, SKU records, vendor communication, stock record, and reorder message.
- 7th: supplier/vendor/customer terms, consignment, preorder, deposit, and delivery promise.
- 6th: warehouse staff, packer, shipping handler, vendor execution, tool, system, inventory count, and service burden.
- 9th/12th: import stock, foreign supplier, long-distance logistics, customs, transit goods, and delivery route.
- 8th/12th/Rahu-Ketu/Mars-Saturn: stockout, overstock, spoilage, damage, theft, wrong SKU, customs delay, refund, and dead stock.
- Dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga when packet-supplied: when to buy, pause, clear, switch supplier, or reduce stock depth.

## Answer Contract

For inventory/procurement questions, answer with:

1. one Lagna/Moon stock-discipline line;
2. one 4th warehouse/inventory-base line;
3. one 2nd/11th capital-tied-in-stock line;
4. one 10th/D10 purchasing/process line;
5. one 3rd/Mercury purchase-order/record/reorder-message line;
6. one 7th supplier/customer-term line;
7. one 6th warehouse/vendor-execution line;
8. one 9th/12th import/logistics/transit line when relevant;
9. one 8th/12th/Rahu-Ketu/Mars-Saturn stockout/overstock/damage/dead-stock-risk line;
10. one timing and action verdict: buy now, hold, reduce SKU, clear old stock, preorder only, change supplier, or set safety stock/reorder point.

Do not translate 2nd/11th gain into safe stock. If dasha supports expansion but 6th/8th/12th are strained, require smaller test orders, preorder, or a stricter reorder point before bulk buying.
