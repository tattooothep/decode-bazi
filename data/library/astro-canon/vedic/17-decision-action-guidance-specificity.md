# Jyotish Decision, Action Guidance, And Go/Wait Specificity Pack

License mode: summary-only operational rule pack. Use for Hourkey runtime readings only. Do not expose raw OCR, licensed source prose, page order, or private source file paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks "ควรทำไหม", "เลือกทางไหนดี", "ลุยหรือรอก่อน", "ลาออกไหม", "ลงทุนไหม", "เริ่ม/หยุด/เลิกดีไหม", "ต้องทำยังไง", or any go/wait/choose/advice question in Jyotish.

The answer must convert Jyotish evidence into a practical action stance without inventing prashna, muhurta, alternate dasha, or annual chart fields.

## Decision Chain

1. Define the asked action and topic bhava: 10th/D10 for work, 2nd/11th/5th/9th for money/fortune, 7th/Venus/Jupiter/D9 for relationship, 6th/8th/12th for health/risk/debt, 3rd/9th/12th for movement/study/foreign, 4th for home/property.
2. Check natal permission: Lagna/Lagnesha when timed, Moon/nakshatra, asked bhava/lord, karaka, dignity, drishti, varga, shadbala/ashtakavarga.
3. Check timing: Vimshottari mahadasha/antardasha, supplied sub-periods, gochara, and ashtakavarga trigger.
4. Check blocker: dusthana/maraka tie, weak dasha lord, affliction to Moon/Lagna/asked lord, combustion, or no-time limitation.
5. Convert evidence into one of four action calls: go, conditional go, wait/prepare, or no.

## Action Rules

- "Go" requires usable natal promise plus dasha support and gochara confirmation.
- "Conditional go" fits when dasha supports the topic but dusthana/malefic/weak-bala factors require safeguards.
- "Wait/prepare" fits when natal promise exists but dasha/gocara timing is not yet supportive.
- "No" requires repeated contradiction from bhava/lord, dasha lord, and gochara/risk indicators.
- Do not turn natal data into prashna or muhurta certainty unless the packet supplies question/electional fields.
- Do not make medical, legal, or financial orders. Phrase as astrological risk/action guidance and advise professional confirmation for high-stakes decisions.

## Output Contract

For decision/action answers, include:

- one asked-action line naming the exact action and bhava/karaka,
- one support line from Lagna/Moon/topic lord/varga/bala,
- one blocker/risk line from dusthana/maraka/affliction/weak dasha,
- one timing line from dasha/gochara/ashtakavarga when supplied,
- one concrete action call: go / conditional go / wait / no,
- one practical next-step line tied to the chart evidence.
