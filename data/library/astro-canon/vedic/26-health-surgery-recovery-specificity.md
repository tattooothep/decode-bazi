# Jyotish Health, Surgery, Accident, Stress, And Recovery Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Vedic/Jyotish. It condenses Parashari/Jataka, dasha, varga, ashtakavarga, gochara, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about health, fatigue, chronic pressure, sleep, stress, accident risk, surgery timing, hospital/rest, check-up, recovery, whether to push work, or how to manage body strain.

This is symbolic risk-management, not medical diagnosis. If the user asks for diagnosis, medication, surgery instruction, or emergency triage, direct them to medical professionals while keeping the chart reading to dasha/gocara/routine caution.

## Evidence Chain

1. **Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon**: vitality, body-mind rhythm, resilience; if no time, use Moon/rashi/nakshatra fallback only.
2. **6th bhava/lord**: illness tendency, habits, service load, enemies, discipline, daily routine.
3. **8th bhava/lord**: chronic pressure, surgery/crisis symbolism, hidden vulnerability, transformation, insurance/debt around treatment.
4. **12th bhava/lord**: sleep, hospital/rest, isolation, retreat, loss, recovery environment.
5. **Saturn**: chronic load, delay, discipline, long recovery.
6. **Mars**: acute heat, cuts/injury symbolism, surgery/accident urgency.
7. **Rahu/Ketu**: irregularity, amplification, hidden/nonstandard pattern.
8. **Sun/Moon/Mercury/Jupiter/Venus**: vitality, mind/body rhythm, reports, physicians/advisors, recovery support when tied to bhava/dasha.
9. **D9/D10 and supplied varga**: maturity and work-driven strain only when supplied.
10. **Shadbala/ashtakavarga**: resilience and weakness confirmation, not diagnosis.
11. **Vimshottari dasha / gochara**: period pressure/support and timing activation.

If birth time is missing, close bhava/lord/varga-house/exact dasha-heavy medical timing unless the packet supplies a reliable fallback.

## Health Topic Rules

Separate:

- vitality/stamina,
- sleep and mental stress,
- work/routine burden,
- chronic pressure versus acute flare,
- accident/surgery symbolism,
- hospital/rest/recovery,
- whether to continue, reduce load, check up, rest, or delay.

Do not name a fixed disease from one graha, one bhava, one dasha lord, one yoga, or one transit. If the packet shows pressure, translate it into practical management advice.

## Surgery / Treatment / Hospital Guard

When the question asks about surgery, treatment, hospital, or procedure timing:

- judge symbolic timing ease/pressure only,
- name Lagna/Moon, 6th/8th/12th, Mars/Saturn/Rahu-Ketu, dasha, and gochara evidence,
- never choose a procedure, medication, diagnosis, dosage, or emergency instruction,
- do not override a clinician,
- give a practical call such as prepare, seek second opinion, reduce risk, avoid rushing if timing is pressured, or proceed only under medical clearance.
