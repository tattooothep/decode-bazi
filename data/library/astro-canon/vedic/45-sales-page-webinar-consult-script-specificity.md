# Jyotish Sales Page, Webinar, And Consultation Script Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Jyotish. It condenses public-domain and licensed-internal operational rules into usable rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about sales page, landing page copy, webinar script, workshop script, consultation script, discovery call, sales call, closing script, DM pitch, proposal pitch, VSL, launch presentation, pitch deck, objection handling, story angle, proof order, CTA wording, or how to write the sales conversation so buyers understand and buy.

Do not treat sales script fit as the same thing as marketing traffic, customer persona, price level, product category, guarantee boundary, or delivery model. This pack decides the persuasion structure, proof order, story angle, objection sequence, call-to-action, and whether the user should use a written sales page, live webinar, short consult call, DM close, or proof-first proposal.

## Sales Message Chain

1. **Lagna/Moon or no-time substitute**: the seller voice, confidence, pressure capacity, personal proof, and whether the person can carry a direct pitch or needs a quieter proof-led script.
2. **3rd/Mercury/2nd**: copy, speech, script, explanation, DM, FAQ, call flow, slides, objection wording, and whether the message is clear enough for the buyer.
3. **10th/D10/Sun/Saturn**: first impression, authority, status, public promise, expert positioning, and whether the page/webinar should lead with credentials, result, story, or diagnosis.
4. **7th/Venus**: direct buyer, sales call, objections, trust, agreement, close, and whether the script should qualify first, educate first, or ask for a decision quickly.
5. **11th**: webinar room, group audience, community reaction, affiliates, platform comments, and whether a group presentation or one-to-one close fits better.
6. **2nd/4th/8th/11th**: offer value, proof library, case assets, retained knowledge, bonus stack, payment action, and whether the CTA should emphasize money, asset, stability, or access.
7. **5th/9th/Jupiter/Moon/Venus**: desire, story, teaching frame, aspiration, creative hook, and the emotional reason the buyer trusts the pitch.
8. **6th/8th/12th/Saturn-Mars-Rahu-Ketu**: overclaim, fear-based copy, manipulative pressure, refund trigger, burnout from calls, buyer resentment, and claims that should be removed.
9. **Dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga**: when to write, test, present live, ask for sales, revise script, or avoid a hard close.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close Lagna/bhava/D10 claims unless the packet supplies reliable fallback. Use Moon, Sun, visible graha condition, dignity, nakshatra only if stable, and broad timing; frame page/webinar/call advice as lower precision.

## Format Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic graha keywords:

- **3rd/Mercury + 10th/D10 strong**: write a clear expert sales page, proof-first webinar, or structured proposal.
- **10th/D10 + 11th strong**: use webinar, live workshop, community launch, or public presentation.
- **7th strong, Mercury/2nd clean**: use consultation call, DM close, objection script, or one-to-one proposal.
- **2nd/4th/11th strong**: lead with value, assets, tangible outcomes, case library, and retained benefit.
- **5th/9th/Jupiter/Moon/Venus strong**: lead with story, transformation, aspiration, and emotional relief.
- **3rd/Mercury/2nd weak or afflicted**: shorten copy, remove jargon, make FAQ and proof clearer before scaling.
- **6th/8th/12th or Saturn-Mars-Rahu-Ketu active**: avoid fear copy, pressure close, exaggerated income claims, or "everyone must buy now" language.
- **7th/8th stressed**: qualify buyer before call, make price/fit clear, and avoid open-ended persuasion.

## Verdict Rules

- If the message channel is strong but buyer lane is weak, publish content and collect warm leads before sales calls.
- If buyer lane is strong but message is unclear, use a short consult script with diagnostic questions rather than a long sales page.
- If authority is strong, open with diagnosis and proof; if 5th/9th/Jupiter/Moon/Venus is stronger, open with story and relief.
- If group/audience lane is strong, use webinar or workshop; if direct counterpart lane is stronger, use 1:1 call or DM close.
- If 6th/8th/12th repeats, reduce hype, reduce pressure, and add proof/qualification before CTA.
