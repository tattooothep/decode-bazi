## Windfall Versus Earned Money

Do not call lottery luck from Jupiter or Rahu alone. Separate:

- earned income: 10th/D10 + 2nd/11th,
- gains/network: 11th,
- speculation: 5th + 2nd/11th,
- windfall/shared money/debt: 8th,
- fortune/support: 9th,
- leak/loss: 12th/6th/8th.

If 10th/D10/11th are stronger than 5th/8th, say the chart favors structured earning, clients, career, or business rather than gambling.
