# Jyotish Reputation, Public Trust, Review, And Crisis Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Vedic/Jyotish. It condenses Parashari/Jataka, dasha, varga, ashtakavarga, gochara, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack for reputation, public image, credibility, reviews, comments, gossip, scandal, public backlash, defamation, being attacked online, platform ban, public apology, and "will people still trust me" questions.

Do not answer from Sun, 10th, or fame language alone. Split visibility, credibility, accusation, audience reaction, money/status damage, and repair timing.

## Core Chain

1. **Public-pressure capacity**: Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon show self, mind, and ability to carry criticism. If birth time is missing, use Moon and graha condition only.
2. **Formal reputation**: 10th bhava/lord, D10, Sun, and relevant karaka show name, title, authority, public role, and professional standing.
3. **Message and evidence**: 3rd bhava, Mercury, 2nd speech, and supplied drishti show posting, replies, documents, proof, and rumor flow.
4. **Audience and gains**: 11th bhava/lord, Jupiter/Venus/Mercury support, and ashtakavarga show followers, allies, patrons, and public support.
5. **Opponent/customer/platform**: 7th bhava/lord shows customers, accusers, sponsors, contracts, and open public opponents.
6. **Material consequence**: 2nd/11th for revenue, 8th for liability/tax/shared risk, 12th for loss, 6th for complaints/service burden/enemies.
7. **Graha condition**: dignity, combustion, retrograde, drishti, dusthana/upachaya context, shadbala/bindu when supplied.
8. **Timing**: dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga activation decides whether the matter is temporary noise, repair phase, or material risk.

## Trust-Chain Rule

Public visibility is not public trust. Require a chain:

- Lagna/Moon + 10th/D10/Sun = public identity and standing,
- 3rd/Mercury/2nd = message, evidence, or rumor path,
- 11th = audience support or group reaction,
- 7th = customer/platform/accuser contact,
- 2nd/8th/12th/6th = money, liability, loss, service burden, and repair cost.

If 10th/D10 is strong but Mercury/3rd is pressured, reputation exists but explanation is weak. If 11th is pressured, crowd response is unstable. If 2nd/11th are protected, noise may not become revenue damage; if 8th/12th/6th are active, reputation risk can become liability, loss, or workload.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close Lagna-dependent bhava, D10, and house-lord claims unless packet supplies a reliable fallback. Use Moon, Sun, Mercury, Saturn/Mars/Rahu/Ketu condition, nakshatra, and dasha only when available.

## Crisis And Repair Rule

For review/backlash/scandal questions, name the situation:

- **misunderstanding**: 3rd/Mercury/2nd problem without deep 8th/12th damage,
- **customer/platform conflict**: 7th and 2nd/8th involved,
- **public pile-on**: 11th involved with malefic pressure,
- **formal/status damage**: 10th/D10/Sun involved,
- **hidden drain**: 6th/8th/12th involved.

Repair uses the strongest support axis: clarify evidence (3rd/Mercury), rebuild role promise (10th/D10), direct customer care (7th), allies/social proof (11th), fix pricing/refund/liability flow (2nd/8th), and reduce service/loss burden (6th/12th).
