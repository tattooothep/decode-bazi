## Purpose

Use this pack for ads, paid traffic, campaign, launch campaign, lead generation, funnel, landing page, CAC, ROAS, CPA, CPL, retargeting, pixel, conversion rate, ad creative, media buying, channel test, affiliate/referral traffic, and "should I spend ad budget" questions.

## Core Chain

1. **Capacity and campaign owner**: Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon show self, mind, and ability to carry campaign pressure. If birth time is missing, use Moon and graha condition only.
2. **Message and ad creative**: 3rd bhava/Mercury and 2nd speech show copy, hook, landing page, tracking terms, comment replies, chat scripts, and campaign clarity.
3. **Visibility and offer authority**: 10th/D10, Sun, Jupiter, and strong dignity show campaign claim, product credibility, and whether the ad promise is believable.
4. **Audience and traffic pool**: 11th bhava/lord shows gains, audience base, warm pool, affiliates, patrons, and whether traffic has quality.
5. **Buyer and conversion**: 7th bhava/lord shows customer, platform, sponsor, checkout counterparty, lead quality, and whether traffic becomes transaction.
6. **Cash recovery**: 2nd bhava/lord and 11th show collected revenue, CAC recovery, ROAS, and whether spend comes back as cash.
7. **Budget risk and leakage**: 6th complaints/workload, 8th fees/refunds/liability, 12th hidden loss/ad drain, and Saturn/Mars/Rahu/Ketu pressure show wasted spend, bad leads, or platform trouble.
8. **Varga and bala**: D10, shadbala, ashtakavarga/bindu when supplied show campaign durability and scaling capacity.
9. **Timing**: dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga activation decides whether to test, scale, pause, retarget, or rebuild.

## Campaign-Chain Rule

Traffic is not the same as customers. Require a chain:

- 3rd/Mercury/2nd = message and landing page,
- 10th/D10 = public claim and offer authority,
- 11th = audience and traffic pool,
- 7th = lead quality and conversion counterparty,
- 2nd/11th = CAC recovery and ROAS,
- 6th/8th/12th = wasted spend, complaint, bad leads, platform leak, hidden cost.

If 3rd/11th are strong but 7th/2nd are weak, the campaign gets attention but does not pay back. If 11th is strong but Mercury/3rd is weak, the audience exists but the hook/copy is wrong. If 8th/12th dominate, do not scale paid ads.
