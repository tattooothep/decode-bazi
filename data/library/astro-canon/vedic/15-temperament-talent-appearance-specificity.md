## Purpose

Use this pack for personality, temperament, strengths, weaknesses, talent, skill, charisma, appearance, body style, personal brand, and "what kind of person is this chart" questions.

Do not answer from rashi sign alone. Use Lagna, Lagnesha, Moon, Sun, nakshatra, graha condition, relevant bhava/lords, varga, bala, dasha, and gochara.

## Core Chain

1. **Lagna and Lagnesha**: body, identity, health-vitality baseline, and how life is steered.
2. **Moon and Moon nakshatra**: mind, habit, emotional style, public responsiveness.
3. **Sun**: authority, confidence, visibility, father/soul signal.
4. **Mercury/Venus/Mars/Jupiter/Saturn**: mind/speech, charm/art, action/courage, wisdom/teaching, endurance/duty.
5. **Relevant bhavas**: 1st for body/personality, 2nd for speech/value, 3rd for skill, 5th for intelligence/creativity, 10th/D10 for public talent.
6. **Graha condition and drishti**: dignity, lord relation, combustion, retrograde, dusthana/upachaya context.
7. **Varga/bala/dasha**: D9 maturity, D10 public delivery, shadbala/ashtakavarga, and timing.

If birth time is missing, close Lagna, bhava/lord, varga-house, and exact body/appearance claims unless packet explicitly supplies a reliable fallback. Use Moon, graha, nakshatra, and dasha only when stable.

## Appearance / Charisma / Personal Brand

Use Lagna, Lagnesha, planets influencing Lagna, Moon, Venus/Sun, and D9 support. No birth time means no firm body/appearance claim.
