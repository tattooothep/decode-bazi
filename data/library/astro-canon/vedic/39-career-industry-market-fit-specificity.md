# Jyotish Career, Industry, Market, And Role-Fit Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Vedic/Jyotish. It condenses Parashari/Jataka, dasha, varga, ashtakavarga, gochara, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks what career, profession, business type, industry, market, niche, role, shop, service, product lane, or monetizable skill suits them.

Do not answer from the 10th bhava, one graha, or one yoga alone. Split talent, role, market, money model, operating burden, public visibility, people channel, asset base, and timing.

## Fit Chain

1. **Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon**: core operating style, pressure tolerance, mental fit, and how the native carries work. If no birth time, use Moon/graha condition only.
2. **10th bhava/lord and D10**: profession, role, title, authority, institution, public duty, business model, and career status.
3. **2nd / 11th / 8th**: earning model, retained money, gains, pricing power, debt/liability, investor/partner cost, and whether the industry retains value.
4. **3rd / Mercury / 2nd speech**: writing, speaking, code, sales language, documents, teaching, consulting, trade, media, and skill transmission.
5. **6th**: daily work, service load, operations, employees, competition, maintenance, health cost, and repetitive execution.
6. **7th / 11th**: clients, customers, partners, platforms, audience, community, network gains, and social proof.
7. **4th / 12th**: property, premises, backend, hidden work, institutions, data/archives, sleep cost, foreign/remote burden, and behind-the-scenes work.
8. **Nakshatra/varga/bala/ashtakavarga**: style and capacity only after bhava/lord/graha facts are anchored.
9. **Dasha/gocara**: decides when to specialize, switch, launch, consolidate, or wait.

## Fit Verdict Rule

Career fit is not the same as money fit.

- If 10th/D10 is strong but 2nd/11th is weak, the role suits status but money retention needs structure.
- If 2nd/11th is strong but 10th/D10 is weak, money can come from trade/assets/client work but career identity may feel unstable.
- If 3rd/Mercury/2nd is strong, use communication, teaching, writing, code, sales, consulting, documentation, or media as a bridge.
- If 7th/11th is stressed, avoid people-heavy industries or use contracts/SOPs before scaling.
- If 6th/8th/12th repeats pressure, avoid high-maintenance/high-liability industries even if the public role looks attractive.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close Lagna-dependent bhava, D10, house lordship, and exact timing unless the packet supplies a reliable fallback. Use Moon, graha dignity, nakshatra, retrograde/combustion, drishti if supplied, and broad dasha only when available; frame industry fit as lower precision.
