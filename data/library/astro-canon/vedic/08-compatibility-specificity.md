# Jyotish Compatibility And Marriage Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Vedic/Jyotish. It condenses the retained Parashari, Stri Jataka, Jataka Parijata, Sarvartha Chintamani, Phaladeepika, Vivaha/Muhurta, Ashtakavarga/Nakshatra, and licensed-internal collation corpus into operational relationship rules. It is not raw source text and must not be quoted, cited, or exposed to users.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about love, marriage, spouse, compatibility, pair reading, or relationship timing.

The goal is sharper relationship answers. Do not answer "เข้ากันได้/ต้องสื่อสาร" without naming:

1. each person's 7th bhava / 7th lord when birth time is reliable,
2. Venus, Jupiter where applicable, Moon, and emotional pattern,
3. D9/Navamsa support or friction when packet supplies it,
4. Tara/Moon relation and rashi relation from `PAIR_INTERACTION_PACKET vedic`,
5. Parashari drishti / degree contacts / bhava overlays from the closed pair packet,
6. current dasha cross-reference when supplied,
7. whether the verdict is attraction, stability, family support, pressure, or timing readiness.

## Single-Chart Relationship Chain

For one chart, read in this order:

1. **7th bhava and 7th lord**: spouse/partner field, public contract, commitment pattern.
2. **Venus**: attraction, desire, pleasure, relationship style. For all genders, Venus is not enough alone.
3. **Jupiter**: dharma, blessing, husband/teacher/protection significator where the tradition applies.
4. **Moon**: emotional rhythm, attachment, care, mind's comfort.
5. **2nd/4th/11th support**: family continuity, home comfort, social acceptance, gains from partnership.
6. **6th/8th/12th pressure**: conflict, debt, crisis, intimacy burden, isolation, hidden cost.
7. **D9/Navamsa**: maturity of the promise; confirms or weakens D1, never replaces D1 by itself.
8. **Dasha and gochara**: timing must repeat 7th/Venus/Jupiter/Moon/D9 themes before calling a period relationship-ready.

Do not declare marriage, separation, divorce, or "soulmate" from one factor.

## Pair Mode Evidence Order

In pair mode, use only `PAIR_INTERACTION_PACKET vedic` for cross-chart claims. Weight in this order:

1. **Moon/Tara both directions**: emotional ease or stress must be read both A to B and B to A.
2. **Moon rashi relation**: same/trikona/kendra supports familiarity; 6/8 or 2/12 creates adjustment/friction.
3. **Lagna rashi relation**: use only when both birth times are known.
4. **Parashari drishti cross contacts**: Saturn/Mars/Rahu/Ketu style pressure, Jupiter/Venus/Moon support, and exact graha-to-graha contacts.
5. **Degree contacts**: same-degree same-rashi intensifies merger; opposite-rashi intensifies polarity.
6. **Bhava overlays**: use only when both birth times are known; especially 1/4/5/7/8/11/12.
7. **Current dasha cross-reference**: if one person's dasha lord is prominent in the other chart, the relationship feels activated through that planet/topic.

If the packet says `ashtakootaScore` or `navamsaSynastry` is not available, do not invent it. Read the pair from the supplied layers and name the missing layer only if the user asks why the verdict cannot be sharper.

## Verdict Categories

Every pair verdict should split the relationship into these categories:

- **Attraction**: Venus/Mars, 5th/7th overlays, same-degree contacts, Moon/Venus/Jupiter softness.
- **Emotional fit**: Moon/Tara, Moon rashi relation, Moon contacts, 4th-house overlays.
- **Stability / marriage readiness**: 7th bhava/lord, D9, Saturn constructiveness, 2nd/4th/11th support, compatible dashas.
- **Pressure / conflict**: Mars/Saturn/Rahu/Ketu contacts, 6/8 or 2/12 rashi relations, 6th/8th/12th overlays, afflicted 7th/Venus/Moon.
- **Timing**: current mahadasha/antardasha and gochara activation. Good compatibility without timing is attraction/potential, not guaranteed commitment.

End with a direct final verdict: "หนุน", "มีแรงดึงแต่ยังหนัก", "อยู่ได้ถ้าวางกติกา", or "ไม่ควรเร่งผูกมัดตอนนี้" based on repeated evidence.

## Tara And Moon Rules

Read Tara both ways:

- Sampat, Kshema, Sadhaka, Mitra, Paramamitra: support, ease, shared benefit, constructive growth.
- Janma: sensitivity and familiarity; can be intimate but reactive.
- Vipat, Pratyari, Naidhana: stress, interruption, rivalry, draining pattern.

If one direction supports and the other stresses, write the asymmetry plainly. Do not average it into "กลางๆ".

Moon relation:

- same rashi: strong familiarity but can amplify mood.
- trikona/kendra: easier rhythm, shared worldview or practical cooperation.
- 6/8: effort, health/routine/emotional mismatch, crisis-learning pattern.
- 2/12: unequal give-take, distance, expenses, or one side feeling drained.
- 7th/opposition: attraction and polarity; needs maturity and timing support.

## Graha Contact Rules

Use Parashari drishti from the packet:

- Jupiter to Moon/Venus/Lagna/7th topics: protection, counsel, forgiveness, growth.
- Venus to Moon/Mars/7th topics: attraction, affection, pleasure, relational glue.
- Moon contacts: emotional responsiveness; benefic contact softens, malefic contact sensitizes.
- Mars contacts: desire, heat, speed, conflict, sexual charge, impatience.
- Saturn contacts: commitment, duty, age/distance, fear, delay, endurance.
- Rahu/Ketu contacts: obsession, foreign/unusual bond, karmic pull, instability, spiritualizing or detaching pressure.

Do not call a malefic contact automatically fatal. It can support commitment when the natal charts and dasha show maturity; it becomes risky when repeated with 6/8/12 pressure and weak 7th/Venus/Moon.

## D9 / Navamsa Use

For single-chart marriage questions:

- D9 strengthens when the relevant graha is own/exalted/vargottama or repeats a benefic theme.
- D9 weakens when the relevant graha is debilitated/enemy and D1 already shows pressure.
- D9 is a maturity confirmation layer. It should not erase a damaged 7th lord or afflicted Venus/Moon without repeated rescue.

For pair mode:

- Use Navamsa synastry only if the packet supplies it.
- If not supplied, do not say the pair reading is impossible; say the verdict is from D1, Moon/Tara, drishti, overlays, and dasha only.

## No-Time Mode

If either chart lacks birth time:

- Close Lagna relation, bhava overlays, 7th-house cross claims, D9-house claims, and dasha certainty if packet marks Moon uncertain.
- Use graha-rashi contacts, Moon/Tara only as conditional when flagged, and Parashari drishti between grahas.
- Do not pretend noon-anchor Lagna is real.

User-facing shape:

"เพราะไม่มีเวลาเกิดของ [name], ผมอ่านความเข้ากันได้จาก graha-rashi/Moon layer เท่านั้น; เรื่องเรือนคู่ครองและ overlay ยังไม่ถือว่าแน่น."

## Output Rule

Every Vedic relationship answer must include:

- one line for each person's relationship baseline,
- one Moon/Tara or no-time substitute,
- one cross-graha contact from the pair packet,
- one D9 or explicit D9 limitation,
- one dasha/timing line if timing is asked,
- one final verdict separated into attraction, stability, pressure, and timing.

If a sentence could fit many couples, rewrite it with owner name + graha/bhava/rashi/tara/dasha field from the packet.
