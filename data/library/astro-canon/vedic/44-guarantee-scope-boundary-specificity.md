## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about guarantee, refund-proof offer, money-back guarantee, no-refund policy, scope of work, deliverables, revision limit, unlimited revisions/support, promise boundary, result guarantee, contract boundary, qualification gate, cancellation terms, or how to prevent complaints/refunds before selling.

Do not treat guarantee/scope fit as the same thing as retention after a refund request, exact pricing, delivery model, customer support, or product category. This pack decides what promise is safe to make, what must be excluded, how strict the scope should be, and whether a guarantee should be strong, conditional, narrow, or avoided.

## Guarantee And Scope Chain

1. **Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon or no-time substitute**: whether the person can carry a firm promise, strict standard, high-touch responsibility, or result-based guarantee without identity/energy strain.
2. **10th bhava/D10/Sun/Saturn**: formal authority, professional responsibility, result ownership, public promise, B2B/enterprise accountability, and whether a guarantee improves trust or creates liability.
3. **3rd/Mercury/9th**: contract wording, policy page, proof, instructions, onboarding, acceptance criteria, revision rules, and whether buyers will understand the boundary.
4. **7th bhava/lord and Venus/Jupiter when relevant**: direct buyer agreement, objections, qualification, refusal/complaint risk, deposit/contract terms, and whether the client can be safely accepted.
5. **11th bhava/lord**: team/platform/community support burden, reviewer pressure, affiliate/channel obligations, and whether service boundaries can be enforced at scale.
6. **2nd/4th/8th/11th**: refund reserve, retained assets, backend library/data, shared-money exposure, cost of honoring promises, and whether the offer can absorb guarantee risk.
7. **6th/8th/12th/Saturn-Mars-Rahu**: hidden labor, burnout, legal/health/financial risk, abusive customer pattern, chargeback/refund stress, and guarantee clauses to avoid.
8. **9th/10th/11th/Jupiter**: satisfaction, perceived fairness, prestige/trust, external reputation, emotional comfort, and whether a stricter policy will feel fair or harsh.
9. **Dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga/varga**: judge strength and policy window from supplied packet only.

## Boundary Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic graha keywords:

- **10th/D10 + Mercury/9th strong, 6th/12th controlled**: clear professional guarantee, written scope, measurable deliverables, acceptance criteria.
- **7th strong, 2nd/4th/11th stable**: conditional guarantee with qualification, deposit, milestones, and refund reserve.
- **3rd/Mercury weak or afflicted**: simplify wording; do not add layered guarantees until copy/onboarding/proof is clear.
- **11th strong but 6th/12th active**: scalable policy needed; no unlimited custom support, no open-ended revisions.
- **2nd/8th weak with Saturn-Mars-Rahu stress**: avoid money-back guarantee; use satisfaction check, credit, rework cap, or narrow replacement.
- **9th/10th/11th/Jupiter strong**: fairness/reputation can carry a firm no-refund policy if expectations are clean.
- **7th/9th/8th afflicted**: require signed agreement, qualification, proof archive, and dispute path before accepting high-risk buyers.

## Verdict Rules

- If authority and documentation are strong, use a narrow written guarantee rather than vague reassurance.
- If buyer trust is strong but hidden labor is high, use conditional guarantee plus strict scope and revision cap.
- If 6th/8th/12th or Saturn-Mars-Rahu repeats, do not promise outcomes; guarantee process or deliverables only.
- If Mercury/3rd is weak, fix copy, onboarding, and acceptance criteria before launching a guarantee.
- If support burden is heavy, remove unlimited revisions/support and state out-of-scope items explicitly.
