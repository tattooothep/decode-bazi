## Purpose

Use this pack for work, business, money, health, year-ahead, or combined "งาน เงิน สุขภาพ" questions. It exists to make Jyotish answers specific to the supplied chart packet instead of broad spiritual/life-advice language.

Do not answer from one graha, one yoga, one dasha lord, or one transit alone.

## Career / Karma Chain

Read career in this order:

1. **Lagna and Lagnesha**: capacity, direction, and how the person acts.
2. **10th bhava/lord and planets in 10th**: profession, status, duty, visibility.
3. **6th/11th/2nd links**: service/competition, gains/network, earning channel.
4. **5th and 9th lords**: intelligence, skill, past merit, teachers, luck, counsel, dharma support. Use them as career accelerators only when tied to 10th/6th/11th/2nd, dasha, D10, or strong dignity.
5. **Sun, Saturn, Mercury, Jupiter, Mars, Venus, Moon, Rahu/Ketu** as work karakas according to the actual house/lord chain.
6. **D10 Dashamsa**: confirms career expression/status when supplied.
7. **Nakshatra**: use Moon nakshatra and the career/dasha lord's nakshatra when supplied as style and motive, not as a standalone verdict.
8. **Dasha + gochara**: mahadasha sets field, antardasha triggers channel, gochara activates or pressures.

Name the career channel from packet evidence: authority, advisory/teaching, technical/systems, trade/language, finance/assets, foreign/unconventional, research/hidden work, creative/beauty, property, service/operations, competitive/action, or spiritual/healing support.

## Wealth / Artha Chain

Separate wealth into lanes:

1. **2nd bhava/lord**: family resources, speech, savings, cash held.
2. **11th bhava/lord**: gains, clients, audience, network, recurring profit.
3. **5th/9th**: fortune, speculation, knowledge products, patronage, merit support.
4. **10th/6th**: earning through work, service, employment, business operations.
5. **8th/12th**: tax, debt, shared money, sudden reversals, hidden cost, foreign/outflow.
6. **Jupiter/Venus/Mercury**: wealth helpers only when house/lord/dasha supports.
7. **Rahu in 2nd/11th**: unusual money channel, foreign/digital/nonstandard gain, appetite, volatility. It is not stable wealth unless the 2nd/11th lord, dasha, bindu, and retention houses support it.

Do not call "rich" from a dhana yoga candidate unless dignity, house placement, dasha, and affliction check support it.

## Health / Risk Chain

Use health symbolically and safely:

1. **Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon**: vitality, mind-body rhythm, resilience.
2. **6th**: illness/workload/habits/enemies/discipline.
3. **8th**: chronic pressure, transformation, hidden vulnerability, crisis-management.
4. **12th**: sleep, loss, isolation, hospital/rest, retreat, foreign drain.
5. **Saturn and Mars**: chronic/load/boundary pressure versus acute heat/action pressure.
6. **Rahu/Ketu**: irregularity, amplification, hidden or nonstandard pattern.
7. **Shadbala/ashtakavarga**: resilience/weakness confirmation, not a diagnosis.
8. **Dasha/gocara**: periods that ask for discipline, rest, check-up, and risk management.

Allowed: vitality, sleep/routine pressure, workload strain, chronic/acute symbolism, check-up, rest, discipline.

Forbidden: diagnosis, fixed disease, surgery instruction, medication advice, death prediction, or replacing clinicians.

## Combined Work Money Health Question

Answer in three mini-verdicts:

1. **งาน**: Lagna/Lagnesha + 10th lord/house + D10 + dasha/gocara.
2. **เงิน**: 2nd/11th + 5th/9th + Rahu/Jupiter/Venus/Mercury if relevant + ashtakavarga/shadbala + dasha.
3. **สุขภาพ**: Lagna/Moon + 6th/8th/12th + Saturn/Mars/Rahu-Ketu + dasha/gocara.

Then add one fused conclusion:

- "career leads money",
- "money grows but retention needs discipline",
- "health/routine limits career speed",
- "foreign/unusual channel brings gains but volatility",
- "dasha demands cleanup before expansion",
- or another packet-backed phrase.

Do not merge all lanes into one vague life statement.

## Timing Discipline

1. Use the target-year refDate supplied by the prompt.

3. State each dasha lord's house/lordship/sign/dignity and topic connection.
4. Confirm with D10 for career, D9 for maturity/dharma/relationship, shadbala/ashtakavarga for strength, and gochara for activation.
5. Do not claim exact month/day unless sub-period or transit-window fields exist.
6. Do not introduce Ashtottari, Chara, Tajika, Jaimini, prashna, or muhurta unless the packet supplies that branch.
