# Jyotish Travel, Foreign Field, Relocation, And Outside Opportunity Specificity Pack

Derived operational summary for travel, foreign/distant field, relocation, outside clients, public exposure, and leaving the home base in Jyotish. Use only as internal interpretation rules; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope

Use this pack when the question asks about travel, commute, moving city, moving country, relocation, migration, foreign opportunity, overseas clients, online/outside audience, work away from home, foreign study, travel risk, or whether to go/stay/delay.

If the question is buying/selling/renting residence, land, condo, vehicle, mortgage, title deed, or lease, use property/vehicle pack first. If it asks visa/permit/legal approval, also use authority/document. If it asks muhurta/electional date selection, also use the specialty guard.

## Evidence Chain

1. **Lagna/Lagnesha or Moon substitute**: capacity to carry movement and disruption. With no birth time, use Moon/nakshatra/graha condition and valid dasha only; do not invent bhava/varga-house claims.
2. **3rd bhava**: short trips, commute, courage to move, documents, communications, local movement.
3. **9th bhava / Jupiter / Sun**: long-distance travel, foreign study, dharma, guru, law, pilgrimage, publishing, visas/institutions when legal/academic.
4. **12th bhava / Rahu / Saturn / Moon**: foreign residence, isolation, retreat, expense/loss, sleep, hospitals/rest, living away from root.
5. **4th bhava / Moon/Venus**: home base, comfort, mother/family root, old/new residence, emotional cost of leaving.
6. **10th/D10 and 11th**: work-driven relocation, foreign clients, audience/network gains, external career result.
7. **2nd / 8th**: travel cost, relocation budget, debt, tax, insurance, hidden cost, shared money.
8. **6th / Mars / Saturn / Rahu-Ketu**: fatigue, conflict, overwork, accident heat, health strain. Use as symbolic risk-management, not certainty.
9. **D9/D10 if supplied + dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga**: maturity, career use, and timing activation. If timing is year-only, do not claim exact month/day.

## Reading Rules

- Do not answer relocation from 12th alone. Require Lagna/Moon capacity, 3rd/9th/12th movement field, 4th base, money/work support, and dasha/gocara timing.
- Strong 9th/12th with weak 4th means foreign pull is real but home/family comfort must be managed.
- Strong 4th with weak 9th/12th means staying, shorter movement, or gradual relocation is safer than forcing a foreign move.
- For foreign clients/outside audience, combine 9th/12th with 10th/11th and Mercury/Jupiter/Rahu. Do not treat it as tourism.
- With no birth time, do not use bhava lordship or varga-house claims unless packet supplies reliable fallback.

## Answer Contract

For a travel/foreign/relocation question, write:
1. one Lagna/Lagnesha or no-time Moon/nakshatra capacity-to-move line,
2. one 3rd/9th/12th travel-foreign-relocation line or explicit no-time limitation,
3. one 4th home-base/comfort line or no-time limitation,
4. one 10th/D10/11th work-client-audience line when relevant,
5. one 2nd/8th cost-hidden-expense line when relevant,
6. one 6th/Mars/Saturn/Rahu-Ketu fatigue/risk-management line when relevant,
7. one dasha/gocara timing line,
8. one decisive practical call: go / go with conditions / prepare first / delay / stay / choose closer option / avoid this trip.
