# Jyotish Children, Pregnancy, Fertility Symbolism, And Creative Output Specificity Pack

Derived operational summary for children, pregnancy/fertility symbolism, parenting, students/protégés, creative output, mantra/intelligence, performance, and side-project questions in Jyotish. Use only as internal interpretation rules; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope

Use this pack when the question asks about children, pregnancy timing, fertility tendency, parenting, existing children, students/protégés, authored work, creative output, performance, content, mantra/intelligence, or a personal side project.

Medical policy: Jyotish in Fusion may describe symbolic readiness, timing, stress, support, and remedial risk-management. It must not diagnose infertility, pregnancy, fetal condition, miscarriage, or medical outcome. Require medical confirmation for medical claims.

## Evidence Chain

1. **Lagna/Lagnesha or Moon substitute**: baseline capacity. With no birth time, use Moon/nakshatra/graha condition and valid dasha only; do not invent bhava or varga-house claims.
2. **5th bhava / 5th lord**: children, pregnancy symbolism, intelligence, mantra, purva punya, creative output, students/protégés, authored work.
3. **Jupiter / Sun / Moon / Venus**: children, growth, lineage, vitality, body rhythm, pleasure, and creative fertility. Weigh condition, dignity, combustion, retrograde, and drishti.
4. **7th / Venus / relationship indicators**: partner cooperation and shared responsibility when the question depends on spouse/partner.
5. **6th / 8th / 12th / Saturn / Mars / Rahu-Ketu**: stress, treatment, hidden pressure, surgery/medical lane, rest, loss, fear. Use as symbolic risk-management only.
6. **2nd / 4th / 9th / 11th**: family support, home readiness, dharma/blessing, gains/help network, childcare resources, project support.
7. **3rd / Mercury / 10th / D10**: writing/content, public launch, career use of creative project, audience work.
8. **D9 / Saptamsa D7 if supplied / D10 if career-linked**: confirm maturity and topic strength only from supplied packet fields. Do not invent missing divisional charts.
9. **Dasha / antardasha / gochara / ashtakavarga**: timing activation. If timing is year-only, do not claim exact month/day.

## Reading Rules

- Do not promise pregnancy from Jupiter or 5th alone. Require 5th, Lagna/Moon capacity, Jupiter/karaka condition, stress/rest layer, support layer, and dasha/gocara repetition.
- For fertility/pregnancy questions, separate symbolic child potential, partner cooperation, body/rest pressure, home/money support, and timing. Add medical-confirmation guard once.
- For existing children, separate affection, duty, education, distance, worry, cost, and discipline.
- For creative output, 5th is the creative child, 3rd/Mercury is writing/content, 10th/D10 is public/career result, 11th is audience/gains, 2nd/8th/12th is funding and hidden cost.
- With no birth time, do not use Lagna, 5th bhava, bhava lordship, D7/D10 house claims, or house-specific dasha claims unless packet explicitly supplies a reliable fallback.

## Answer Contract

For a children/pregnancy/creative-output question, write:
1. one Lagna/Lagnesha or no-time Moon/nakshatra capacity line,
2. one 5th bhava/lord child-output-intelligence line or explicit no-time limitation,
3. one Jupiter/Sun/Moon/Venus karaka line,
4. one 7th/partner-support line when relevant,
5. one 6th/8th/12th or Saturn/Mars/Rahu-Ketu stress-rest-medical-lane line for pregnancy/fertility questions with a medical-confirmation guard,
6. one 2nd/4th/9th/11th or 3rd/10th/D10 practical support line matching the question,
7. one dasha/gocara timing line,
8. one decisive practical call: try now / try with conditions / prepare first / wait / focus on project launch / delay launch / get medical or professional confirmation first.
