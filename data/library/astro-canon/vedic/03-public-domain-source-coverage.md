## Scope By Source Family

- **Brihat Jataka**: use as a classical cross-check for graha nature, dignity, rashi results, bhava effects, yogas, and dasha principles already supported by the packet.
- **Brihat Jataka commentaries / Vijnananda / Bhattotpala / Vivriti / Vimala / Sharada manuscript witnesses**: use as commentary cross-checks to sharpen the Brihat Jataka layer. They never replace computed packet facts, Parashari functional lordship, dasha, varga, or strength fields.
- **Brihat Parashar / Parasara Hora Shastra BPHS witnesses**: use as Parashari backbone for graha, bhava, dasha, varga, yoga, arishta/relief, and functional lordship logic. Compare Padma Nabha Sharma and Girish Chand Sharma witnesses when extracting rules. They may sharpen the reading only after the computed packet supplies the relevant graha, house, dignity, dasha, varga, and strength fields.
- **Hora Ratnam / Daivajna Vallabha licensed incoming witnesses**: use as internal corroboration for hora/natal aphorisms, graha/bhava specificity, and concise judgment language after packet facts are anchored. They are not public-domain prompt text and must not appear as quoted source material.
- **Samaya Shuddhi Viveka licensed incoming witness**: use only as internal support for time purity, calendar suitability, and muhurta-style constraints when the packet supplies electional/panchang fields. Do not apply it to ordinary natal readings.
- **Daivagya Kamadhenu / Hora Shastra / Yavana Jataka licensed incoming collation witnesses**: use as internal corroboration for older hora, graha/bhava, rashi, yogic, and Yavana/cross-cultural result language after packet facts are anchored. They can sharpen specificity but cannot override BPHS/Laghu-Parashari functional lordship, dasha, varga, shadbala/ashtakavarga, or computed packet fields.
- **Row licensed incoming witnesses**: use `Stri Jataka or Female Horoscopy` only as a relationship/marriage-specific support layer after 7th bhava, Venus/Jupiter, Moon, D9, and dasha facts are present. Use Row `Brihat Jataka` and `Sarvartha Chintamani` as English collation for natal topic detail. Use `The Astrological Self Instructor` as terminology/training support only. Use `Chappanna or Prasana Sastra` only for prashna/question-chart packets. None of these sources can create missing Lagna, bhava, D9, dasha, or prashna fields.
- **Saravali**: use to sharpen planet-in-sign, planet-in-house, combinations, and concrete native-description language. Do not let Saravali sign keywords override Lagna lord, bhava lordship, dasha, and packet strength.
- **Phaladeepika and added Phala Deepika witnesses**: use for bhava results, yogas, arishta/relief logic, ashtakavarga, dasha indications, and profession/wealth combinations. Treat concrete indications as a specificity layer only after the computed packet confirms the required graha, bhava, dignity, and lordship.
- **Jataka Parijata / Stri Jataka support**: use Jataka Parijata for yogas, planet/house combinations, marriage/children/wealth/career indicators, and practical result language. English 1932 witness is primary; Sanskrit OCR is cross-check only. Use Stri Jataka only to sharpen relationship/female-horoscope context when packet facts already support that topic.
- **Jataka Tattva**: use for compact natal aphorisms, bhava results, house lord combinations, and topic-specific judgment cross-checks. Apply only after packet facts supply the relevant Lagna, bhava, lordship, and dignity/strength.
- **Manasagari / Jataka Alankara / Jataka Chandrika / Chamatkara Chintamani / Jataka Sara / Lagna Chandrika / Jataka Abharana / Jataka Bharanam / Jataka Paddhati / Hora Sara manuscript**: use as natal specificity witnesses for graha-in-bhava, lagna-specific logic, bhava lord combinations, yogas, temperament, marriage/children/wealth/status indicators, and concise aphoristic results. Missing-page or manuscript witnesses are cross-check only. These sources sharpen a reading after packet facts are anchored; they do not override BPHS, functional lordship, dasha, varga, or strength fields.
- **Sambhu Hora Prakash**: use as a supplementary natal/hora witness for yogas, graha/bhava specificity, and practical result language after the packet provides the relevant graha, bhava, lordship, dignity, dasha, or varga evidence.
- **Bhava Kutuhala / Bhava Prakasha**: use as house/topic-result support for concrete bhava readings. Apply only after the packet provides the relevant Lagna, bhava, lordship, graha placement, dignity/strength, dasha, or varga evidence. Do not use these witnesses to invent houses in no-time mode.
- **Jyotisha/Jyotir/Nav Ratnamala**: use as compilation/collation support for jyotisha terminology, topic grouping, and supplementary result language after the packet provides the relevant graha, bhava, lordship, dignity/strength, dasha, varga, or muhurta evidence. Treat manuscript witnesses as corroboration, not primary authority.
- **Nakshatra Ratnakara / Jyotisha Vichara Vivaha-Ashtakavarga**: use only when packet fields include nakshatra, ashtakavarga, marriage-electional, or related timing factors. They can sharpen topic language but must not create nakshatra/ashtakavarga data in no-time mode.
- **Uttara Kalamrita**: use for special yogas, significators, house-specific effects, remedial caution language, and refinement of marriage/wealth/status readings. Do not override packet facts.
- **Laghu/Madhya Parashari**: use for Parashari functional benefic/malefic judgment, kendra-trikona yoga logic, dasha lordship weighting, and maraka/dusthana caution.
- **Jaimini Sutras**: use only when the packet supplies Jaimini fields such as chara karaka, atmakaraka, karakamsa, pada/arudha, or rashi drishti. If those fields are absent, do not create Jaimini claims from a Parashari packet.
- **Sarvartha Chintamani**: use for topic-specific bhava/lord/yoga detail, especially wealth, career, family, relationship, children, health indications, and result specificity. Apply only after the packet confirms the relevant Lagna, house lordship, dignity, dasha/varga, and strength fields. Row's English witness is collation/support only and must not be quoted.
- **Garga Hora Shastra**: use as an older hora/yoga support witness for classical planet, sign, yoga, and timing themes. It can corroborate a Parashari reading, but must not override the computed packet or the BPHS/Laghu-Parashari backbone.
- **Bhrigu Sutram / Bhrigu Prashna Nadi / Bhrigu Prashnavali**: use as supplementary aphorism/prashna/nadi context. Use prashna rules only when the packet is explicitly a prashna/horary question chart. For natal charts, use Bhrigu-family sources only as a supporting specificity layer after Parashari packet facts are already anchored.
- **Muhurta Chintamani / Muhurta Martanda / Jyotisha Muhurta / Muhurta Ratna / Muhurta Darpana / Muhurta Sindhu / Muhurta Ganapati / Kalaprakashika / Vivaha witnesses**: use only for electional/muhurta questions, date/time selection, activity suitability, tithi/nakshatra/weekday/yoga/karana style reasoning, and marriage/travel/business-action timing when the packet supplies the relevant electional date/time fields. ## Dasha And Yoga Guard

- A good dasha lord can still give mixed results when it owns dusthana/maraka houses or sits in 6/8/12.
- A difficult dasha lord can give usable results when it owns a trikona/kendra, forms viparita, or is strengthened by dignity/varga.
- Declare yoga only when the packet supplies the required planets, houses, dignity, and cancellation checks. Use “candidate” when cancellation is not checked.
- Do not use shadbala/ashtakavarga score to erase a severe house/dasha problem. Explain both support and friction.
