## Purpose

Use this pack for price, package, tier, coupon, discount, promotion, bundle, upsell, cross-sell, add-on, top-up, credit pack, paid plan, raise price, lower price, intro offer, and "what offer should I sell" questions.

Do not answer from popularity, sales, or retention alone. Split price power, perceived value, customer willingness, delivery burden, cash margin, discount leakage, and timing.

## Core Chain

1. **Capacity and promise owner**: Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon show self, mind, and ability to carry the offer promise. If birth time is missing, use Moon and graha condition only.
2. **Offer authority and positioning**: 10th/D10, Sun, Jupiter, and strong dignity show product promise, professional authority, and premium position.
3. **Price and stored wealth**: 2nd bhava/lord shows price received and stored cash; 11th shows gains, warm audience, patrons, and upsell pool.
4. **Customer willingness and terms**: 7th bhava/lord shows buyer, subscriber, sponsor, platform, contract, checkout counterparty, and price acceptance.
5. **Offer message and checkout clarity**: 3rd/Mercury and 2nd speech show price explanation, coupon terms, sales page, billing copy, and support messages.
6. **Delivery cost and leakage**: 6th workload/complaints, 8th refunds/fees/liability, 12th hidden loss/discount drain, and Saturn/Mars/Rahu/Ketu pressure show margin erosion.
7. **Varga and bala**: D10, shadbala, ashtakavarga/bindu when supplied show durability of offer delivery and pricing power.
8. **Timing**: dasha/gocara/ashtakavarga activation decides whether to raise price, test a short promo, bundle, or wait.

## Pricing-Chain Rule

Price is not the same as demand. Require a chain:

- 10th/D10 = offer promise and authority,
- 2nd = price/margin received,
- 11th = user base and upsell pool,
- 7th = buyer/customer/platform accepts terms,
- 3rd/Mercury/2nd = price explanation and coupon clarity,
- 6th/8th/12th = delivery cost, refund, discount leak, hidden loss.

If 10th/11th are strong but 2nd is weak, the offer has value but is underpriced. If 2nd is strong but 7th is pressured, the price can sell yet creates customer friction. If 8th/12th dominate, avoid broad coupons and protect margin.

## Discount / Promotion Rule

For coupon, launch promo, and discount questions, name the pressure:

- **safe short promo**: 3rd/Mercury clear + 7th cooperative + 2nd still supported,
- **underpricing risk**: 10th/11th strong but 2nd weak,
- **discount abuse**: 2nd/8th/12th pressure,
- **unclear offer**: 3rd/Mercury/2nd speech weak,
- **premium room**: 10th/D10/11th/7th supported with clean 2nd.

Do not recommend broad discounting when leak markers are stronger than customer markers.
