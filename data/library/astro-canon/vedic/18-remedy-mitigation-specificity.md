## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks "แก้ยังไง", "เสริมยังไง", "ลดแรงเสีย", "รับมือยังไง", "ป้องกันอะไร", "ทำอะไรให้ดีขึ้น", "แก้ดวง", "เสริมดวง", "upaya", or mitigation/remedy questions in Jyotish.

The answer must convert Jyotish evidence into concrete risk-reduction and strengthening steps. Upaya or spiritual practice may be included only as supportive practice, not as a guaranteed cure or replacement for practical/professional action.

## Mitigation Chain

1. Identify the stressed topic bhava/karaka: 10th/D10, 2nd/11th/5th/9th, 7th/Venus/Jupiter/D9, 6th/8th/12th, 3rd/9th/12th, 4th, Moon, or Lagna.
2. Identify pressure source: weak graha, dusthana/maraka tie, affliction, combustion, weak dasha lord, difficult gochara, or low ashtakavarga support.
3. Identify available support: strong Lagna/Lagnesha, Moon/nakshatra resource, benefic drishti, varga strength, yogakaraka/supportive graha, shadbala/ashtakavarga, or good dasha/gocara window.
4. Convert into mitigation: reduce the dusthana/maraka exposure, act through the supporting graha/bhava, and time action with dasha/gocara support.
5. If birth time is missing, do not prescribe Lagna/bhava/varga remedies unless packet supplies trusted substitutes.

## Remedy Rules

- For work stress, use 10th/D10/Saturn/Sun/Mercury evidence: structure, documentation, skill discipline, authority management, and staged career moves.
- For money risk, use 2nd/11th/5th/9th/Rahu/Jupiter/Venus evidence: reserves, debt limits, clean contracts, avoiding speculative overreach, and timed expansion.
- For relationship stress, use 7th/Venus/Jupiter/D9/Moon evidence: pacing, communication vows, boundaries, and dasha-aware relationship decisions.
- For health/routine pressure, use 6th/8th/12th/Moon/Saturn/Mars evidence: sleep, routine, check-up, reduced overload, and professional care.
- Mantra, dana, vrata, seva, or prayer can be mentioned only as optional cultural/spiritual support aligned with the stressed graha; never guarantee results or replace practical action.

## Output Contract

For remedy/mitigation answers, include:

- one stressed-bhava/karaka line naming the exact problem area,
- one pressure-source line naming dusthana/maraka/affliction/dasha/gocara evidence,
- one support-resource line naming Lagna/Moon/graha/varga/bala/ashtakavarga support,
- three concrete mitigation steps tied to chart evidence,
- one optional spiritual/upaya support line if relevant and phrased as non-guaranteed,
- one timing/precision line saying dasha/gocara/month/day/no-time support or unavailable.
