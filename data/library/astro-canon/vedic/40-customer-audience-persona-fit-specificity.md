# Jyotish Customer, Audience, And Persona-Fit Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Vedic/Jyotish. It condenses Parashari/Jataka, dasha, varga, ashtakavarga, gochara, and licensed-internal operational rules. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR/source text, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks who to sell to, which customer group fits them, who should be their target audience, what client persona is suitable, what type of followers/subscribers/customers convert, or which customers should be avoided.

Do not treat customer fit as the same thing as marketing traffic. Split audience persona, trust path, buying motive, conversion channel, repeat behavior, team/service burden, money quality, and timing.

## Persona Chain

1. **Lagna/Lagnesha and Moon**: delivery style, pressure tolerance, mental fit, and customer temperament the native can handle. If no birth time, use Moon/graha condition only.
2. **7th bhava/lord and Venus/Jupiter where relevant**: direct clients, buyers, contracts, one-to-one customer relationship, and trust before payment.
3. **11th bhava/lord**: audience, community, subscribers, network gains, referrals, platforms, and social proof.
4. **3rd / Mercury / 2nd speech**: message, copy, chat, sales script, documentation, teaching, content, code/spec handoff, and how customers understand the offer.
5. **10th bhava/D10/Sun**: public authority, professional status, institutional buyer, B2B/B2G, and expertise-led conversion.
6. **2nd / 11th / 8th**: buyer budget, cash retention, pricing power, debt/refund/shared-money risk, and whether customers leave money behind.
7. **6th / 12th**: service load, support burden, hidden labor, staff/process strain, complaint handling, and sleep/recovery cost.
8. **Nakshatra/varga/bala/ashtakavarga**: customer style and capacity only after bhava/lord/graha facts are anchored.
9. **Dasha/gocara**: decides when to target a new segment, raise buyer quality, narrow persona, or stop serving a draining group.

## Customer Type Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic graha keywords:

- **7th strong and clean**: direct client, one-to-one service, premium buyer, contract buyer, referral sale.
- **11th strong and clean**: community, membership, subscriber, followers, group buyer, platform audience.
- **3rd/Mercury/2nd strong**: customers who need explanation, education, documentation, content, chat, consulting, code/spec clarity.
- **10th/D10/Sun strong**: professional, institutional, executive, status-sensitive, enterprise, regulated, authority-seeking buyer.
- **2nd/11th strong**: customers with budget, repeat purchase, retained subscription, product/data/property value, stored value.
- **4th/12th strong**: home, care, retreat, private, institutional, foreign/remote, archive/data, sleep/rest or hidden-work markets.
- **6th/8th/12th/Saturn-Mars-Rahu stress**: avoid high-maintenance, high-liability, refund-prone, vague-scope, emergency, or underpriced customers.

## No-Time Rule

If birth time is missing, close 7th/11th/10th/house-lord/D10 claims unless the packet supplies reliable fallback. Use Moon, graha dignity, nakshatra, retrograde/combustion, drishti if supplied, and broad dasha only when available; frame persona fit as lower precision.

## Verdict Rules

- If 7th is stronger than 11th, prefer fewer high-trust buyers over mass audience.
- If 11th is stronger than 7th, build community/membership/platform but protect service capacity.
- If 3rd/Mercury/2nd is weak, avoid customers who need heavy explanation unless documentation or sales script is fixed first.
- If 2nd/11th is good but 6th/8th/12th repeats, customers pay but drain operations; narrow scope, require deposits, or cap support.
- If 10th/D10 is good but 7th weak, authority can rise while conversion remains weak; do not scale ads before offer trust is fixed.

## Output Contract

Every answer using this pack must include:

- one Lagna/Moon or no-time substitute delivery-style line,
- one 7th direct-customer or counterparty line,
- one 11th community/platform audience line,
- one 3rd/Mercury/2nd message/sales-script/documentation line,
- one 10th/D10 authority/status buyer line,
- one 2nd/11th/8th buyer-budget and retention-value line,
- one 6th/12th customer-risk or support-burden line,
- one dasha/gocara timing line when relevant,
- one concrete persona call: target premium clients / target community buyers / target institutions / target foreign or online buyers / sell through referrals / sell through education / avoid mass market / avoid high-maintenance clients / narrow the offer / require deposit / wait.
