# Jyotish Employment, Job Offer, Interview, Promotion, And Salary Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Vedic/Jyotish. Use as operational rules only; do not expose raw OCR, licensed prose, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack for job applications, interviews, job offers, accepting work, promotions, title changes, salary negotiation, boss/workplace, resignation, transfer, contract roles, and employment timing.

Do not answer employment from the 10th bhava alone. Split role/status, service conditions, salary, interview/documents, employer/counterparty, team/network, dasha, and gochara.

## Employment Chain

Read in this order:

1. **Lagna/Lagnesha or no-time substitute + Moon**: capacity to act and handle job pressure.
2. **10th bhava/lord + D10 + Sun/Saturn**: profession, role, title, promotion, employer structure, authority.
3. **6th bhava/lord**: employment/service, daily work, coworkers, competition, workload, conflict.
4. **2nd bhava/lord**: salary, cash held, speech in negotiation.
5. **11th bhava/lord**: gains, increment, referrals, network support, repeated income.
6. **3rd bhava/lord + Mercury**: application, interview, resume, communication, shortlisting, negotiation.
7. **7th bhava/lord**: employer/counterparty, contract role, consultant/client-facing work.
8. **9th/12th/4th**: foreign company, relocation, remote/backstage work, workplace base.
9. **Dasha + gochara + shadbala/ashtakavarga**: whether the period can carry the job move.

## Topic Rules

- **Interview / application**: 3rd/Mercury plus 10th/D10 employer lane and dasha/gocara.
- **Job offer / accept role**: 10th role + 7th contract/employer + 2nd/11th salary/gain + 6th workload.
- **Promotion / title**: 10th/D10, 11th gains, Sun/Saturn/Jupiter support, dasha/gocara confirmation.
- **Salary negotiation**: 2nd/11th first, then 10th authority, 7th counterparty, 3rd speech/communication.
- **Resignation / job change**: compare 6th/10th pressure in current role with 10th/11th/2nd support for the next role.

## No-Time Mode

If `hasBirthTime=false`, close Lagna-dependent bhava/lord/D10/dasha-heavy claims unless a valid fallback is supplied. Use Moon, Mercury/Sun/Saturn/Jupiter/Venus condition, planetary aspects, and nakshatra only when stable. State that employment timing is limited.

- one exact employment-action line naming apply/interview/offer/promotion/salary/resignation,

- one 6th workload-service-coworker line,
- one 2nd/11th salary-gains line,
