# Jyotish Business, Investment, And Contract Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Vedic/Jyotish. Use as operational rules only; do not expose raw OCR, licensed prose, page order, or private paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack for business, opening a shop, investment, buying/selling, contracts, clients, partners, suppliers, loans, expansion, pricing, and trade decisions.

Do not answer business from the 2nd or 10th alone. Split work channel, cashflow, gains, speculation, contracts/partners, debt/shared money, dasha, and gochara.

## Business Chain

Read in this order:

1. **Lagna/Lagnesha or no-time substitute**: capacity to act and carry risk.
2. **10th bhava/lord + D10**: business role, operations, status, duty.
3. **2nd bhava/lord**: cash held, savings, speech/pricing, family capital.
4. **11th bhava/lord**: gains, customers, network, recurring profit.
5. **7th bhava/lord + Venus/Mercury/Jupiter as relevant**: trade, clients, counterparties, business partner, agreement.
6. **5th/9th**: speculation, intelligence, luck, knowledge products, patronage.
7. **8th/12th**: loans, tax, shared money, hidden loss, foreign/outflow, leverage.
8. **Dasha + gochara + ashtakavarga/shadbala**: whether the period can carry the action.

## Action Calls

- **go**: 10th/2nd/11th/7th repeat support, dasha lord connects to business lanes, and gochara confirms.
- **conditional go**: business promise exists but dusthana, weak bala, debt/leverage, or partner affliction requires safeguards.
- **wait/prepare**: natal promise exists but dasha/gocara or partner/debt lane is not ready.
- **no**: repeated contradiction from topic bhava/lord, dasha lord, risk houses, and gochara.

## Contract And Investment Rules

- 7th in business questions = trade/counterparty/client/contract partner. Do not use marriage language unless asked.
- 8th = loans, investor/shared money, tax, debt, sudden reversal, and leverage.
- 11th strong with 2nd weak = gains possible but saving/retention must be disciplined.
- 5th speculation needs support from 2nd/11th, dasha, bala, and no severe 8th/12th contradiction.
- Rahu in money lanes can show digital/foreign/unusual gain and volatility; it is not stable profit by itself.
- Do not give buy/sell/hold, price target, guaranteed profit, or portfolio sizing.

## Output Contract

Every business answer must include:

- one business-action line naming the exact action and bhava/karaka,
- one 10th/D10 operating-channel line,
- one 2nd/11th/5th/9th cashflow-gain-speculation line,
- one 7th/8th/12th contract-debt-risk line,
- one dasha/gochara/ashtakavarga timing line when supplied,
- one concrete call: go / conditional go / wait / no,
- one practical control: cash reserve, staged investment, contract review, partner boundary, accounting, or compliance step.
