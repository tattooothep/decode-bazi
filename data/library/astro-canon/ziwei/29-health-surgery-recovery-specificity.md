# Zi Wei Health, Surgery, Accident, Stress, And Recovery Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Zi Wei. It condenses public-domain Zi Wei witnesses, Daoist Canon/Quanshu timing summaries, Feixing Cetian extracted rules, and modern licensed collation into operational reading rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR, page order, private paths, or source prose.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about health, fatigue, chronic pressure, sleep, stress, accident risk, surgery timing, hospital/rest, check-up, recovery, whether to push work, or how to manage body strain.

This is symbolic risk-management, not medical diagnosis. If the user asks for diagnosis, medication, surgery instruction, or emergency triage, direct them to medical professionals while keeping the chart reading to timing/routine caution.

## Evidence Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: constitution, focus of the body/life, capacity to carry the period.
2. **疾厄**: health pressure, illness symbolism, injury, hidden vulnerability, medical/routine burden.
3. **福德**: sleep, stress, mind, rest, recovery quality.
4. **官祿 / 僕役**: work/service load, team burden, repetitive strain, pressure from duty.
5. **遷移**: travel, commute, road/movement risk, hospital/outside-place trigger.
6. **父母**: doctors, institutions, reports, paperwork, insurance/official medical documents when relevant.
7. **田宅**: home recovery environment, rest base, family caretaking pressure.
8. **三方四正**: topic support and pressure around 疾厄/命身/福德.
9. **四化 and malefics**: 化忌/羊陀火鈴/空劫/天刑/大耗 mark pressure, delay, procedure, leak, shock, or burden; 祿權科/輔弼/昌曲/魁鉞 can show support, clarity, helper, or competent care when tied to the relevant palace.
10. **大限 / 流年 / 流月 / 流日**: timing activation only when supplied.

## Health Topic Rules

Separate:

- vitality/stamina,
- sleep and mental stress,
- workload/routine pressure,
- accident or travel/commute strain,
- chronic pressure versus acute flare,
- recovery environment,
- whether to continue, reduce load, check up, rest, or delay.

Do not name a fixed disease from one star, one 化忌, or one annual star. If the packet only shows pressure, translate it into concrete management advice.

## Surgery / Treatment / Hospital Guard

When the question asks about surgery, treatment, hospital, or procedure timing:

- judge symbolic timing ease/pressure only,
- name 疾厄, 命身, 福德, 父母, 遷移, and timing evidence,
- never choose a procedure, medication, diagnosis, dosage, or emergency instruction,
- do not override a clinician,
- give a practical call such as prepare documents, seek second opinion, reduce risk, avoid rushing if timing is pressured, or proceed only under medical clearance.

## Output Contract

Every answer using this pack must include:

- one 命宮/身宮 carrying-capacity line,
- one 疾厄 body-pressure line,
- one 福德 sleep/stress/recovery line,
- one 官祿/僕役 workload-routine line when work or overuse is relevant,
- one 父母 doctor/document/institution line when treatment/procedure is relevant,
- one 遷移 accident/travel/hospital-outside-place line when movement or procedure is relevant,
- one 大限/流年/流月/流日 timing line when a year/month/date is asked,
- one medical-safety line: symbolic reading only, use clinicians for diagnosis/treatment,
- one decisive practical call: push / reduce load / rest / check up / prepare / postpone / proceed only with medical clearance.
