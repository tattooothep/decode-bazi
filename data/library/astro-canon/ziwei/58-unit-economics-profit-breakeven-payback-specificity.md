# Zi Wei Unit Economics, Profit-Margin, Break-Even, And Payback Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about profit quality, margin, unit economics, break-even, payback, LTV/CAC, contribution margin, gross/net profit, cost per order, cost per customer, ARPU/AOV, margin floor, or whether a business model is economically worth doing.

Do not treat unit economics as the same thing as cashflow/runway, price strategy, windfall/speculation, marketing campaign optimization, retention/churn rescue, sales-team QA, team compensation, partner-risk, or technology build. If the user asks "cash left / reserve / runway / burn rate", use the cashflow pack. If the user asks "raise price / discount / coupon / upsell", use the pricing pack. If the user asks "CAC/ROAS for ad creative or funnel traffic", use the marketing pack. This pack is for whether each sale, cohort, customer, product, or plan is profitable after real costs.

## Canon Routing

Zi Wei profit judgment must join 財帛 with 官祿, 田宅, 兄弟/父母/夫妻, 僕役, 疾厄, and 四化. A strong revenue palace does not prove profit if 僕役, 疾厄, 化忌, or 煞曜 drain the model.

- 命宮/身宮: whether the native can hold discipline, simplify the model, and stop vanity revenue.
- 財帛: revenue per sale, margin quality, collection, and whether money retained is real.
- 官祿: the business model, delivery machine, monetization structure, and profit engine.
- 田宅: fixed-cost base, retained assets, inventory/office/platform base, and break-even absorption.
- 兄弟/父母/夫妻: messages, written terms, invoices, contracts, buyer promise, channel terms, and documentation.
- 僕役: staff, contractor, vendor, tool, AI, SaaS, support, fulfillment, and variable cost per unit.
- 疾厄/化忌/煞曜: refund drag, tax drag, rework, hidden labor, bad debt, complaint, leakage, and margin killers.
- 大限/流年/流月/流日/四化: when the profit model can be tightened, tested, or paused.

## Answer Contract

For unit economics questions, answer with:

1. one 命宮/身宮 margin-discipline line;
2. one 財帛 revenue-per-unit and collection-quality line;
3. one 官祿 profit-model or operating-engine line;
4. one 田宅 fixed-cost/break-even-base line;
5. one 兄弟/父母/夫妻 price-term/invoice/channel-promise line;
6. one 僕役 variable-cost/staff/tool/vendor line;
7. one 疾厄/化忌/煞曜 hidden-cost/refund/tax/rework-risk line;
8. one timing line from 大限/流年/流月/流日/四化 when available;
9. one practical verdict: raise margin floor, stop an unprofitable SKU, repackage, cap variable cost, collect before delivery, or test smaller.

Never let 化祿 or a strong 財帛 palace hide a bad cost structure. If 化忌, 煞曜, or 僕役 pressure attacks the model, name exactly which cost or promise must be cut before growth.
