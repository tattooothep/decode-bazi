## Purpose

Use this pack for subscription, membership, renewal, repeat purchase, refund, cancellation, chargeback, customer churn, recurring revenue, retained customer base, customer service burden, and "will users stay or cancel" questions.

Do not answer from sales or popularity alone. Split new customer acquisition, renewal, refund pressure, customer-service load, cash retention, and timing.

## Core Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: service-pressure capacity and whether customer load becomes personal stress.
2. **夫妻**: customer, subscriber, sponsor, platform, contract, refund opponent, or payment counterparty.
3. **財帛 / 田宅**: payment received versus money/customer base retained; 田宅 is the stored base and recurring stability.
4. **僕役 / 福德**: user community, support load, customer mood, staff/helpers, and emotional burden.
5. **兄弟**: support messages, billing explanation, replies, help docs, comments, and dispute communication.
6. **官祿**: product/service promise, role, delivery quality, brand obligation, and whether the offer matches expectation.
7. **疾厄 / 化忌 / 煞曜**: complaint pressure, service fatigue, refund leakage, hidden defect, and stress.
8. **Timing**: 大限/流年/流月/流日 only when supplied.

## Retention-Chain Rule

New sales are not retention. Require a chain:

- 官祿/兄弟 = offer promise and support communication,
- 夫妻 = customer/subscriber/platform relation,
- 僕役/福德 = user mood and support capacity,
- 財帛 = payment received,
- 田宅 = money/customer base retained,
- 疾厄/化忌/煞曜 = refund, fatigue, complaint, or hidden leak.

If 財帛 is supported but 田宅 is damaged, say "sales without clean retention." If 夫妻 is damaged, customer/platform relation drives churn. If 兄弟 is damaged, billing explanation and support scripts need repair before scaling.

## Refund / Cancellation Rule

For refund/cancel/chargeback questions, name the pressure:

- **billing confusion**: 兄弟 + 財帛,
- **customer dissatisfaction**: 夫妻 + 官祿,
- **community pile-up**: 僕役/福德,
- **cash leakage**: 財帛 + 田宅 + 大耗/化忌,
- **hidden service defect**: 疾厄/化忌/空劫.

Do not promise zero refunds. Give a symbolic risk level and a practical retention action.
