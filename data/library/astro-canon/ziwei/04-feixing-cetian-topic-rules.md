## Verdict Weighting

A strong Zi Wei verdict needs repeated evidence. Do not judge from one star alone.

Weight order:
1. 命宮/身宮 and their 三方四正 define the person's life frame.
2. The asked palace defines the topic.
3. 生年四化 shows lifelong pattern.
4. 大限宮 and 大限四化 show the current decade-stage.
5. 流年命宮, 流年四化, and 流年星 show the year trigger.
6. 流月/流日 are short-window triggers only when supplied by packet.

Verdict strength:
- High confidence: natal palace + 三方四正 + 大限/流年 repeat the same direction.
- Medium confidence: topic palace is clear but timing layer is mixed.
- Low confidence: only one minor star or one annual star supports the claim.

If good and bad signs coexist, split them:
- "แรงหนุน" = what helps.
- "แรงเสียดทาน" = what blocks or stresses.
- "ข้อสรุป" = which side is stronger after timing is considered.

## Four Transformations In Practice

化祿:
- Increases flow, desire, opportunity, income chance, social ease, or attraction.
- Good when it supports 命/身, 財帛, 官祿, 田宅, 福德, or the asked palace.
- Risk: creates appetite and attachment; not always clean gain.

化權:
- Increases command, pressure, movement, competition, responsibility, control.
- Good for career, status, leadership, decisive action.
- Risk: power struggle, impatience, emotional pressure, conflict if 煞星 repeat.

化科:
- Protects through name, paperwork, exams, formal recognition, reputation, mentor support.
- Good for study, documents, official matters, public image, repair after trouble.
- Risk: slower than 化祿/化權; more formal than emotional.

化忌:
- Creates attachment, blockage, debt, worry, delay, entanglement, or a point that cannot be ignored.
- It is not automatic disaster. It becomes heavy when repeated by 大限/流年 or hit by 羊陀火鈴/空劫.
- For user advice, translate it into the real-life knot that needs management.

Layer rule:
- 生年忌 = lifelong knot.
- 大限忌 = decade-stage knot.
- 流年忌 = year trigger.
- When the same topic receives two or three 忌 layers, the answer should be direct and cautious.

Good relationship signs:
- 夫妻 has stable main stars with support from 吉星 or 化祿/化科.
- 夫妻 and 命/福德 support each other.
- partner-related 四化 brings 祿/科 to 命, 夫妻, 福德, or 田宅.
- pair packet shows spouse palace to life palace relation plus supportive crossSiHua.

Stress relationship signs:

- 羊陀火鈴/空劫/天刑 cluster at 夫妻, 命, 福德, or 流年 trigger.

- pair packet shows support but also strong cross 忌/pressure; report both, do not let one nice match erase the stress.

Answer style:

- Do not declare marriage/divorce from one star, one 紅鸞/天喜, or one crossSiHua line.

4. 化權/化科 for authority and formal recognition.

Career support:

- 官祿 linked with 財帛/田宅/F福德 by support = work can convert into money/security.

Career stress:

- 羊陀火鈴 with 官祿 = sharp pressure, conflict, urgency, restructuring.

Answer style:
- If 官祿 has both support and pressure, call it "โตจากภาระ" rather than purely good or bad.
- If timing supports action but natal career palace is weak, recommend controlled steps, not a reckless leap.

## Money 財帛

Read money in this order:
1. 財帛宮 and its 三方四正.
2. 田宅 for assets, home, property, storage.
3. 福德 for spending style, enjoyment, long-term comfort.
4. 官祿 for income source through work.
5. 化祿/祿存 as gain signal; 化忌/空劫 as leakage or stuck money.

Money support:
- 財帛/田宅/官祿 repeat support = money has a channel.
- 化祿 or 祿存 in 財帛/田宅/官祿/福德 = flow, gain, useful resource.

Money stress:
- 財帛 化忌 = cash-flow worry, debt, sticky obligation, money tied to a person/topic.
- 空劫 at 財帛/田宅 = hollow promise, sudden gap, unstable value.
- 煞星 plus 忌 = pressure cost, penalties, repair expense, conflict over money.

Answer style:
- Separate "หาเงินได้ไหม" from "เก็บเงินอยู่ไหม".
- Do not call wealth from 化祿 alone if 財帛 is weak and leakage repeats.
- Do not call ruin from 化忌 alone if 田宅/官祿/福德 strongly protect it.

## Timing Rules

For any timing answer:
1. Check natal promise first. If natal topic is absent, timing trigger may still create an event but weaker.
2. Check current/queried 大限. The decade must be named in the reasoning if the answer is important.
3. Check 流年. The year is active when 流年命宮, 流年四化, or 流年星 touches the asked palace or 命/身.
4. Check 流月/流日 only if packet supplies them.
5. Give action timing as "เหมาะ/ไม่เหมาะ/ระวัง" not absolute fate.

High-probability timing:
- natal topic + 大限 + 流年 repeat the same palace/topic.
- 化忌 repeats across layers for a blockage.
- 化祿/權/科 repeats across layers for gain/action/recognition.
- annual stars land directly in asked palace or 命/身.

Low-probability timing:
- only one 流月/流日 contact without natal/decade/year support.
- romantic annual star without 夫妻/命/福德 support.
- money star without 財帛/田宅/官祿 support.

## Pair Mode Rules

Use only the supplied pair packet. Do not derive additional cross-chart reactions.

Read pair in this order:
1. Each person's own 命宮/身宮 and 夫妻宮.
2. 命宮 relation between A and B.
3. A 夫妻 to B 命, and B 夫妻 to A 命.
4. crossSiHua: whose 化祿/權/科/忌 lands where in the other chart.
5. focal palace star overlap for 命, 夫妻, 財帛, 官祿, 福德.
6. Each person's 大限 window; a good match needs timing readiness, not only natal attraction.

Pair verdict rule:
- Good match: spouse-life relation plus supportive crossSiHua plus tolerable personal charts.
- Karmic/sticky match: strong attraction plus 化忌/煞 pressure.
- Uneven match: one chart receives support but the other receives pressure; say this plainly.
- Do not conclude "แต่งได้แน่" or "เลิกแน่" from one overlay.
