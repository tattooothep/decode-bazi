# Zi Wei Property, Vehicle, Residence, And Relocation Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Zi Wei. It condenses public-domain Zi Wei witnesses, Daoist Canon/Quanshu timing summaries, Feixing Cetian extracted rules, and modern licensed collation into operational reading rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR, page order, private paths, or source prose.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about buying, selling, renting, building, renovating, land, house, condo, shopfront, vehicle, mortgage, title deed, moving home, relocation, or whether a residence/property is good for them.

Do not answer from 田宅 alone. A serious property answer must separate residence quality, money, documents/counterparty, timing, and the pressure of moving.

## Evidence Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: whether the native can carry the decision and disruption.
2. **田宅**: property, residence, land, stored asset, family root, repair/hidden condition.
3. **財帛**: affordability, cashflow, debt pressure, resale/gain potential.
4. **官祿**: work income supporting the asset, shopfront/workplace use, public status.
5. **父母**: title deed, bank documents, permits, official papers, elder authority.
6. **夫妻 / 僕役**: seller, buyer, landlord, tenant, agent, bank, contractor, counterparty.
7. **遷移**: relocation, distance, commute, vehicle movement, life outside the old base.
8. **疾厄 / 福德**: hidden defects, stress, sleep/rest comfort, maintenance burden.
9. **三方四正 + 四化**: topic support/friction; use 生年, 大限, 流年, 流月/流日 only when supplied.

## Property / Land / House

Read 田宅 as the asset/root, then 財帛 for funding and 父母/夫妻/僕役 for papers and counterparties.

Separate:

- asset quality,
- affordability,
- debt/mortgage pressure,
- document/permit risk,
- seller/agent/bank friction,
- repair or hidden defect,
- whether the place improves 福德/rest.

化祿/祿存/化科 can ease money or paperwork only when tied to the relevant palace. 化忌/空劫/大耗/煞 in the asset, money, or paper lane points to delay, leak, repair, or dispute.

## Vehicle / Commute

Treat vehicle as 田宅-owned asset plus 遷移 movement. Use 財帛 for payment and 疾厄 for accident/maintenance stress.

Separate:

- practical benefit,
- payment pressure,
- repair/maintenance,
- road/commute pressure,
- whether to buy, repair, postpone, or reduce budget.

## Relocation / Moving Home

Use 田宅 and 遷移 together. 田宅 shows base quality; 遷移 shows outside pull and distance. 官祿 and 財帛 decide whether the move is useful and affordable.

Separate work move, family move, foreign/distant move, temporary move, and permanent base.

## Output Contract

Every answer using this pack must include:

- one 命宮/身宮 carrying-capacity line,
- one 田宅 asset/base line,
- one 財帛 affordability/debt line,
- one 父母 or 夫妻/僕役 document-counterparty line for purchase/sale/lease,
- one 遷移 relocation/vehicle/commute line when relevant,
- one 四化/三方四正 support-versus-friction line,
- one timing line when a year/month/date is asked,
- one decisive practical call: buy / buy with conditions / rent / wait / do not buy / sell / renovate / move / stay.
