# Zi Wei Children, Pregnancy, Protégé, And Creative Output Specificity Pack

Derived operational summary for children, pregnancy/fertility symbolism, younger people, students/protégés, creative output, and side-project questions in 紫微斗數. Use only as internal interpretation rules; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope

Use this pack when the question asks about having children, pregnancy timing, fertility tendency, parenting, existing children, younger people, students/protégés, creative work, authored output, performance, content, or a personal project.

Medical policy: Zi Wei in Fusion may judge symbolic timing, pressure, readiness, support, and risk-management. It must not diagnose infertility, pregnancy, fetal condition, miscarriage, or medical outcome. Require medical confirmation for medical claims.

## Evidence Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: baseline capacity, stress tolerance, and life focus.
2. **子女**: first palace for children, pregnancy symbolism, younger people, students/protégés, produced work, creative output, and side project.
3. **夫妻**: partner cooperation, relationship readiness, disagreement about children, and shared responsibility.
4. **福德**: joy, pleasure, rest, emotional readiness, fertility symbolism, mental pressure, and the ability to sustain care.
5. **疾厄**: body/stress/recovery pressure. Use as symbolic risk-management only; do not diagnose.
6. **田宅 / 財帛**: home readiness, family base, childcare cost, resources, project budget, studio or production base.
7. **官祿 / 兄弟 / 遷移**: public launch, content distribution, teaching, audience, younger team, documents, outside reach.
8. **文昌/文曲/化科/祿權科/魁鉞 if supplied**: polish, recognition, child/project support, and help from teachers/seniors.
9. **大限 / 流年 / 流月 / 流日 / 四化**: timing activation. If month/day are absent, answer at the available decade/year layer.

## Reading Rules

- Do not promise a child from 子女 alone. Require 子女, 命身 capacity, 四化/三方四正, support/friction, and timing.
- For pregnancy/fertility questions, separate symbolic child potential, partner cooperation, body/rest pressure, home/money readiness, and timing. Add medical-confirmation guard once.
- For existing children, separate closeness, worry, cost, discipline, education, and distance from the child.
- For creative/project output, read 子女 as output, 兄弟 as content/communication, 官祿 as public status, 財帛 as money return, 遷移 as outside audience, and 福德 as creative joy.
- 化忌 or strong malefic pressure in 子女/福德/疾厄 should become a practical bottleneck: delay, stress, medical check, cost, partner conflict, child worry, or production rework.

## Answer Contract

For a children/pregnancy/creative-output question, write:
1. one 命身 capacity line,
2. one 子女 children/output/protégé line,
3. one 夫妻 partner-cooperation line when relevant,
4. one 福德 joy/rest/emotional-readiness line,
5. one 疾厄 body-pressure line for pregnancy/fertility questions with a medical-confirmation guard,
6. one 田宅/財帛/官祿/兄弟/遷移 practical support line matching the question,
7. one 四化/timing line,
8. one decisive practical call: try now / try with conditions / prepare first / wait / focus on project launch / delay launch / get medical or professional confirmation first.
