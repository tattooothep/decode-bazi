## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks who to sell to, which customer group fits them, who should be their target audience, what client persona is suitable, what type of followers/subscribers/customers convert, or which customers should be avoided.

Do not treat customer fit as the same thing as marketing traffic. Split audience persona, trust path, buying motive, conversion channel, repeat behavior, team/service burden, money quality, and timing.

## Persona Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: delivery style, pressure tolerance, and the customer temperament the native can handle without losing themselves.
2. **夫妻**: direct clients, buyers, one-to-one customer relationship, contract counterparties, and whether sales require trust before payment.
3. **僕役**: community, group customer, subscriber base, followers, platforms, staff-supported service, vendors, and volume audience.
4. **兄弟**: message, copy, chat, teaching, documentation, sales script, content, code/spec handoff, and how customers understand the offer.
5. **遷移 / 父母**: public reach, outside market, foreign/non-local customers, institutional trust, paperwork, platform authority, and brand credibility.
6. **官祿**: customer status level, professional authority, B2B/B2G, enterprise buyer, and whether customers buy because of expertise.
7. **財帛 / 田宅**: buyer budget, retained cash, subscription/stored value, property/data/product asset, and whether customers leave money behind.
8. **福德**: comfort, taste, emotional safety, spiritual/advisory value, luxury/inner-life market, and satisfaction.
9. **疾厄 / 化忌 / 煞曜**: complaint pattern, refund risk, burnout, hidden service cost, technical debt, and customers to avoid.
10. **大限 / 流年 / 四化**: when to target a new segment, raise buyer quality, narrow persona, or stop serving a draining group.

## Customer Type Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic star keywords:

- **夫妻 strong and clean**: direct client, one-to-one service, premium buyer, relationship-led sale, referral sale.
- **僕役 strong and clean**: group customer, community, membership, subscriber, platform audience, staff-assisted service.
- **兄弟/昌曲/化科 strong**: customers who need explanation, education, content, documentation, consultation, code/spec clarity.
- **遷移/父母 strong**: public/foreign/non-local/institutional customers; conversion from credibility, official trust, or outside reach.
- **官祿 strong**: professional, enterprise, authority-seeking, status-sensitive, regulated, executive, or institutional buyer.
- **財帛/田宅 strong**: customers with budget, asset purchase, retained subscription, property/data/product value, repeat stored value.
- **福德 strong**: wellness, comfort, spiritual, advisory, luxury, taste-led, emotionally safe customer segment.
- **疾厄/化忌/煞曜 stressed**: avoid clients who require emergency response, vague scope, conflict, refunds, underpriced service, or endless revisions.

## Verdict Rules

- If 夫妻 is stronger than 僕役, prefer fewer high-trust buyers over mass audience.
- If 僕役 is stronger than 夫妻, build community/membership/platform but protect service capacity.
- If 兄弟 is weak, avoid customers who need heavy explanation unless documentation or sales script is fixed first.
- If 財帛 looks good but 疾厄/化忌 repeats, customers pay but drain operations; narrow scope, require deposits, or cap support.
- If 遷移/父母 is good but 夫妻 weak, visibility or authority can rise while conversion remains weak; do not scale ads before offer trust is fixed.
