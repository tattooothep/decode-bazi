# Zi Wei Sales Team, Pipeline, QA, And Follow-Up Specificity Pack

Derived runtime rules for sales team management, CRM pipeline hygiene, lead follow-up, call-review QA, no-show recovery, lost-deal review, and sales coaching. Use as private internal guidance only; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope Gate

Do not treat sales team QA as the same thing as sales page/script writing, paid marketing traffic, hiring a sales role, team compensation/commission plan, customer success/renewal, post-sale support, affiliate/referral partner program, or partner due diligence. This pack is for the operating system after demand exists: lead routing, CRM stages, follow-up cadence, call quality, rep coaching, demo/consult attendance, no-show recovery, lost-deal review, and deciding whether the owner or reps should handle sales calls.

Trigger examples: sales team process, sales QA, CRM pipeline, lead follow-up, follow-up sequence, lead handoff, call review, call recording, lost deal review, no-show recovery, sales coaching, sales manager dashboard, pipeline hygiene, stage conversion, close rate, demo booking, consult follow-up, objection library in practice, sales rep quality.

## Evidence Chain

1. 命宮 / 身宮: owner sales-control, standard-setting, and whether the person should personally keep the close.
2. 官祿: sales authority, process standard, CRM discipline, manager dashboard, and final close governance.
3. 兄弟 / 父母: follow-up messages, call notes, written SOP, review scripts in practice, reminder cadence, lead handoff, and evidence trail.
4. 夫妻: lead/customer counterparty, consult/demo buyer readiness, objection pressure, closing friction, and no-show pattern.
5. 僕役: sales reps, setters/closers, team execution, call quality, training adoption, and whether reps follow the playbook.
6. 財帛 / 田宅: pipeline value, cash conversion, CRM/data asset, retained lead list, revenue forecast, and leakage between stage and cash.
7. 福德: trust climate, morale, referral readiness, buyer confidence after sales conversation, and whether pressure damages goodwill.
8. 疾厄 / 化忌 / 煞曜: mis-selling, overpromise, no-show, ghosting, lost deal, refund seed, compliance/liability, and internal conflict.
9. 大限 / 流年 / 流月 / 流日 / 四化: timing for owner-led calls, rep handoff, QA audit, pipeline cleanup, coaching sprint, or pausing team scale.

## Decision Vocabulary

Final call must choose a concrete sales-ops action: keep owner selling / assign sales rep only to qualified calls / implement CRM stages / daily pipeline review / call recording QA / coach reps / tighten lead handoff / add follow-up SLA / add no-show recovery / run lost-deal review / pause scaling sales team / move calls back to owner / replace rep / improve script before scaling.
