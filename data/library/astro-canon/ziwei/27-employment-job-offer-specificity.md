# 紫微斗數 · Employment, Job Offer, Interview, Promotion, And Salary Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Zi Wei. Use as operational rules only; do not expose raw OCR, page order, private paths, or source prose.

## Purpose

Use this pack for job applications, interviews, job offers, accepting work, promotions, title changes, salary negotiation, boss/workplace, resignation, transfer, contract roles, and employment timing.

Do not answer employment from 官祿 alone. Split role/status, salary, hiring document/interview, employer/contract, team climate, workload, and timing.

## Employment Chain

Read in this order:

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: capacity to carry the role and adapt to the workplace.
2. **官祿**: job role, title, status, promotion, employer structure, formal authority.
3. **財帛**: salary, compensation, bonus, cash movement.
4. **父母**: HR paperwork, offer letter, institution, certificate, approval, official document.
5. **兄弟**: interview, communication, messaging, internal coordination.
6. **夫妻 / 僕役**: employer/counterparty contract, hiring manager, team, coworkers, subordinates.
7. **疾厄 / 福德**: workload, stress, routine, psychological ease, burnout risk.
8. **遷移 / 田宅**: relocation, branch transfer, remote/foreign work, workplace base.
9. **生年四化 + 大限/流年/流月/流日四化 + annual stars**: what opens, formalizes, pressures, or blocks employment.

## Topic Rules

- **Interview / application**: 父母 paperwork + 兄弟 communication + 官祿 employer lane.
- **Job offer / accept role**: 官祿 role + 夫妻 contract/employer + 財帛 salary + 疾厄 workload.
- **Promotion / title**: 官祿 plus 化權/化科/魁鉞/昌曲 when supplied, then 大限/流年 support.
- **Salary negotiation**: 財帛 first, then 官祿 authority and 夫妻/兄弟 negotiation channel.
- **Resignation / job change**: compare current 官祿/疾厄 pressure with new opportunity from 遷移/財帛/timing.

## Action Calls

- **รับ/ลุย**: 官祿 and 財帛 support repeat, documents/counterparty are manageable, and timing opens work/status.
- **รับแบบมีเงื่อนไข**: role is real but 忌/煞/空劫/大耗 hits salary, team, document, or workload lanes.
- **ต่อรอง/รอผลก่อน**: interview/document lane is active but still unsettled.
- **ไม่ควรรับ/ยังไม่ควรลาออก**: role, salary, workload, and timing repeat pressure without enough support.

## Output Contract

Every employment answer must include:

- one exact employment-action line naming apply/interview/offer/promotion/salary/resignation,
- one 官祿 role-status-employer line,
- one 財帛 salary/compensation line,
- one 父母/兄弟 interview-document-communication line,
- one 夫妻/僕役 employer-contract-team line,
- one 疾厄/福德 workload-stress line when relevant,
- one 大限/流年/流月/流日/四化 timing line when supplied,
- one concrete call: รับ/ลุย / รับแบบมีเงื่อนไข / ต่อรองหรือรอผลก่อน / ไม่ควรรับหรือยังไม่ควรลาออก.
