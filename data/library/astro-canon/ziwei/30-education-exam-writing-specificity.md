# Zi Wei Education, Exam, Certification, Writing, And Learning Specificity Pack

Derived operational summary for education, exam/certification, writing/speaking/content, teacher/support, cost, and timing questions in 紫微斗數. Use only as internal interpretation rules; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope

Use this pack when the question asks about school, university, admission, course, certification, license exam, thesis, research, scholarship, grades, exam pass/fail, writing, presentation, teaching, content, document submission, portfolio, mentor, or teacher support.

If the same question also asks about government approval, visa, lawsuit, tax, or formal legal paperwork, also use the authority/document pack. If it asks about job interview or promotion, also use the employment pack. If it asks about foreign study, keep 遷移 in the chain but do not replace education with generic travel.

## Evidence Chain

Use 父母, 兄弟, 官祿, 福德, 財帛, 遷移, 文昌文曲化科, and timing in this order:

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: baseline ability, temperament, pressure tolerance, and whether the person can carry the chosen study path.
2. **父母**: school, institution, teacher, authority, formal documents, certificates, examination board, official letter, visa/school paper, and approval channel.
3. **兄弟**: classmates, communication, writing, speaking, presentation, short documents, teamwork in study, and evidence exchange.
4. **官祿**: exam result as status, degree/certificate as career authority, public evaluation, license value, and whether study converts into professional standing.
5. **福德**: focus, memory, sleep, mood, inner pressure, burnout, and long preparation rhythm.
6. **財帛**: tuition, scholarship, cost, funding gap, exam fee, course investment, and whether money pressure blocks the path.
7. **遷移**: foreign school, online/outside institution, travel for exam, outside audience, publishing beyond home base.
8. **文昌 / 文曲 / 化科 / 魁鉞 if supplied**: academic polish, writing, paper quality, exam clarity, teacher recognition, and formal reputation. Use them only if the packet supplies them.
9. **大限 / 流年 / 流月 / 流日 / 四化**: timing permission. If month/day are absent, answer at decade/year precision only.

## Reading Rules

- Do not call an exam result from one star alone. Require palace, 三方四正 or 四化, and timing.
- For exam pass/fail, 父母 shows institution/document/exam board, 兄弟 shows writing/answer/communication, 官祿 shows formal result/status. All three must be separated.
- For writing/content/presentation, 兄弟 and 文昌/文曲 are first, 官祿 is public result, 福德 is consistency, and 遷移 is outside reach.
- For scholarship/course cost, 財帛 must be named. Strong academic signs with weak 財帛 means "can study, but budget or scholarship plan is the condition."
- For teacher/mentor help, use 父母/魁鉞/support stars only if supplied. Do not invent a nobleman.
- If 化忌 hits the education lane, specify the practical bottleneck: documents, anxiety, communication, cost, teacher friction, delay, or retake.

## Answer Contract

For an education/exam/writing question, write:
1. one 命身 capacity line,
2. one 父母 institution/document/exam-board line,
3. one 兄弟 writing/communication/preparation line,
4. one 官祿 result/certification/status line,
5. one 福德 focus/stress/rest line if relevant,
6. one 財帛/遷移/timing condition line,
7. one decisive practical call: take exam / submit / publish / enroll / apply scholarship / retake / wait / change course.

If timing is incomplete, say the exact missing timing layer once and still answer from the available 大限/流年 layer.
