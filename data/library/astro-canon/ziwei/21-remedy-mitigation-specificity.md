## Purpose

## Mitigation Chain

1. Identify the stressed topic palace: 官祿, 財帛/田宅, 夫妻, 疾厄, 遷移, 兄弟/僕役/父母, 福德, or 命/身.
2. Identify pressure source: 化忌, 煞曜, 空劫, 刑耗, weak palace, weak 主星, repeated clash in 三方四正, 大限, 流年, 流月, or 流日.
3. Identify available support: 化祿, 化權, 化科, strong 主星, helpful 三方四正, supportive palace, 吉曜, 大限/流年 support, or pair-packet support in relationship questions.
4. Convert into mitigation: reduce exposure to the stressed palace, use the support palace/star, and time action away from repeated 忌/煞 triggers when possible.
5. If month/day fields are unavailable, give decade/year-level mitigation only and do not invent auspicious dates.

## Remedy Rules

- For work stress, use 官祿 and 父母/僕役 support: role clarity, documentation, institutional backing, and controlled visibility.
- For money risk, use 財帛/田宅: reserves, contract review, staged spending/investment, and avoiding all-in decisions when 忌/耗 repeats.
- For relationship stress, use 夫妻/福德/命身 and pair packet: boundaries, pacing, repair conversation, and third-party clarity when needed.
- For health/routine pressure, use 疾厄/福德/命身: rest, routine, check-up, emotional decompression, and professional care.
- Spiritual practice can be framed as calming 福德/心性 support only; never as guaranteed cure.
