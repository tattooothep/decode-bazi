## Authority And Document Chain

Read in this order:

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: ability to face authority, negotiate, and carry the process.
2. **父母**: documents, official papers, institutions, elders, certifications, approvals, bureaucracy.
3. **官祿**: office authority, career status, regulator, court/administrative power, public rank.
4. **夫妻**: contract, one-to-one counterparty, open opponent, agency-facing agreement.
5. **兄弟 / 僕役**: communication, agents, staff, team, witnesses, operational handling.
6. **財帛 / 田宅**: fees, tax, collateral, property, retained asset, money affected by the document.
7. **疾厄 / 福德**: hidden stress, delay, health/routine burden, reserve and mental capacity.
8. **生年四化 + 大限/流年/流月/流日四化 + annual stars**: what opens, formalizes, delays, or blocks the approval.

## Topic Rules

- **Visa / immigration / relocation permit**: read 父母 + 遷移 + 官祿, then money/fees and timing.
- **License / approval / certification**: read 父母 first, then 官祿, 文昌/文曲/化科 when supplied, and 忌/煞/空劫 blockers.
- **Court / dispute / official pressure**: read 官祿 + 夫妻 + 父母 + 疾厄; do not declare victory from one support marker.
- **Tax / inheritance / property documents**: read 財帛 + 田宅 + 父母 + 官祿, separating payment pressure from asset outcome.
- **Contract signing**: read 夫妻 as counterparty/contract, 父母 as document, 官祿 as enforceability, 僕役/兄弟 as handlers.
