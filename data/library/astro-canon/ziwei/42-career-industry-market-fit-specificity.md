# Zi Wei Career, Industry, Market, And Role-Fit Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Zi Wei. It condenses public-domain Zi Wei witnesses, Daoist Canon/Quanshu timing summaries, Feixing Cetian extracted rules, and modern licensed collation into operational reading rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR, page order, private paths, or source prose.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks what career, profession, business type, industry, market, niche, role, shop, service, product lane, or monetizable skill suits them.

Do not answer from 官祿 alone. Split talent, role, market, money model, operating burden, public visibility, people channel, asset base, and timing.

## Fit Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: core work temperament, stamina, pressure tolerance, and whether the native can sustain the work style.
2. **官祿**: profession, role, title, authority, institution, business model, and public duty.
3. **財帛 / 田宅**: earning lane, asset base, inventory, premises, data/property/storefront, and whether the industry can retain money.
4. **兄弟**: writing, speaking, sales language, documents, code, teaching, consulting, trading, media, and skill transmission.
5. **僕役 / 夫妻**: staff, vendors, clients, customers, partners, platforms, and counterparty-heavy industries.
6. **遷移 / 父母**: outside market, travel, public institution, documents, brand authority, online reach, foreign/non-local market.
7. **福德**: taste, advisory value, spiritual/inner work, comfort market, luxury, healing/support, and the work that keeps the mind usable.
8. **疾厄 / 化忌 / 煞曜**: industry fatigue, hidden cost, technical debt, conflict, service burden, risk, or work that burns the native out.
9. **大限 / 流年 / 四化**: when to pick, switch, specialize, open a shop, go independent, build portfolio, or stay employed.

## Industry Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic star keywords:

- **紫微/天府/天相/天梁 + 官祿/父母**: management, institution, advisory, compliance, formal authority, premium service.
- **武曲/廉貞/七殺/破軍 + 官祿/財帛**: finance, operations, execution, transformation, high-pressure roles, rebuilding, sales with discipline.
- **太陽/太陰/天同 + 命身/福德/遷移**: public service, care, hospitality, comfort markets, audience-facing work, education/media when supported.
- **天機/巨門/昌曲/化科 + 兄弟/父母**: analysis, writing, consulting, teaching, documents, code, research, product/specification work.
- **貪狼/紅鸞/天喜/桃花系 + 財帛/夫妻/遷移**: beauty, entertainment, luxury, social commerce, relationship-led sales, but require leakage guard.
- **空劫/火鈴/羊陀/化忌**: volatile industry, crisis work, repair work, hidden cost, technical debt, conflict, or burnout unless controlled by structure.

## Fit Verdict Rule

Career fit is not the same as money fit.

- If 官祿 is strong but 財帛/田宅 weak, the role suits status but money retention needs structure.
- If 財帛 is strong but 官祿 weak, money can come from trade/assets but career identity may feel unstable.
- If 兄弟/昌曲/化科 is strong, use communication, teaching, writing, code, sales, consulting, documentation, or media as a bridge.
- If 僕役/夫妻 is stressed, avoid people-heavy industries or use contracts/SOPs before scaling.
- If 疾厄/化忌/煞曜 repeats, avoid high-conflict/high-emergency/high-maintenance industries even if money looks attractive.
