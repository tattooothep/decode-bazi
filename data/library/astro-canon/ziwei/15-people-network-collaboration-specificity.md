# Zi Wei People, Network, And Collaboration Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Zi Wei. It condenses public-domain Zi Wei witnesses, Daoist Canon/Quanshu timing summaries, Feixing Cetian extracted rules, and modern licensed collation into operational reading rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR, page order, private paths, or source prose.

## Purpose

Use this pack for friends, siblings, coworkers, teams, staff, subordinates, clients, business partners, bosses, elders, mentors, institutions, networks, and collaboration questions.

Do not answer from one main star, one 化祿, or one annual star alone. Use the asked palace, 三方四正, 四化, auxiliary/malefic balance, and timing.

## People Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: baseline personality and capacity to attract/manage people.
2. **Asked palace**: 兄弟, 僕役, 夫妻, 父母, 官祿, 福德, 財帛.
3. **三方四正**: support/pressure around the people palace.
4. **Main stars and brightness**: style of people only after palace and brightness are named.
5. **四化**: 生年 first, then 大限/流年 when timing matters.
6. **Auxiliary/malefic balance**: 輔弼/昌曲/魁鉞/祿權科 versus 羊陀火鈴/空劫/天刑/化忌/大耗.
7. **大限/流年/流月/流日**: timing activation only when supplied.

If birth time is missing or degraded, close 命宮/身宮/12宮/大限 palace claims unless packet supplies a reliable fallback.

## Peers / Siblings / Communication · 兄弟

Use 兄弟 for siblings, peers, classmates, coworkers at equal level, communication, and coordination.

Good 兄弟 with 昌曲/魁鉞/祿權科 supports conversation, paperwork, and useful peer links. 兄弟 with 化忌/煞/空劫 warns delay, gossip, pressure, or unreliable coordination.

## Friends / Team / Subordinates · 僕役

Use 僕役 for friends, team, staff, subordinates, helpers, followers, and network quality.

Separate:

- supportive friends,
- staff/team reliability,
- subordinate management,
- social cost,
- people who drain time or resources.

Do not declare betrayal from one 煞曜. Require palace + 三方四正 + 四化/timing repetition.

## Clients / Business Partner / Counterpart · 夫妻

Use 夫妻 for formal one-to-one counterpart, client, contract partner, supplier, or business partner only when the question is non-romantic. Keep love/marriage language out unless the user asks for it.

Check 財帛/官祿 links to see whether the counterpart brings money/status, and 福德/僕役 to see whether cooperation feels smooth.

## Boss / Elders / Institution · 父母 and 官祿

Use 父母 for elders, documents, formal backing, official support, and institutions. Use 官祿 for boss, workplace hierarchy, status, and management.

If 父母 supports but 官祿 is pressured, documents/elders help but workplace execution is heavy. If 官祿 supports but 僕役 is weak, rank improves while team support lags.

## Output Contract

Every answer using this pack must include:

- one 命宮/身宮 baseline,
- one people-palace line,
- one role/status line naming friend/team/client/boss as asked,
- one 三方四正 or 四化 support-versus-friction line,
- one timing line when a year/month/date is asked,
- one decisive practical verdict for the exact people question.
