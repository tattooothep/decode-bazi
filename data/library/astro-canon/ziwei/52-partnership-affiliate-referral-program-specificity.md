# Zi Wei Partnership, Affiliate, And Referral Program Specificity Pack

Private derived runtime rules for questions about partner programs, affiliate programs, referral programs, channel partners, ambassadors, co-marketing, joint ventures, revenue share, commission structure, resellers, agency partners, partner tiers, partner onboarding, and partner fraud/conflict risk.

## Purpose

Do not treat partnership program fit as the same thing as romantic relationship, marketing traffic, sales script, customer success referral ask, customer support, or generic business investment. This pack is for a structured external growth channel where other people or organizations bring audience, trust, distribution, or sales in exchange for commission, status, reciprocal value, or shared revenue.

## Partnership Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: owner capacity, boundary enforcement, and whether the native can carry shared incentives without losing control.
2. **官祿**: strategic authority, partner selection standard, compliance, public responsibility, and professional credibility.
3. **兄弟 / 父母**: referral message, affiliate instructions, contract documents, tracking terms, introductions, and communication flow.
4. **夫妻**: contractual partner relationship, expectation match, revenue share, commission promise, and agreement stability. Do not read this as romance unless the user asks romance.
5. **僕役**: affiliate pool, ambassadors, reseller network, agencies, community partners, partner managers, and scaled coordination.
6. **財帛 / 田宅**: commission economics, margin after payouts, retained assets, partner-generated recurring value, and backend ownership.
7. **福德 / 遷移**: trust transfer, reputation fit, shared audience belief, outside-market goodwill, and public face of the partnership.
8. **疾厄 / 化忌 / 煞曜**: partner fraud, tracking dispute, commission abuse, brand contamination, hidden support burden, legal/accounting leak, and termination risk.
9. **大限 / 流年 / 流月 / 流日 / 四化**: when to recruit, pilot, sign, scale, pause, or terminate partner channels.

## Program Mapping

- Strong 官祿 + 兄弟/父母 + 僕役: partner program can work as public distribution with clear message, contract, and coordinated channel.
- Strong 夫妻 + 財帛: revenue-share or affiliate agreement can be commercial, but only if tracking and payout terms are explicit.
- Strong 福德/遷移: referral, ambassador, and outside-audience trust channels are favored over cold paid acquisition.
- Afflicted 疾厄/化忌/煞曜: do not open broad affiliate access; use invite-only pilot, capped commissions, manual approval, and fraud checks.
- Strong 僕役 but weak 兄弟/父母: many partners can appear, but onboarding documents and tracking rules will decide whether they sell correctly.
- Strong 兄弟 but weak 財帛: buzz and introductions may happen, but commission model can leak margin.

## Verdict Rules

- If partnership evidence is mixed, split the answer into **partner type**, **program structure**, **payout/terms**, **tracking/onboarding**, and **risk control**.
- Do not say "find partners" generically. Name the channel: affiliate link, referral bounty, reseller, agency partner, ambassador, JV webinar, co-marketing, creator affiliate, strategic alliance, or no partner yet.
- If 疾厄/化忌/煞曜 is strong, recommend invite-only, low cap, manual approval, short pilot, fraud audit, or termination clause.
- If timing is asked, tie the call to 大限/流年/流月/流日/四化 and avoid pretending daily electional precision unless a date/time candidate packet exists.
