# Zi Wei Palace, Si Hua, And Timing Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Zi Wei. It applies the retained public-domain Zi Wei witnesses, Daoist Canon line, Feixing Cetian licensed extraction, modern licensed collation, and Quanshu timing summaries as operational rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, or expose raw source/OCR text.

## Purpose

Use this pack to make Zi Wei answers concrete. A valid Zi Wei answer must not say only "career is good" or "relationship has pressure." It must name the asked palace, its stars, brightness, 四化, 三方四正 support/friction, and timing layer supplied by the packet.

## Core Reading Chain

For every topic:

1. **命宮 / 身宮 baseline**: state the person's operating style and life focus. If 命宮 has no main star, use 借對宮 only when packet/prompt says so.
2. **Asked palace**: use the palace matching the topic: 夫妻, 財帛, 官祿, 疾厄, 田宅, 遷移, 福德, 子女, 父母, 兄弟, 僕役.
3. **Main stars and brightness**: 廟/旺/平/陷 modifies how cleanly the star works.
4. **Auxiliary and煞 stars**: use only as support/friction, not standalone verdict.
5. **生年四化**: 化祿/權/科/忌 show lifelong flow, authority, visibility, and knot/pressure.
6. **三方四正**: the asked palace must be judged with its triangle and opposite palace when packet supplies the structure.
7. **大限 / 大限四化**: decade layer sets the current life field.
8. **流年 / 流年四化 / 流年星**: target-year layer activates the field.
9. **流月 / 流日**: use only when supplied and only for short-term timing, not lifetime fate.
10. **Pair packet**: in pair mode, use only `PAIR_INTERACTION_PACKET ziwei`; do not invent cross-palace hits.

Do not answer from one star alone. Do not let one 化祿 erase a severe 化忌 or煞 pressure. Do not let one 化忌 erase repeated support from main stars, 三方四正, and timing.

## Topic Gates

### Career / 官祿

Use 官祿 palace, its main stars, brightness, 四化, 財帛/命宮/遷移 support, 大限/流年 activation, and any 科/權 indicators.

Name the work style:

- 紫微/天府: management, authority, institution, resource control.
- 武曲: finance, execution, assets, technical discipline.
- 天機: planning, systems, consulting, movement, change.
- 太陽: visibility, public-facing, leadership, giving.
- 天梁: protection, advisory, compliance, senior support.
- 七殺/破軍/貪狼: disruption, sales, rebuilding, risk, ambition, unconventional route.
- 文昌/文曲/化科: documents, teaching, writing, exams, reputation.

### Money / 財帛 and 田宅

Separate:

- cashflow/income from 財帛,
- storage/property from 田宅,
- career source from 官祿,
- network/gains from 僕役/福德 when relevant,
- leakage or pressure from 化忌/煞 and opposing palace.

化祿 in money-related palaces shows inflow or ease, but retention needs stable star condition and support from 田宅/官祿. 化忌 shows a knot: debt, leakage, attachment, delayed payment, hidden cost, or emotional spending depending on palace.

### Relationship / 夫妻

Use 夫妻 palace, opposite palace, 命宮, 福德, 四化, 紅鸞/天喜 if supplied, 大限/流年 timing, and pair packet in pair mode.

Separate:

- attraction,
- emotional stability,
- pressure/knot,
- family/social context,
- commitment timing.

Do not declare marriage/divorce from 夫妻宮 alone. Pair mode requires another chart and closed cross-chart packet.

### Health / 疾厄

Use 疾厄 palace, 命宮, 福德, 煞 stars, 化忌, 大限/流年 activation, and supplied monthly/daily timing.

Allowed language: vitality, stress, sleep, routine, chronic pressure, check-up, discipline, rest.

Forbidden: diagnosis, medication, surgery instruction, death prediction.

### Home / 田宅

Use 田宅, 財帛, 父母, 福德, 大限/流年. Separate property, family root, living environment, documents, hidden repair/debt, and relocation.

### Travel / 遷移

Use 遷移, 命宮 opposite relation, 田宅, 官祿, and timing. Separate travel, relocation, external market, foreign clients, public exposure, and being pulled away from home.

## Timing Specificity

For a year question:

1. Name current 大限 and its palace/theme.
2. Name target 流年 palace and 流年四化.
3. Compare the asked palace against 大限 and 流年.
4. Use 流月/流日 only when the user asks shorter timing and packet supplies it.
5. If target year is supplied in packet, do not say "only 2026 is available."

For month/day:

- Use 流月/流日 as trigger, not as standalone fate.
- Require repetition from natal palace + 大限/流年 before calling a date strong.

## No-Time Mode

If birth time is missing:

- Close 命宮, 身宮, 12宮, 星曜落宮, 三方四正, 大限, 流年宮位, 流月, 流日, pair cross-palace reading.
- Birth-year 四化 names may remain only as non-palace context.
- Do not infer a palace from the date.

## Output Rule

Every Zi Wei evidence bullet should name:

- the palace,
- main star(s) and brightness,
- relevant 四化 or star support/friction,
- timing layer if the question asks time,
- and one concrete life consequence.

Bad: "You have career luck."

Good shape: "官祿 has [star/brightness] while [四化/timing] activates it, so the career channel is [specific style], but [palace/friction] makes [specific caution]."
