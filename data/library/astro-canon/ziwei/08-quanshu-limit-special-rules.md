# 紫微斗數全書 · 大限/二限/太歲 special judgment pack

Source use:
- Derived operating summary from public-domain `紫微斗數全書` timing and special-judgment chapters, especially 卷三 sections on 大限, 二限太歲, 行限分南北斗, 羊陀迭並, 七殺重逢, 大小限星辰過十二宮, 立命行限宮, and related judgment verses.
- This is not a raw transcription. It is a runtime rule pack for Hourkey Zi Wei readings.
- Use only after the structured packet supplies natal palaces, 大限, 流年, 流月/流日 when relevant, star brightness, 四化, and annual stars. Do not calculate missing layers inside AI.

## Core Timing Chain

Read timing in this order:

1. Natal promise: 命宮, 身宮, topic palace, 三方四正, borrowed opposite palace when 命宮 is empty.
2. 大限: decade palace, decade age range, 大限四化, and whether decade palace supports or strains the asked topic.
3. 流年: annual 命宮, 流年四化, annual stars, and overlap with natal/decare topic.
4. 流月/流日: short activation only when supplied by the packet.

Do not answer a future-year question from natal personality alone if 大限/流年 fields are in the packet. Name the decade stage first, then the year trigger.

## 大限 Ten-Year Rules

大限 is not just "good decade / bad decade". It is the stage where natal promise becomes active or blocked.

- Strong natal + strong 大限 = direct growth; state the concrete field: job rank, income channel, relationship stability, home/property, health routine.
- Weak natal + strong 大限 = recovery or turnaround. Wording: "พื้นเดิมเหนื่อย แต่ช่วงนี้มีตัวดันให้พลิก".
- Strong natal + weak 大限 = talent exists but results require discipline; expect delay, duty, or extra cost.
- Weak natal + weak 大限 = reduce risk, avoid all-in decisions, and use small controlled steps.

If 大限四化 repeats the same palace/topic as 生年四化, give it heavier weight than a single annual star.

## 二限 / 小限 / 太歲

Use 二限/小限/太歲 as trigger layers, not as independent fate.

- 太歲 and annual stars can activate pressure already shown by natal + 大限.
- 小限 is useful for one-year practical focus, but it must be checked against 大限 and 流年.
- If 小限 is good but 大限/流年 are mixed, recommend limited action rather than life-changing commitment.
- If 小限 is stressed but 大限/流年 support the topic, call it "yearly friction inside a larger supportive cycle".

Do not let one lucky annual star erase a decade-level obstruction.

## Benefic Rescue And Malefic Pressure

Classical timing chapters repeatedly use rescue versus pressure logic:

- 紫微, 天府, 天相, 天梁, 左輔, 右弼, 文昌, 文曲, 天魁, 天鉞, 化祿, 化權, 化科 can rescue or organize a difficult limit when they are correctly placed and connected to the topic.
- 擎羊, 陀羅, 火星, 鈴星, 空劫, 天刑, 化忌, 大耗, 病符, 官符 increase pressure when repeated on 命/身/topic palace/流年.
- Rescue must be near the topic. A remote lucky star is support, not proof.
- Pressure must repeat before it becomes a strong warning. One malefic alone is not enough for severe wording.

User-facing rule: name both sides. Example: "งานมีตัวดัน แต่เงินรั่วจากภาระเอกสาร/ค่าใช้จ่าย" instead of one flat score.

## 羊陀 / 火鈴 / 空劫 In Timing

Use these as friction patterns:

- 羊陀 on 命/身/topic palace = cut, conflict, delay, body stress, or hard decision.
- 火鈴 = urgency, volatility, heat, impulsive action, mechanical/fire/electrical risk in mundane wording.
- 空劫 = gap, loss of expected result, hollow promise, missing support, sudden interruption.
- When these repeat through 大限 + 流年 + 流月/流日, recommend reducing exposure and tightening documents, money, health routine, or communication.

Do not write fatalistic outcomes. Convert them into practical cautions.

## 七殺 / 破軍 / 貪狼 Timing

These stars can be productive or disruptive depending on support:

- 七殺 with 紫微/祿/輔弼/昌曲/魁鉞 support = authority, decisive change, leadership under pressure.
- 七殺 without rescue, especially with repeated 煞/忌 in the timing layers = hard break, conflict, injury-risk symbolism, or high-pressure decision. Use caution wording.
- 破軍 = dismantle/rebuild. With good timing it supports reform, relocation, reset, or startup action. With 空劫/忌 it becomes waste, reversal, or unstable promise.
- 貪狼 = appetite, attraction, social drive, desire to expand. With 火鈴 and clean support it can produce fast gain; with 忌/羊陀/空劫 it becomes indulgence, entanglement, or overreach.

For relationship questions, do not call attraction stability. For career questions, do not call disruption failure if 官祿/大限 support reform.

## Child/Elder And High-Risk Language Guard

Classical child-limit and elder-limit wording is severe. In production readings:

- Do not predict death, fixed lifespan, disability, or unavoidable calamity.
- For child, elderly, pregnancy, surgery, or illness topics, convert severe timing patterns into: "ต้องตรวจ/คุม/ลดความเสี่ยง/ให้ผู้เชี่ยวชาญดูจริง".
- If packet evidence repeats stress on 命/身/疾厄 with 煞/忌/空劫 and timing activation, say it is a high-caution period, not a certainty.
- If packet evidence has rescue stars around 疾厄/命身, state the protection/recovery/order factor.

## Topic Mapping For Timing Answers

Career:
- 官祿 + 大限/流年 化權/化科/魁鉞/昌曲 = role push, formal recognition, document/exam/interview support.
- 官祿 + 忌/羊陀/官符 = pressure from boss, contract, official process, dispute, or accountability.

Money:
- 財帛/田宅 + 祿/祿存/科 = earning channel, retained asset, paperwork support.
- 財帛/田宅 + 忌/空劫/大耗 = leak, repair, tax/debt/document cost, promised money not fully materializing.

Relationship:
- 夫妻/命/福德 + 祿/科/紅鸞/天喜 = attraction or emotional/social opening.
- 夫妻/命/福德 + 忌/權/羊陀火鈴 = attachment plus pressure, control, quarrel, or duty. Name who carries pressure in pair mode.

Health:
- 疾厄/命/身/福德 + repeated 忌/煞/空劫 = rest, check-up, routine, avoid overwork.
- 化科/天梁/天府 around health topics = treatment order, elder/professional help, recovery support.

Home/property:
- 田宅 + 祿/科/魁鉞 = home/property/document help.
- 田宅 + 忌/空劫/大耗 = repairs, moving burden, family cost, contract delay.

Travel/relocation:
- 遷移 + 天馬/祿/權 = movement, outside opportunity, clients, public exposure.
- 遷移 + 忌/羊陀/空劫 = stressful travel, public friction, relocation cost, or failed outside promise.

## Output Rule

For future-year answers, always include:

1. the 大限 age range and decade palace,
2. the 流年 year palace and main 四化/annual-star trigger,
3. the topic palace result,
4. a plain verdict,
5. one concrete action recommendation.

Avoid broad wording. If the answer can fit many charts, add the exact palace/star/timing reason.
