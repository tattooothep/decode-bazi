> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Zi Wei. It condenses public-domain Zi Wei witnesses, Daoist Canon/Quanshu timing summaries, Feixing Cetian extracted rules, and modern licensed collation into operational reading rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR, page order, private paths, or source prose.

## Purpose

Use this pack for reputation, public image, credibility, reviews, comments, gossip, scandal, public backlash, defamation, being attacked online, platform ban, public apology, and "will people still trust me" questions.

Do not answer from 化科, 化祿, or fame language alone. Split visibility, credibility, accusation, audience reaction, money/status damage, and repair timing.

## Core Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: baseline identity, public pressure capacity, and how personally the matter lands.
2. **官祿**: status, title, career name, public role, brand promise, and formal standing.
3. **父母**: documents, public statements, institutional approval, reputation record, and official protection or blame.
4. **兄弟**: speech, posts, comments, evidence flow, peer talk, and rumor channels.
5. **僕役 / 福德**: audience, followers, support network, emotional absorption, and whether the crowd helps or attacks.
6. **夫妻**: open counterparty, customer, sponsor, platform, accuser, or contractual public opponent.
7. **財帛 / 田宅**: sales damage, stored base, retained customers, assets, and whether public noise becomes material damage.
8. **四化 and malefics**: 化科 can clarify, 化祿 can soften, 化權 can assert, 化忌/羊陀火鈴/空劫/大耗 can inflame or leak.
9. **Timing**: 大限/流年/流月/流日 only when supplied.

## Trust-Chain Rule

Public visibility is not public trust. Require a chain:

- 命/身 + 官祿/父母 = public identity and formal standing,
- 兄弟 = message, evidence, comment, or rumor path,
- 僕役/福德 = audience support or crowd pressure,
- 夫妻 = customer/platform/sponsor/accuser contact,
- 財帛/田宅 = actual business or base consequence.

If 官祿/父母 are supported but 兄弟 is damaged, the chart points to wording/evidence repair. If 僕役/福德 is damaged, public mood and group reaction are the main risk. If 財帛/田宅 is also damaged, the issue can affect revenue, customer retention, or assets.

## Crisis And Repair Rule

For review/backlash/scandal questions, name the situation:

- **misunderstanding**: 兄弟/文昌文曲/化科 problem without deep 財帛 damage,
- **customer/platform conflict**: 夫妻 and 財帛 involved,
- **public pile-on**: 僕役/福德 hit by 化忌 or煞曜,
- **formal/status damage**: 官祿/父母 involved,
- **base damage**: 田宅 and 財帛 both involved.

Repair uses the strongest support axis: clarify with 化科/昌曲, soften with 祿, set boundaries with 權, apologize or document through 父母, direct customer care through 夫妻, and rebuild base through 田宅.
