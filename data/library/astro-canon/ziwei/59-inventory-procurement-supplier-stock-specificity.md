# Zi Wei Inventory, Procurement, Supplier, And Stock Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about inventory, stock, stockout, overstock, procurement, purchase order, supplier, vendor lead time, MOQ, warehouse, import stock, safety stock, reorder point, raw material, or whether to buy/hold/clear goods.

Do not treat inventory/procurement as the same thing as cashflow/runway, unit economics, pricing, marketing traffic, customer support, sales-team QA, partner due diligence, or generic business. If the user asks only "cash left / burn / reserve", use the cashflow pack. If the user asks "profit per unit / margin / break-even", use the unit-economics pack. This pack is for physical or operational stock decisions and supplier/warehouse flow.

## Canon Routing

Zi Wei inventory judgment must join 田宅, 財帛, 官祿, 兄弟/父母, 夫妻, 僕役, 遷移, and 疾厄/化忌/煞曜. A bright 財帛 palace does not prove stock safety if 田宅 traps capital, 僕役 fails counting/shipping, or 化忌 enters the supplier/warehouse chain.

- 命宮/身宮: whether the owner can keep stock discipline and avoid emotional buying.
- 田宅: warehouse, stored goods, inventory base, raw material, fixed stock, and goods trapped in place.
- 財帛: capital tied in goods, stock value, purchase affordability, and cash recovered after sale.
- 官祿: operating system, purchasing policy, inventory process, and stock-to-sales rhythm.
- 兄弟/父母: purchase order, SKU records, document standard, supplier communication, and reorder trigger.
- 夫妻: supplier/vendor/customer terms, consignment, preorder, deposit, and delivery promise.
- 僕役: warehouse staff, packer, shipping handler, vendor execution, tool, system, and inventory count.
- 遷移: imported stock, outside supplier, long-distance logistics, transit goods, and delivery route.
- 疾厄/化忌/煞曜: stockout, overstock, spoilage, damage, theft, wrong SKU, customs delay, refund, and dead stock.
- 大限/流年/流月/流日/四化: when to buy, pause, clear, switch supplier, or reduce stock depth.

## Answer Contract

For inventory/procurement questions, answer with:

1. one 命宮/身宮 stock-discipline line;
2. one 田宅 warehouse/inventory-base line;
3. one 財帛 capital-tied-in-stock line;
4. one 官祿 purchasing/process line;
5. one 兄弟/父母 purchase-order/record/reorder-message line;
6. one 夫妻 supplier/customer-term line;
7. one 僕役 warehouse/vendor-execution line;
8. one 遷移 import/logistics/transit line when relevant;
9. one 疾厄/化忌/煞曜 stockout/overstock/damage/dead-stock-risk line;
10. one timing and action verdict: buy now, hold, reduce SKU, clear old stock, preorder only, change supplier, or set safety stock/reorder point.

Never let 化祿 in 財帛 hide a bad stock cycle. If 化忌, 煞曜, or 僕役 pressure appears, name exactly where the goods get stuck, delayed, damaged, or refunded.
