# Zi Wei Decision, Action Guidance, And Go/Wait Specificity Pack

License mode: summary-only operational rule pack. Use for Hourkey runtime readings only. Do not expose raw OCR, licensed source prose, page order, or private source file paths.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks "ควรทำไหม", "เลือกทางไหนดี", "ลุยหรือรอก่อน", "ลาออกไหม", "ลงทุนไหม", "เริ่ม/หยุด/เลิกดีไหม", "ต้องทำยังไง", or any go/wait/choose/advice question in Zi Wei Dou Shu.

The answer must convert palace and 四化 evidence into a practical action stance. Do not answer only with star descriptions.

## Decision Chain

1. Define the asked action and topic palace: 官祿 for work, 財帛/田宅 for money/property, 夫妻 for relationship/counterparty, 疾厄 for health/routine pressure, 遷移 for movement, 兄弟/僕役/父母 for people/team/institution.
2. Check natal permission: 命宮, 身宮, asked palace, 三方四正, 主星, 輔煞, and natal 四化.
3. Check timing: 大限, 大限四化, target-year 流年, 流年四化, annual stars, and 流月/流日 only when supplied.
4. Check blocker: 化忌, 煞曜, 空劫, 刑耗, weak palace, repeated clash to asked palace, or pair-packet friction in relationship decisions.
5. Convert evidence into one of four action calls: ลุย, ลุยแบบมีเงื่อนไข, รอก่อน/จัดระบบก่อน, หรือไม่ควร.

## Action Rules

- "ลุย" requires support from asked palace plus 命/身 or 三方 and timing reinforcement.
- "ลุยแบบมีเงื่อนไข" fits when 化祿/權/科 or strong palace supports the action but 化忌/煞曜/risk palace imposes constraints.
- "รอก่อน" fits when natal promise is usable but 大限/流年 trigger is not ready or pressure is peaking.
- "ไม่ควร" requires repeated harm from natal palace, timing, and asked palace or pair interaction.
- Do not make medical, legal, or financial orders. Phrase as astrological risk/action guidance and advise professional confirmation for high-stakes decisions.

## Output Contract

For decision/action answers, include:

- one asked-action line naming the exact action and palace,
- one support line from 命宮/身宮/asked palace/三方四正/四化,
- one blocker/risk line from 化忌/煞曜/空劫/weak palace,
- one timing line from 大限/流年/流月/流日 when supplied,
- one concrete action call: ลุย / ลุยแบบมีเงื่อนไข / รอก่อน / ไม่ควร,
- one practical next-step line tied to the chart evidence.
