## Status Lanes

- Attraction: 夫妻↔命 contact, 祿/科 to 命/夫妻/福德, 桃花/紅鸞/天喜 trigger, 贪狼/廉貞/破軍 style pull.
- Commitment: stable 夫妻, 福德 comfort, 田宅 support, 父母 approval, 大限/流年 repeating support.
- Conflict/control: 化忌 or one-sided 化權 on 命/夫妻/福德, 羊陀火鈴/空劫 repetition, pressured 田宅/父母.
- Breakup/divorce risk: 夫妻 harmed plus 福德/田宅 harmed plus timing pressure. One 化忌 alone is not enough.
- Reconciliation: 科/祿 repair, 福德 softening, 父母/兄弟 communication path, timing easing after a pressured period.

- commit / marry with conditions,
