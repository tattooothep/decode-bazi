# 紫微斗數 · Business, Investment, And Contract Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Zi Wei. Use as operational rules only; do not expose raw OCR, page order, private paths, or source prose.

## Purpose

Use this pack for business, opening a shop, investing, buying/selling, contracts, partners, clients, suppliers, loans, expansion, and money tied to operations.

Do not answer business from 財帛 alone. Split operating power, cashflow, retained assets, counterparty, contract/document risk, and timing.

## Business Chain

Read in this order:

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: ability to lead, negotiate, absorb pressure, and execute.
2. **官祿**: operating model, role, public/business status, institution/regulation.
3. **財帛**: cashflow, sales, pricing, payment movement.
4. **田宅**: premises, inventory, retained assets, capital base, property.
5. **夫妻 / 僕役 / 兄弟 / 父母**: one-to-one business counterpart, staff/team, peers, documents/institution.
6. **福德 / 疾厄**: reserves, stress, hidden cost, debt load, recovery capacity.
7. **生年四化 + 大限/流年四化 + annual stars**: what opens, controls, formalizes, or blocks the business.

## Action Calls

- **ลุย**: 官祿 plus 財帛/田宅 repeat support, 命身 can carry it, and 大限/流年 reinforces.
- **ลุยแบบมีเงื่อนไข**: 祿/權/科 supports action but 忌/煞/空劫/大耗 hits contract, cash, or team lanes; use staged spending and written controls.
- **รอก่อน**: natal business promise exists but timing or counterparty/document palace is not ready.
- **ไม่ควร**: repeated harm hits 官祿, 財帛/田宅, counterparty/document palace, and timing.

## Contract And Partner Rules

- 夫妻 in non-romantic business questions = contract partner, buyer/seller, supplier, or one-to-one client.
- 父母 is document, license, institution, legal form, audit, senior authority.
- 僕役/兄弟 is team, staff, peers, operational allies, and people cost.
- 化祿 = inflow/access; 化權 = control/pricing/execution; 化科 = documentation/reputation/formality; 化忌 = knot, delayed payment, contract burden, debt, or attachment.
- Do not call stable profit from 化祿 alone unless 財帛/田宅 retention and timing support repeat.

## Output Contract

Every business answer must include:

- one business-action line naming the exact action and palace,
- one 官祿 operating-channel line,
- one 財帛/田宅 cashflow-versus-asset line,
- one 夫妻/僕役/父母 counterparty-contract-document line,
- one 大限/流年/四化 timing line when supplied,
- one concrete call: ลุย / ลุยแบบมีเงื่อนไข / รอก่อน / ไม่ควร,
- one practical control: cash reserve, staged investment, contract review, partner boundary, or document/compliance step.
