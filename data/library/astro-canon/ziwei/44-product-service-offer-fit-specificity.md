## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks what product to sell, what service to offer, what package/offer should be built, whether to sell physical product, digital product, advisory service, subscription, premium package, low-ticket entry, bundle, course, tool, or done-for-you service.

Do not treat offer fit as the same thing as industry fit, customer persona, marketing traffic, pricing, or technology build. Split offer promise, delivery burden, buyer trust, money model, asset/inventory/data base, message clarity, scale path, risk, and timing.

## Offer Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: native delivery style, product promise they can sustain, pressure tolerance, and whether the offer should be personal-service, systemized, advisory, creative, or asset-backed.
2. **官祿**: professional authority, offer category, public result, B2B/B2G/regulated offer, and whether the product should carry expert status.
3. **財帛 / 田宅**: cash model, margin, subscription/stored value, inventory, property/data/content asset, and whether the offer leaves retained value.
4. **兄弟 / 子女**: explanation, copy, teaching, documentation, creative output, course, content, script, code/spec, and product clarity.
5. **夫妻**: direct buyer trust, contract scope, bespoke service, refund/refusal risk, and whether the offer needs consultation before payment.
6. **僕役**: group delivery, membership, platform sale, vendors, staff, community, and whether the offer can scale beyond the owner.
7. **福德**: comfort, taste, advisory/spiritual value, wellness, luxury, inner-life benefit, and customer satisfaction after purchase.
8. **疾厄 / 化忌 / 煞曜**: support load, hidden labor, defect/complaint pattern, liability, burnout, and offer types to avoid.
9. **大限 / 流年 / 流月 / 流日 / 四化**: when to launch, productize, bundle, retire, narrow, or test the offer.

## Offer Type Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic star keywords:

- **官祿 + 兄弟/子女 strong**: course, consulting, report, audit, content product, expert package, certification, training.
- **財帛 + 田宅 strong**: physical product, data/content library, subscription, property/home asset, durable package, stored-value product.
- **夫妻 strong**: premium one-to-one service, contract service, bespoke package, high-trust consultation, referral-only offer.
- **僕役 strong**: group program, membership, community package, marketplace/platform offer, staff-assisted service.
- **福德 strong**: wellness, comfort, beauty/taste, advisory, spiritual, luxury, rest/recovery, inner-life product.
- **遷移 / 父母 strong**: foreign/non-local, public credibility, institutional, document-backed, platform-authorized, outside-market offer.
- **疾厄/化忌/煞曜 stressed**: avoid unlimited support, vague custom work, emergency promise, underpriced operations, high-liability offer, or complex product without process.

## Verdict Rules

- If 命身/夫妻 is stronger than 僕役, sell a focused premium service before building a mass product.
- If 財帛/田宅 is stronger than 官祿, productize stored value instead of selling pure labor.
- If 兄弟/子女 is weak, do not launch a complicated offer until explanation, copy, docs, or onboarding is fixed.
- If 夫妻 is weak, avoid high-trust bespoke packages; sell clearer fixed-scope offers.
- If 疾厄/化忌 repeats, reduce scope, cap revisions, add deposit, or avoid service-heavy products.
