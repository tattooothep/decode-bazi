# 紫微斗數 · 14 main stars topic matrix

Source use:
- Licensed internal operational extraction and cross-check with the open Zi Wei core notes.
- Use this matrix to interpret packet-supplied 主星 in 夫妻, 財帛, and 官祿.
- Do not expose, quote, cite, or name private source text. User-facing answer must only show chart-based reasoning.
- Brightness matters: 廟/旺 strengthens constructive side; 陷 magnifies friction; 四化 and 煞星 can override the clean reading.

## How To Use

For a question about relationship, money, or career:
1. Find the asked palace: 夫妻, 財帛, or 官祿.
2. Read the main star(s) in that palace from the packet.
3. Check brightness: 廟旺利平陷.
4. Check 三方四正 and opposite palace.
5. Check 四化 layers: 生年 -> 大限 -> 流年 -> 流月/流日 if supplied.
6. Then give a verdict. Do not read a star as automatically good or bad without palace + brightness + timing.

## Star Matrix

### 紫微
- 夫妻: wants respect and status in partnership; stable when supported, lonely/commanding if isolated. With 化權, power dynamics must be managed.
- 財帛: can manage large resources, brand, authority money, institutional money. Needs helpers or clear structure; 煞/空劫 cause big-looking but leaky plans.
- 官祿: strongest for leadership, management, public position, senior responsibility. Best with 左右/昌曲/魁鉞/科權祿; isolated 紫微 means burden of command.

### 天機
- 夫妻: relationship changes through thinking, negotiation, distance, planning; flexible but restless. 化忌 brings overthinking and suspicion.
- 財帛: money from strategy, skill, tech, planning, trade, analysis; unstable if no fixed system. Good for multiple channels, weak for passive holding.
- 官祿: advisory, planning, analytics, engineering, teaching, consulting, moving parts. 化權 means active problem-solving; 陷 plus 煞 means nervous workload.

### 太陽
- 夫妻: in a woman's chart can describe husband pattern; generally open, giving, visible partner. 陷 or 化忌 gives distance, pride, burden, father/husband worry.
- 財帛: money through visibility, service, public work, leadership, male/senior clients. Bright Sun gives clean earning; dark Sun gives effort without equal return.
- 官祿: public role, authority, management, service, reputation. Best when bright; 化祿/科 helps recognition, 化忌 makes name/reputation pressure.

### 武曲
- 夫妻: practical, strict, duty-heavy love; stable through responsibility but emotionally dry. With 煞 or 忌, conflict over money/control.
- 財帛: core wealth star; accounting, assets, finance, hard-earned money, decisive profit. 廟旺 supports accumulation; 煞/空劫 produces sharp gain-loss swings.
- 官祿: finance, operations, military, enforcement, engineering, execution. 化權 is strong command; 化忌 creates work-money pressure or rigid conflict.

### 天同
- 夫妻: gentle, comfort-seeking, friendly partner bond; can avoid hard conflict. 陷/忌 makes dependence, softness, emotional avoidance.
- 財帛: money through comfort, service, food, care, leisure, people goodwill; not aggressive wealth unless supported by 祿/府/武.
- 官祿: service, care, hospitality, support roles, welfare, client comfort. Good for stable easy work; weak for high-pressure command unless supported.

### 廉貞
- 夫妻: intense attraction, rules, jealousy, moral tests, desire and conflict. 化祿 gives charm; 化忌 makes entanglement, emotional debt, scandal risk.
- 財帛: money through management, risk, regulated fields, aesthetics, performance, or controlled desire markets. 煞/忌 = legal/contract/temptation pressure.
- 官祿: discipline, administration, law, compliance, performance, command under rules. With 化權 strong control; with 忌 reputation or rule trouble.

### 天府
- 夫妻: stable, protective, practical partner; values security and household order. Too heavy can become conservative or possessive.
- 財帛: storehouse star; savings, reserves, property, treasury, asset management. Strong for retaining wealth, weaker for fast speculative gain.
- 官祿: administration, finance, management, institutions, resource control. Reliable career base; needs activation by 權/科/祿 for rapid rise.

### 太陰
- 夫妻: emotional, private, nurturing, feminine/motherly pattern; night/private life matters. 陷/忌 gives mood, distance, hidden worry.
- 財帛: assets, savings, real estate, quiet accumulation, women's market, design/beauty. Bright Moon stores wealth; dark Moon leaks through uncertainty.
- 官祿: research, design, finance, care, property, behind-the-scenes work. Good when calm/bright; 化忌 means paperwork, female/client, or hidden pressure.

### 貪狼
- 夫妻: strong attraction, social desire, romance, pleasure; unstable if unsupported. With 火/鈴 can be passionate but volatile.
- 財帛: profit from sales, entertainment, desire markets, networking, risk, sudden opportunity. 廟旺 can bring windfall; 陷 makes greed/leakage.
- 官祿: sales, entertainment, marketing, nightlife, negotiation, people-facing growth. Needs discipline; 化祿 boosts opportunity, 忌 brings scandal/overreach.

### 巨門
- 夫妻: communication-heavy, debate, suspicion, verbal conflict; good if used for honest dialogue. 化忌 makes words become the knot.
- 財帛: money through speech, teaching, law, consulting, media, analysis, dispute handling. Risk from contracts, complaints, unclear wording.
- 官祿: law, speaking, research, investigation, audit, teaching, media. Strong for verbal/intellectual work; 煞/忌 = controversy and reputation dispute.

### 天相
- 夫妻: courteous, cooperative, role-conscious partner; needs fairness and mutual respect. Weak if squeezed by bad stars or dependency.
- 財帛: money through service, mediation, management, client trust, official processes. Protects money more than creates risky windfall.
- 官祿: assistant-to-power, management, diplomacy, HR, client service, legal/formal coordination. Strong with 紫府/魁鉞/科.

### 天梁
- 夫妻: protective, elder/teacher tone, moral support; can feel distant or corrective. Good for stability, less for heat.
- 財帛: money through protection, medicine, teaching, public welfare, senior support, problem-solving. Not greedy; wealth grows through trust.
- 官祿: mentor, teacher, advisor, medicine, law, religion, public service, crisis protector. 化科 is strong protection; 煞 makes burdensome rescue work.

### 七殺
- 夫妻: intense, independent, sharp partner dynamic; needs boundaries. Good for strong people, bad for soft communication if 煞/忌 repeat.
- 財帛: money through risk, decisive action, competition, restructuring, military/technical fields. Can gain fast but must control loss.
- 官祿: command, emergency, military, police, surgery, engineering, entrepreneurship, breakthrough. Strong when 廟旺 and controlled; 陷 with 煞 = job shocks.

### 破軍
- 夫妻: disruptive, changeable, unconventional relationship; separation/reform themes if unsupported. Can rebuild if both accept change.
- 財帛: break old assets to create new money; startup, renovation, speculation, liquidation. High volatility; do not call stable wealth without storage support.
- 官祿: reform, startup, crisis work, tech disruption, demolition/rebuild, independent action. Powerful for change; poor for conservative stability.

## Four Transformations By Topic

Relationship:
- 化祿 to 夫妻/命/福德 = attraction, ease, willingness to connect.
- 化權 to 夫妻 = strong pull but also control/pressure.
- 化科 to 夫妻 = formal protection, reconciliation, public name, documents.
- 化忌 to 夫妻 = attachment, worry, debt, jealousy, unresolved knot.

Money:
- 化祿 to 財帛/田宅/官祿 = earning channel or resource.
- 化權 to 財帛 = active money movement, pressure to earn/control.
- 化科 to 財帛 = protected money through papers, reputation, formal channel.
- 化忌 to 財帛 = stuck money, debt, obligation, leakage; confirm with 三方 before calling loss.

Career:
- 化祿 to 官祿 = opportunity and smoother work flow.
- 化權 to 官祿 = promotion, command, heavier duty, competition.
- 化科 to 官祿 = recognition, exam, license, paper, reputation support.
- 化忌 to 官祿 = blockage, boss/reputation worry, delayed process, work burden.

Final rule: when star nature and 四化 disagree, the transformation shows the event trigger; the star shows the style of the event; brightness and 三方 decide whether the result is usable or damaging.
