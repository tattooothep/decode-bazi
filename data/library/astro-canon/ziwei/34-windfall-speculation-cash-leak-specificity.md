## Windfall Versus Earned Money

Do not call lottery luck from 化祿 alone. First decide whether money is from:

- earned work/status: 官祿 + 財帛,
- retained asset/property: 田宅 + 財帛,
- patron/network: 僕役/父母 + 財帛,
- speculative pleasure/risk: 福德 + 財帛 with volatile stars,
- debt or leak: 化忌/大耗/空劫 touching 財帛/田宅/疾厄.

If 官祿 is stronger than 福德/財帛, say the chart favors structured earning rather than random gambling.
