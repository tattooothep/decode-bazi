## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks how to deliver an offer: one-to-one, high-touch, low-touch, group program, membership, subscription, self-serve course/library, done-for-you, done-with-you, audit/report, fixed-scope package, retainer, agency service, marketplace/platform, or hybrid delivery.

Do not treat delivery model as the same thing as offer fit, pricing, marketing, customer persona, support operations, or technology build. Delivery model decides how much human labor, team load, documentation, repeatability, service boundary, queue pressure, and scale capacity the chart can carry.

## Delivery Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: owner stamina, personal delivery style, pressure tolerance, and whether the native can carry a high-touch promise.
2. **官祿**: formal role, service authority, promise ownership, professional standard, and whether the delivery must be expert-led.
3. **兄弟 / 子女**: explanation, SOP, scripts, documentation, chat flow, handoff, course material, report structure, creative output, and repeatability.
4. **夫妻**: direct client expectation, contract boundary, one-to-one service, bespoke request risk, revision pressure, and trust load.
5. **僕役**: team, assistants, vendors, group delivery, community, platform, staffing, and whether scale can be shared beyond the owner.
6. **財帛 / 田宅**: margin, retained asset, backend base, inventory, library, data/content asset, subscription base, and whether delivery creates stored value.
7. **福德**: customer comfort, satisfaction, recovery space, soft value, advisory tone, and whether the model preserves mental bandwidth.
8. **疾厄 / 化忌 / 煞曜**: bottleneck, hidden labor, support burden, complaint risk, burnout, liability, defect queue, and delivery model to avoid.
9. **大限 / 流年 / 流月 / 流日 / 四化**: when to launch, cap intake, hire, document, automate, convert to group, pause, or narrow delivery.

## Delivery Model Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic star keywords:

- **命身 + 夫妻 / 官祿 strong, 僕役 weaker**: premium one-to-one, high-trust advisory, fixed client slot, referral-only service.
- **僕役 + 兄弟 strong**: group program, cohort, membership, community support, platform-assisted service, staff-assisted model.
- **兄弟/子女 + 田宅 strong**: self-serve course, template library, report product, documentation pack, recorded training, knowledge base.
- **官祿 + 兄弟 strong, 疾厄 active**: audit/report/fixed-scope package; avoid open-ended done-for-you.
- **官祿 + 夫妻 strong, 財帛 stable**: done-for-you or done-with-you package with deposit, milestone, and revision cap.
- **財帛 + 田宅 + 僕役 strong**: subscription, retainer, membership, stored-value backend, recurring delivery with defined scope.
- **福德 strong with low 疾厄**: advisory, wellness, reflective, community-care, high-satisfaction model.
- **疾厄/化忌/煞曜 repeats**: avoid unlimited chat, vague custom work, urgent-response promise, all-inclusive retainer, and owner-only support.

## Verdict Rules

- If one-to-one trust is stronger than team/community capacity, choose premium limited slots before group scale.
- If 兄弟/子女/田宅 is stronger than 夫妻, build self-serve or documented delivery before custom service.
- If 僕役 is strong but 兄弟 is weak, group delivery needs clearer SOP and moderation before scaling.
- If 疾厄/化忌 repeats, cap intake, cap revisions, require deposit, narrow scope, or pause new clients.
- If 財帛/田宅 is weak, do not promise recurring delivery until backend, margin, and retained assets are fixed.
