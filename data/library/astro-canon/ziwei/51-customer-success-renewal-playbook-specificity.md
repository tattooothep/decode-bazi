## Success Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: whether the owner can carry accountability, enforce rhythm, follow up, and keep clients progressing without over-rescuing.
2. **官祿**: success standard, service authority, outcome definition, professional milestone, and what "done well" means.
3. **兄弟 / 父母**: check-in messages, progress review questions, reminder cadence, instructions, education content, documentation, and success notes.
4. **夫妻**: direct client relationship, renewal conversation, expectations, trust, objections, and willingness to continue.
5. **僕役**: cohort/community/customer pool, team handoff, customer-success staff, group momentum, and whether success can be managed at scale.
6. **財帛 / 田宅**: renewal revenue, retained value, expansion account, implementation asset, saved money/time, and whether success turns into durable value.
7. **福德**: client satisfaction, emotional relief, trust, loyalty, values alignment, and when to ask for referral or testimonial.
8. **疾厄 / 化忌 / 煞曜**: at-risk clients, non-adoption, missing work, hidden dissatisfaction, over-service, refund pressure, bad-fit rescue, and when to stop chasing.
9. **大限 / 流年 / 流月 / 流日 / 四化**: when to start check-ins, run success reviews, renew, expand, ask for referrals, rescue, downgrade, or close the account.

## Playbook Mapping

Map from packet evidence, not generic star keywords:

- **官祿 strong**: define measurable success milestones and formal review points.
- **兄弟/父母 strong**: use written check-ins, scorecards, progress notes, education sequence, and reminder templates.
- **夫妻 strong**: use direct renewal calls, expectation reset, account plan, and relationship-led expansion.
- **僕役 strong**: use cohort momentum, group office hours, staff triage, community check-ins, and scalable health scoring.
- **財帛/田宅 strong**: renew around retained value, implementation assets, cost saved, revenue created, or account expansion.
- **福德 strong**: ask for referral/testimonial after relief or trust is visibly created.
- **疾厄/化忌/煞曜 heavy**: run rescue plan, reduce scope, require client action, downgrade, or stop before over-service becomes loss.

## Verdict Rules

- If customer value is strong but communication lane is weak, create success scorecard before asking for renewal.
- If relationship lane is strong but risk lane is active, renew only after expectation reset and visible milestone.
- If scale lane is strong, move success management into group cadence plus exception handling.
- If retained-value lane is strong, ask for expansion after asset/result is delivered, not before.
- If hidden-risk lane repeats, do not over-rescue; set action deadline, downgrade, or close the account.
