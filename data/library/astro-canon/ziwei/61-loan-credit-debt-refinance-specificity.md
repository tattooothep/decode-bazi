# Zi Wei Loan, Credit, Debt, And Refinance Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about loans, credit approval, credit cards, debt repayment, debt restructuring, refinancing, installment plans, interest rate, principal, collateral, guarantor, creditor pressure, collection, default, delinquency, or whether to borrow, refinance, restructure, consolidate debt, pay down debt, negotiate with creditors, or avoid new debt.

Do not treat loan/credit/debt as the same thing as cashflow/runway, unit economics, inventory, tax/accounting, property purchase, general legal approval, windfall/speculation, or generic business. If the question is only "cash left / burn / reserve", use the cashflow pack. If it is only "buy house / sell land / move home", use the property pack. If it asks loan terms, repayment, refinance, collateral, guarantor, credit approval, default, or creditor pressure, this pack must be included.

## Canon Routing

Zi Wei loan/debt judgment must join 命宮/身宮, 財帛, 田宅, 官祿, 夫妻, 兄弟/父母, 僕役, and 疾厄/化忌/煞曜. Debt is a promise chain. 財帛 strength can help repayment, but it cannot erase weak documents, bad counterparties, or 化忌 pressure.

- 命宮/身宮: whether the owner has borrowing discipline, repayment capacity, and the ability to keep promises under pressure.
- 財帛: cash-in, installment burden, debt-service capacity, interest drain, payment priority, and whether the loan improves or weakens liquidity.
- 田宅: collateral, reserve base, retained asset, mortgage/security value, old debt tied to assets, and whether the base can carry the obligation.
- 官祿: lender approval standard, formal duty, credit policy, official contract process, and responsibility to maintain status.
- 夫妻: creditor, bank officer, guarantor, co-borrower, partner, counterparty contract, and negotiation channel.
- 兄弟/父母: application paperwork, credit file, debt statement, repayment schedule, proof folder, and formal evidence trail.
- 僕役: admin execution, auto-pay, bookkeeper/staff handling payments, collection process, and follow-up discipline.
- 疾厄/化忌/煞曜: default, delinquency, penalty interest, collection pressure, hidden fee, debt spiral, guarantor damage, refinance trap, and covenant breach.
- 大限/流年/流月/流日/四化: when to borrow, refinance, restructure, pay down, negotiate, avoid new debt, or face creditor pressure.

## Answer Contract

For loan/credit/debt/refinance questions, answer with:

1. one 命宮/身宮 borrower-discipline/repayment-capacity line;
2. one 財帛 cash-in/installment/debt-service/interest line;
3. one 田宅 collateral/reserve/asset-security line;
4. one 官祿 lender-approval/formal-duty/credit-policy line;
5. one 夫妻 creditor/guarantor/co-borrower/counterparty-contract line;
6. one 兄弟/父母 application-document/credit-file/repayment-schedule line;
7. one 僕役 payment-admin/auto-pay/collection-follow-up line;
8. one 疾厄/化忌/煞曜 default/penalty/hidden-fee/refinance-trap line;
9. one timing and action verdict: borrow, borrow only with cap, refinance, restructure, consolidate, negotiate, pay down first, avoid new debt, prepare documents, increase reserve, or wait.

Never let 化祿 in 財帛 turn debt into a free win. If 化忌, 煞曜, weak 父母/兄弟 paperwork, or stressed 夫妻 counterparty appears, name the exact loan condition that can fail.
