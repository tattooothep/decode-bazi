# Zi Wei Career, Wealth, And Health Specificity Pack

> Runtime derived summary for Fusion Zi Wei. This pack condenses public-domain Zi Wei witnesses, Daoist Canon/Quanshu timing summaries, Feixing Cetian extracted rules, and modern licensed collation into operational reading rules only. Do not quote, cite, reproduce, expose OCR, page order, private paths, or source prose.

## Purpose

Use this pack when the user asks about work, money, health, property, yearly direction, or a combined "งาน เงิน สุขภาพ" question. The answer must sound like it belongs to this chart, not a generic horoscope.

Do not answer with one global label such as "ดี", "กลาง", or "กดดัน" without naming the palaces and timing fields that caused it.

## Career / 官祿 Chain

Read career in this order:

1. **命宮 and 身宮**: baseline temperament and life focus.
2. **官祿 palace**: main star(s), brightness, auxiliary support,煞 pressure, and whether the palace borrows from the opposite palace.
3. **三方四正 of 官祿**: include 命宮, 財帛, and opposite palace support/friction when packet supplies it.
4. **四化**: 化權 for authority/execution, 化科 for reputation/documents/exams, 化祿 for opportunity/resources, 化忌 for knot, delay, pressure, or over-attachment.
5. **遷移**: outside market, clients, travel, public exposure, foreign/distant channels.
6. **大限 and target 流年**: decade shows the life field; annual layer shows what activates now.

Career verdict must state:

- work style: management, technical/execution, consulting/systems, sales/network, public-facing, institutional, rebuilding/disruption, creative/communication;
- where the opportunity comes from: boss, institution, clients, network, documents, relocation, property/family base, self-employment;
- one concrete friction: control, paperwork, conflict, delay, unstable role, overwork, hidden cost, reputation risk;
- final call: advance, consolidate, restructure, wait, or avoid expansion.

## Money / 財帛 and 田宅 Chain

Separate money into four lanes:

1. **財帛**: cashflow, income handling, spending behavior.
2. **官祿**: income source and work channel.
3. **田宅**: retained assets, property, home base, business premises.
4. **福德 / 僕役 / 遷移**: spending comfort, gains from network, outside-market income when relevant.

Weighting:

- 化祿/祿存 in money lanes shows inflow, ease, sponsor, asset access, or saleable opportunity.
- 化科 helps paperwork, reputation, loan/document clarity, exams/certification, lawful support.
- 化權 helps control, pricing power, management, decisive action.
- 化忌/羊陀火鈴/空劫/大耗 shows leakage, debt knot, repair, sudden expense, emotional spending, delayed payment, contract burden, or a source of pressure.

Do not call "rich" from 化祿 alone. Retention needs 田宅/財帛 support and no repeated leakage. If earning is strong but retention is weak, say "เงินเข้าได้ แต่ต้องล็อกระบบเก็บเงิน".

## Health / 疾厄 Chain

Use health symbolically and safely:

1. **疾厄 palace**: main star(s), brightness,煞, 化忌, 天刑, 空劫.
2. **命宮 / 身宮**: vitality, constitution, body-focus of the decade.
3. **福德**: sleep, stress, mind, recovery quality.
4. **大限 / 流年**: whether the current period activates 疾厄, 命身, 福德, or a repeated煞/忌 line.
5. **流月 / 流日**: short-window trigger only when supplied.

Allowed output:

- stress signal, fatigue pattern, sleep/routine issue, check-up reminder, recovery discipline, workload adjustment, risk management, follow professional care.

Forbidden output:

- diagnosis, named fixed disease, death prediction, medication, surgery instruction, or telling the user to ignore clinicians.

If the packet shows pressure but no medical field-level detail, say exactly what kind of life management is indicated, not a disease name.

## Combined Work Money Health Question

For a combined question, answer in three separate mini-verdicts:

1. **งาน**: 官祿 + 命身 + 大限/流年.
2. **เงิน**: 財帛 + 田宅 + 官祿 + leakage/support.
3. **สุขภาพ**: 疾厄 + 福德 + 命身 + timing.

Then add one fused conclusion:

- "งานนำเงิน", "เงินรั่วจากงาน/บ้าน", "สุขภาพเป็นตัวจำกัดงาน", "ควรเก็บฐานก่อนขยาย", or another packet-backed phrase.

The fused conclusion must not erase the three mini-verdicts. If work is strong and health is stressed, say both clearly.

## Timing Discipline

For future-year questions:

1. Use the resolved target year from the prompt.
2. Name the 大限 age range and its palace/theme.
3. Name the target 流年 palace and annual 四化 / annual stars when supplied.
4. Connect the annual activation to 官祿, 財帛, 田宅, 疾厄, 福德, or 遷移.
5. Do not say the year is unavailable if the packet has that target 流年 row.
6. Do not extrapolate monthly/day detail unless 流月/流日 for that date/window is supplied.

If the question asks a year but packet only has natal + decade, answer from natal+大限 and explicitly lower timing precision to decade-level.

- one 命宮/身宮 baseline line,
