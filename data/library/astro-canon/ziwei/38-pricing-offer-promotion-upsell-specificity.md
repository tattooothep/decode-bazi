## Purpose

Use this pack for price, package, tier, coupon, discount, promotion, bundle, upsell, cross-sell, add-on, top-up, credit pack, paid plan, raise price, lower price, intro offer, and "what offer should I sell" questions.

Do not answer from popularity, sales, or retention alone. Split price power, perceived value, customer willingness, delivery burden, cash margin, discount leakage, and timing.

## Core Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: capacity to carry the offer promise and whether pricing pressure becomes personal stress.
2. **官祿**: product/service promise, professional authority, premium positioning, and whether the offer is credible.
3. **財帛**: price received, margin, cash flow, and whether payment arrives cleanly.
4. **田宅**: package durability, stored credits, bundled value, subscription base, and whether value remains after promotion.
5. **夫妻**: buyer, subscriber, sponsor, platform, contract, and whether the customer accepts the term.
6. **兄弟**: sales page, price explanation, coupon rules, checkout copy, support message, and offer clarity.
7. **僕役 / 福德**: user base, demand mood, community appetite, perceived benefit, and whether warm users can be upsold.
8. **疾厄 / 化忌 / 煞曜**: refund leakage, discount abuse, hidden defect, service fatigue, and margin erosion.
9. **Timing**: 大限/流年/流月/流日 only when supplied.

## Pricing-Chain Rule

Price is not the same as demand. Require a chain:

- 官祿 = offer promise and authority,
- 財帛 = price/margin received,
- 夫妻 = buyer/customer/platform accepts terms,
- 僕役/福德 = user base and perceived value,
- 兄弟 = price explanation and coupon clarity,
- 田宅 = package/credit value retained,
- 疾厄/化忌/煞曜 = discount leak, refund pressure, or hidden delivery cost.

If 官祿 and 福德 are strong but 財帛 is weak, the offer has value but is underpriced. If 財帛 is strong but 夫妻 is damaged, the price can sell yet causes customer friction. If 田宅 is damaged, avoid long bundles or stored-credit promises.

## Discount / Promotion Rule

For coupon, launch promo, and discount questions, name the pressure:

- **safe short promo**: 兄弟 clear + 夫妻 cooperative + 財帛 still supported,
- **underpricing risk**: 官祿/福德 strong but 財帛 weak,
- **discount abuse**: 財帛/田宅 pressure + 疾厄/化忌,
- **unclear offer**: 兄弟 damaged,
- **premium room**: 官祿/福德/夫妻 supported with clean 財帛.

Do not recommend broad discounting when the leak markers are stronger than the customer markers.
