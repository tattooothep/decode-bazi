## Purpose

Use this pack for customer support, service quality, complaint handling, ticket backlog, delivery, fulfillment, onboarding, SLA, operations workload, staff capacity, service recovery, product delivery, and "can we serve customers well" questions.

Do not answer from sales, marketing, price, or reputation alone. Split demand, service promise, staff capacity, customer pressure, communication, operating cost, bottleneck, and timing.

## Core Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: ability to carry service pressure, complaint load, repeated operations, and customer-facing stress.
2. **兄弟**: support replies, documentation, chat scripts, ticket wording, escalation language, and instruction clarity.
3. **夫妻**: customer, subscriber, platform counterparty, complaint source, service expectation, and whether the client accepts recovery.
4. **官祿**: official service standard, product promise, SOP, quality control, and whether the service claim can be fulfilled.
5. **僕役**: staff, helpers, subcontractors, queue handling, community helpers, and whether service load can be shared.
6. **財帛 / 田宅**: support cost, refund reserve, delivery base, inventory, CRM/list, stored records, and retained operational value.
7. **疾厄 / 化忌 / 煞曜**: backlog, burnout, bad handoff, complaint pileup, platform penalty, hidden service leak, or operational illness.
8. **Timing**: 大限/流年/流月/流日 only when supplied.

## Service-Chain Rule

A sale is not a delivered result. Require a chain:

- 兄弟 = support wording, ticket flow, documentation,
- 夫妻 = customer expectation and complaint pressure,
- 官祿 = service promise and SOP,
- 僕役 = staff/helper/queue capacity,
- 財帛/田宅 = support cost, reserve, inventory, delivery base, records,
- 疾厄/化忌/煞曜 = bottleneck, burnout, hidden leak, complaint or platform risk.

If 夫妻 is pressured while 兄弟 is weak, customers complain because communication and recovery are unclear. If 官祿 is strong but 僕役/疾厄 are weak, the promise is good but operations cannot carry the load. If 財帛/田宅 are weak, do not expand service before fixing delivery cost and records.
