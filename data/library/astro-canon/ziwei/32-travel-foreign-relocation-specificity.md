# Zi Wei Travel, Foreign Field, Relocation, And Outside Opportunity Specificity Pack

Derived operational summary for travel, foreign/distant field, relocation, outside clients, public exposure, and leaving the home base in 紫微斗數. Use only as internal interpretation rules; do not quote or expose source text.

## Scope

Use this pack when the question asks about travel, moving city, moving country, relocation, migration, foreign opportunity, overseas clients, outside market, online/outside audience, work away from home, travel risk, or whether to go/stay/delay.

If the question is buying/selling/renting a residence, land, condo, vehicle, mortgage, title deed, or lease, use the property/vehicle pack first. If it asks visa/permit/legal approval, also use the authority/document pack. If it asks exact date selection, also use the specialty/electional guard.

## Evidence Chain

1. **命宮 / 身宮**: baseline capacity, stress tolerance, and life focus.
2. **遷移**: first palace for travel, relocation, outside field, foreign/distant opportunity, public exposure, and life beyond the old base.
3. **田宅**: home base, old/new residence, family root, whether leaving home disrupts stability.
4. **官祿**: career reason for travel or relocation, external status, work opportunity abroad/outside.
5. **財帛**: relocation/travel budget, foreign income, cashflow, cost leak, and whether outside opportunity pays.
6. **父母**: official papers, school/company documents, visa/permit lane, elder/institution pressure.
7. **夫妻 / 僕役 / 兄弟**: partner, agent, companion, clients, team, local helper, counterparties.
8. **疾厄 / 福德**: fatigue, stress, sleep, isolation, recovery, and psychological cost of movement.
9. **天馬/流年天馬 if supplied + 四化 + 大限/流年/流月/流日**: movement activation and timing. Use only supplied fields.

## Reading Rules

- Do not answer relocation from 遷移 alone. Require 命身 capacity, 遷移, 田宅, money/work support, and timing.
- Strong 遷移 with weak 田宅 means the outside field is active but home/root stability is the condition.
- Strong 田宅 with weak 遷移 means staying, short trips, or gradual relocation is safer than a sudden distant move.
- For foreign work/clients, combine 遷移 with 官祿/財帛/僕役. For foreign study or documents, also include 父母.
- If 化忌 or malefics hit the travel/relocation lane, name the concrete bottleneck: document delay, partner disagreement, cost leak, fatigue, unstable housing, client friction, or timing delay.

## Answer Contract

For a travel/foreign/relocation question, write:
1. one 命身 capacity-to-move line,
2. one 遷移 outside-field/travel/relocation line,
3. one 田宅 old-base/new-base line,
4. one 官祿/財帛 work-money support line,
5. one 父母/夫妻/僕役/兄弟 document-companion-client line when relevant,
6. one 疾厄/福德 fatigue/rest/risk-management line when relevant,
7. one 四化/timing line,
8. one decisive practical call: go / go with conditions / prepare first / delay / stay / choose closer option / avoid this trip.
