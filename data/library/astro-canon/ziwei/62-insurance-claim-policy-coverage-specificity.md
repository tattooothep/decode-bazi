# Zi Wei Insurance, Claim, Policy, And Coverage Specificity Pack

Use this pack only when the user asks about insurance, policy coverage, claim approval, claim denial, appeal, premium, deductible, coverage limit, beneficiary, insurer, broker, adjuster, indemnity, payout, lapse, renewal, rider, exclusion, pre-existing condition, medical insurance, vehicle insurance, property insurance, business insurance, or whether to buy coverage, renew, cancel, file a claim, appeal, or wait.

Do not treat insurance as ordinary debt, tax, property purchase, health treatment, lawsuit, or generic document approval. If the question is only illness/treatment/surgery, use the health pack. If it asks whether the policy pays, whether the claim is accepted, whether coverage is enough, whether to appeal a denial, or whether to buy/renew/cancel coverage, this pack must be included.

Insurance/Claim Judgment

Zi Wei insurance judgment must join 命宮/身宮, 財帛, 田宅, 官祿, 夫妻, 兄弟/父母, 僕役, 疾厄, 四化, and timing. Insurance is not just "documents"; it is a contract promise that pays only when the named risk, evidence, and exclusion rules line up.

- 命宮/身宮: insured-risk/exposure capacity, personal risk discipline, and whether the owner can execute the claim/appeal without emotional overreach.
- 財帛: premium affordability, deductible, reimbursement cash-in, payout amount, coverage limit, and whether the claim money actually solves the burden.
- 田宅: insured asset, home/vehicle/equipment/business base, stored reserve, and whether the asset condition supports or weakens the claim.
- 官祿: insurer/company decision, underwriting authority, approval/denial standard, employer group plan, and official claim outcome.
- 夫妻: insurer as contract counterparty, beneficiary, named insured/co-insured, promise conflict, and negotiated settlement.
- 兄弟/父母: policy wording, application, certificate, claim form, medical/repair proof, communication record, deadline, and official paperwork.
- 僕役: broker, agent, adjuster, claims handler, hospital/repair vendor, service queue, and follow-up execution.
- 疾厄 plus 煞曜/化忌: loss event, illness/accident, exclusion, lapse, denial, hidden uncovered risk, fraud suspicion, and penalty.
- 四化/大限/流年/流月/流日 when present: claim trigger, document trigger, approval/denial point, renewal/lapse timing, and appeal window.

Answer Contract

For insurance/claim questions, the answer must include:

1. one 命宮/身宮 insured-risk/exposure capacity line;
2. one 財帛 premium/deductible/coverage-limit/payout-cash line;
3. one 田宅 insured-asset/reserve/asset-condition line when asset/property/vehicle/business coverage is involved, or explicitly say why it is not the main axis;
4. one 官祿 insurer/underwriting/approval-authority line;
5. one 夫妻 insurer-contract/beneficiary/counterparty promise line;
6. one 兄弟/父母 claim-file/policy-wording/evidence/deadline line;
7. one 僕役 broker/agent/adjuster/service-follow-up line;
8. one 疾厄/化忌/煞曜 exclusion/lapse/denial/fraud-risk/uncovered-loss line;
9. one 四化/timing/action line with a clear action: file claim, appeal, renew, buy coverage, increase coverage, reduce rider, cancel after replacement, wait, avoid uncovered risk, prepare evidence, or escalate to regulator.

Do not let a favorable 財帛 or 官祿 alone become "claim approved." A claim requires policy wording, evidence, counterparty promise, exclusion check, and timing. Do not promise guaranteed payout.
