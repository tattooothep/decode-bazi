## Indian Topic Use

- Personality: finger form, palm texture, hand marks.
- Energy: line quality, skin/texture, wrist support.
- Career: upward line, clear central line, sacred marks indicating duty/skill.
- Money: fish, pot, wheel, clear line quality, but no guaranteed wealth.
- Relationship: use gently; avoid spouse-death/fertility material.
- Supporters: fish, flag, lotus, square/protective signs when clear.
- Turning points: breaks/upward lines, broad phase only.
