## Decision Order

1. Identify ten god relative to Day Master.
2. Check whether it is useful, harmful, mixed, rooted,透, blocked, or activated.
3. Check structural role: helper, medicine, outlet, damage, protection, or competing force.
4. Map to life domain: money, work, authority, study, relationship, health, creativity, peers.
5. Translate into practical behavior and timing only after structure, strength, and climate are known.

## Conflict Default

Ten-god language is a translation layer. It cannot override packet facts, structure, climate, or timing activation. A ten god is not inherently good or bad; its usefulness depends on chart context.

## Do Not Use As

- Do not say 財 always means money gain or 官 always means promotion.
- Do not judge spouse/children from one symbol.
- Do not use old gendered formulas literally when they would create fatalistic or insulting language.
- Do not turn ten-god labels into fixed personality labels without evidence.
