## Decision Order

1. Start from 月令 and locked month branch.
2. Identify frame candidates: 正官, 七殺, 財, 印, 食神, 傷官, 建祿, 陽刃, special/derived structures.
3. Check 透干, root, season, 清濁, and whether the route is coherent.
4. Identify 相神: the helper that lets the frame work.
5. Identify damage: 傷官破官, 官殺混雜, 財破印, 梟印奪食, 比劫奪財, 七殺無制, muddy 財印/官殺 route.
6. Identify rescue: 印, 食, 財, 官殺, 比劫, 合, 制, 化, 通關, or luck supply.
7. Decide 成格 / 破格 / 帶忌 / 救應 and assign confidence.
8. Translate into status, work pattern, relationship pattern, and life-domain consequence.

## Conflict Default

Zi Ping structure authority wins for 格局/相神. QTBJ is climate-only and must not override locked structure. DTS strength/flow can explain why a structure carries or fails, but it must not change the month-command frame.
