## Decision Order

1. Lock the exact pair/set: stem pair, branch pair, 三合, 三會, 六合, 六冲, 刑, 害, 破, 暗合, 墓庫.
2. Confirm evidence type: packet-listed, closed-list synastry, visible locked pillars, or user-only claim.
3. Locate it: natal, luck, year, month, cross-person; year/month/day/hour proximity.
4. Identify the affected target: useful god, taboo god, spouse palace, resource/wealth/official/output star, health marker, vault, or timing point.
5. Judge strength: season, root, visible stem, transformation condition, obstruction, mediator.
6. Judge effect: bind, transform, move, open, expose, block, rescue, break, over-activate.
7. Translate to life only after the affected layer is known.

## Conflict Default

Packet evidence wins. If the user asks about an interaction that the packet does not list and the locked pillars do not visibly show, the answer must downgrade it to unsupported premise. 合 is not automatically good, 冲 is not automatically bad, and 墓庫 clash is not automatically loss.

## Do Not Use As

- Do not invent 子子自刑, 辰戌冲, 寅戌半合, 暗合, or cross-chart reactions because a user prompt mentions them.
- Do not decide family, love, or business compatibility from one year branch.
- Do not claim 合化 unless season, root, clear route, and lack of serious obstruction are present.
- Do not use interaction rhetoric as event prediction without luck/year activation.
